# LinkedIn: What My First 10 Sales Taught Me

What I learned from my first 10 sales:

1. The people who bought fastest were the ones who recognized their problem in the first sentence. Not the ones who needed convincing.

2. Nobody asked about credentials. They asked "what do I get and how fast?"

3. The $47 price point removed almost all objections. People said yes to figure out if it was for them.

4. Every buyer who told me why they bought used different words than my marketing. Their language was better. I started using it.

5. The offer I was most nervous about shipping was the one that converted best. The polished version I spent weeks on didn't.

The pattern: speed beats perfection. Specificity beats breadth. Their words beat your words.

If you're waiting to feel ready before you launch — you're overthinking it. Ship something small. Let the first 10 sales teach you what to build next.
