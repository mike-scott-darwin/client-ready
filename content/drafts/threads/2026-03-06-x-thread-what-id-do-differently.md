# Thread: What I'd Do Differently

---

1/7

If I had to start my coaching business over from zero, here's what I'd do differently:

---

2/7

I'd stop trying to serve everyone.

"I help people with mindset and confidence" served nobody.

The moment I got specific — one audience, one problem, one outcome — people started paying.

---

3/7

I'd validate before I build.

My first offer took 3 months to create.

My best offer took one afternoon to validate and an hour to build. The order matters.

---

4/7

I'd charge more, sooner.

I started at $500 because I didn't feel "ready" for $5K.

Nobody took the $500 offer seriously. The first person I charged $5K said yes in one conversation.

Price signals value. Low price signals low confidence.

---

5/7

I'd stop posting and start talking to people.

Content is a megaphone. But a megaphone is useless when you don't know what to say.

5 DM conversations taught me more about my audience than 6 months of posting.

---

6/7

I'd treat it like engineering, not art.

Test, validate, build. Not feel, hope, launch.

The coaches who scale fastest aren't the most creative. They're the most disciplined about testing.

---

7/7

Summary:

1. Get specific
2. Validate first
3. Charge what it's worth
4. Talk to people before you post for people
5. Engineer it — don't wing it

None of this is sexy. All of it works.
