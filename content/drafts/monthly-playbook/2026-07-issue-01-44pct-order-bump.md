## Why it hits 44%

My 44% bump is a **done-for-you** offer — "AI extracts your offer + 24-hour launch." The DIY-flavoured bumps sat around 14%. Same checkout, 3× the take-rate. Four things make a bump convert like that:

1. **It completes the purchase they just made.** They built the offer → the bump *launches it for them.* The literal next step. (Relevance = 80% of take-rate.)
2. **It sells done-for-you / speed.** People pay to *skip the work* — why mine beat the DIY bumps 3-to-1.
3. **It's a reflex, not a decision.** Impulse price, one sentence, price on the checkbox, never pre-checked.
4. **The value is anchored.** "~~$197~~ → $37, only at checkout."
