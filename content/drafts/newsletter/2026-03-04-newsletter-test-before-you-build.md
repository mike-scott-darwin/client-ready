# Test Before You Build

**Subject line:** Stop building things nobody asked for

**Preview text:** The most expensive mistake in coaching isn't a bad funnel.

---

Every coach I've worked with made the same mistake.

They had an idea. A good one. Maybe great. And they spent 3 months building it.

Course modules. Slide decks. Workbooks. A logo. Maybe even a website.

Then they launched. Crickets.

Not because the idea was bad. Because they never tested it.

Here's what engineers do before they build anything: they validate. They don't assume. They test.

Coaching should work the same way.

**Before you build a single thing:**

1. Write the offer in one sentence
2. Call 5 people who fit the profile
3. Say: "I'm building X for Y. Would you pay $Z for this?"
4. 3 out of 5 say yes — build it
5. Less than 3 — rewrite and test again

That's it. No course. No funnel. No content calendar. Just a conversation.

The coaches charging $5K+ didn't build first. They validated first. The building was the easy part — because they already knew people wanted it.

---

The worst version of this mistake? Building something great that nobody wants. You spent 3 months. You're proud of it. And then you have to either sell something people didn't ask for or start over.

Test first. Build second. It takes one afternoon and five conversations.
