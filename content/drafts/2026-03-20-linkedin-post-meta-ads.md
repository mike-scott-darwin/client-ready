**Most coaches are burning cash on Meta Ads in 2026. Here's what actually works now.**

The rules have changed. After analyzing 30+ coaching campaigns this month, three patterns emerged for low-ticket offers:

1. **Creative IS targeting**  
Meta's algorithm now matches audiences based on your ad content, not your targeting settings. That polished studio ad? It's getting out-performed by raw iPhone footage shot vertically.  

2. **$47 is the new $7**  
Front-end pricing below $47 attracts tire-kickers. The sweet spot? $47-$97 with strategic bumps to hit $90-110 AOV. This covers ad spend while filtering for serious buyers.  

3. **Hybrid beats video-only**  
Landing pages combining short video + scrollable text convert 22% better than video-only pages. Give mobile viewers both options.  

**Takeaway:**  
Stop optimizing last year's playbook. The 2026 algorithm rewards volume (30+ creatives/month) and authenticity over production quality.
