# LinkedIn: The Problem Isn't Consistency

Posting consistently for 12 months and nobody's buying?

It's not the algorithm. It's not the content.

It's the offer underneath.

Most coaches believe content drives sales. So they post more. Try reels. Write threads. Nothing changes.

Because content doesn't drive sales. A clear offer does. Content just amplifies whatever's underneath it.

Quick test: Can you explain what you sell in one sentence that makes someone say "How?"

If not — stop posting for 48 hours. Write down the specific outcome you deliver. Say it to five people. Rewrite until someone reaches for their wallet.

Then go back to posting. Now you're amplifying something worth amplifying.

The difference between content that gets likes and content that gets clients? The offer underneath.
