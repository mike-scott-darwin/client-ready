# The Arc: Prompting, Codifying, Compounding

There are three stages of AI maturity. Most people are stuck at stage one.

Stage 1: Prompting. You type something into ChatGPT. You get something back. You tweak the prompt. You try again. Each interaction starts from zero. Nothing carries forward. Every session is a blank slate.

Stage 2: Codifying. You start writing down what you re-explain every time. Your business. Your audience. Your voice. You create files that AI reads before generating. Suddenly outputs are 3x better with zero prompt engineering. The context does the heavy lifting.

Stage 3: Compounding. Your reference stack grows over time. Research gets synthesized into reference. Decisions get documented. Proof gets updated. Every week, your AI knows more about your business than it did last week. The quality curve bends upward.

Most AI advice lives at Stage 1. Better prompts. Prompt templates. Prompt chains. It's all Stage 1 thinking applied to a Stage 2 problem.

The jump from 1 to 2 is uncomfortable. It requires you to do the identity work of articulating what your business actually is. Not the elevator pitch. The real, deep, specific version.

But once you make that jump, you never go back. Because you've experienced what AI can do when it actually knows who you are.

Stage 1 is using AI. Stage 3 is running a business with AI.
