# LinkedIn: The Guru Playbook Is Broken

The guru playbook:

1. Quit your job
2. Build a personal brand
3. Post every day for 6 months
4. Launch a $2K course
5. Scale to $100K/month

Sounds great. Works for about 0.1% of people.

Here's what actually works for the other 99.9%:

1. Keep your job
2. Build a clear offer (not a personal brand)
3. Validate it with 5 conversations
4. Sell it before you build anything
5. Scale when revenue says you can

It's not sexy. It doesn't make good Instagram content. Nobody's going to film a documentary about it.

But it works. Because it's engineering, not performance.

The guru playbook sells courses about the guru playbook. The engineering approach builds businesses.

Pick one.
