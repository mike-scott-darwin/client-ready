# One Offer. One Funnel. That's It.

Here's the exact funnel strategy I'd use if I were starting from zero today. One offer. One funnel. No complexity.

Step one: Create a front-end offer between $27 and $97. Something specific that solves one clear problem. Not a "transformation" -- a tool, a system, a template that gets them a result in hours, not months.

Step two: Add one bump on the checkout page. Something complementary. If the front-end is a DIY system, the bump is a done-for-you version. Keep it simple. One checkbox.

Step three: Send buyers into an email sequence. Three to five emails over two weeks. Deliver value, build trust, introduce your higher-ticket offer.

That's the whole thing.

No webinar funnel. No 47-step automation. No tripwire-to-downsell-to-upsell-to-cross-sell maze that takes three months to build and never converts.

Most coaches overcomplicate this because the gurus selling funnel software need you to believe it's complicated. It's not.

One offer that's validated. One page that sells it. One bump that increases average order value. One email sequence that builds the relationship.

Get that working first. Optimize later. Scale after that.

The coaches who make consistent revenue aren't running complex funnels. They're running simple ones that actually convert.
