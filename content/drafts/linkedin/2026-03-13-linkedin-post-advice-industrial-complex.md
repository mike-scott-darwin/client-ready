# LinkedIn: The Advice Industrial Complex

There is an entire industry built on telling you what you are doing wrong.

Your mindset is wrong. Your niche is wrong. Your pricing is wrong. Your content strategy is wrong. Your morning routine is wrong.

And conveniently, they have a course to fix each one.

Here is what I have learned from building an actual business: Most of that advice is reverse-engineered from survivorship bias. Someone succeeded, looked backward, and called their path a system.

But their path included timing, luck, existing connections, and a dozen variables they do not mention in the webinar.

The coaches who succeed are not the ones who consume the most advice. They are the ones who pick one clear path and execute until they have data.

Not opinions. Data.

Five real conversations. Five real sales attempts. Five real pieces of feedback from humans who fit your audience.

That is worth more than every masterclass combined.

Stop consuming. Start testing.
