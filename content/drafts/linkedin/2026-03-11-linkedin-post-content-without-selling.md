# LinkedIn: You Are Not a Content Creator

Somewhere along the way, coaches started believing their job was to create content.

Post every day. Grow a following. Build an audience. Monetize later.

This is a content creator's playbook. Not a coaching business playbook.

Content creators monetize attention. Coaches monetize transformation.

If you are posting five times a week and nobody is buying, the problem is not your content frequency. It is the absence of a clear offer underneath it.

Every post should do one of three things: demonstrate expertise, address a specific objection, or make the offer.

If your content does none of these — if it is just "value" floating in space — you are running a free education channel. Not a business.

The fix is not more content. It is clearer content with a reason to exist.

Ask yourself before every post: What does this lead to?

If the answer is "engagement," rethink it. If the answer is "my offer," ship it.
