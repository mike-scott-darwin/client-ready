# LinkedIn: Why I Charge 47 Dollars Before I Charge 5,000

Most coaches try to sell high-ticket first. Premium positioning. Authority branding. Discovery calls.

I did the opposite. I built a 47-dollar offer.

Not because I couldn't charge more. Because I wanted proof before I scaled.

At 47 dollars, people buy faster. You learn faster. You iterate faster. And you find out whether your offer actually solves the problem — not whether your sales skills can compensate for a weak one.

Here is what 47 dollars taught me that 5K never could:

The exact words people use to describe their problem. Which part of the delivery they actually valued. What they skipped entirely. What made them come back and ask for more.

That data is worth more than any high-ticket sale. Because it tells you what to build next.

The 47-dollar offer isn't the business. It's the proof the business works.

Once you have that proof, scaling to 5K is a conversation. Not a leap of faith.
