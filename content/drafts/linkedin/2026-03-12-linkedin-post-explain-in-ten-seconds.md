# LinkedIn: The 10-Second Offer Test

I have a test I run on every offer before it goes live.

I show the headline to someone who has never seen my business. I give them 10 seconds. Then I ask: "What do you think this is, and would you buy it?"

If they cannot answer both questions clearly, the offer fails.

Not the design. Not the copy. The offer itself.

Most coaches spend weeks perfecting their sales page. Colors. Testimonials. Guarantee badges. None of it matters if the core promise is not clear in the first 10 seconds.

Here is why this matters: Your audience gives you less than 10 seconds. On a feed, in a DM, on a landing page. If they cannot immediately understand what you do and why it matters to them, they scroll.

No amount of retargeting fixes a confusing first impression.

The solution is not better marketing. It is a simpler offer.

One problem. One outcome. One clear sentence.

If you can nail that, the rest of the funnel builds itself.
