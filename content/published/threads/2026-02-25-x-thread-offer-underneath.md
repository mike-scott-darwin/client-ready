# Thread: The Offer Underneath

**Repurposed from:** Newsletter — "The Offer Underneath"

---

🧵 1/7

Posting 3x a week for 12 months and nobody's buying.

The algorithm isn't broken. The content isn't bad.

The problem is what's underneath it.

---

2/7

Most coaches think content drives sales.

So they post more. Try reels. Write threads. Batch a month of content on Sunday.

Nothing changes.

---

3/7

Content doesn't drive sales. A clear offer drives sales.

Content just amplifies whatever's underneath it.

If the offer is fuzzy, you're amplifying confusion.

---

4/7

The test:

Can you explain your offer in one sentence?

Not your elevator pitch. Not your bio.

One sentence that makes someone say "How?"

If you can't — the content isn't the problem.

---

5/7

Here's what to do:

1. Stop posting for 48 hours
2. Write down what you actually help people do (the specific outcome, not the transformation)
3. Say it to five people — watch their face
4. Rewrite until someone says "How much?"

---

6/7

Then go back to posting.

But now you're amplifying something worth amplifying.

---

7/7

This is the difference between content that gets likes and content that gets clients.

The offer underneath.

Fix that first. Everything else gets easier.
