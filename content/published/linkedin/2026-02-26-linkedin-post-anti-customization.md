# LinkedIn: Stop Customizing Your Offer

I used to rewrite my offer for every single lead.

Different pricing. Different scope. Different deliverables. Every call felt like starting from scratch.

Then I realized: if it needs rewriting, it's not clear enough.

The coaches charging $5K+ don't have 10 different offers. They have one. It's just that clear.

"Custom" doesn't mean personalized. It means unfocused.

A clear, repeatable offer does three things:
- Attracts the right people without explanation
- Sells without a custom pitch every time
- Lets you deliver at a higher level because you're not reinventing the wheel

Stop customizing your offer for every prospect. Build one that works for all of them.
