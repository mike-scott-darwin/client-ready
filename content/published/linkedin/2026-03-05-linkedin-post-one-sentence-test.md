# LinkedIn: The One-Sentence Test

Here's a test that will save you months of wasted effort:

Explain your offer in one sentence. Out loud. To someone who doesn't know you.

Not your elevator pitch. Not your tagline. One sentence that makes them say "How much?"

If they tilt their head — it's not clear enough.

Most coaches fail this test. Not because they're bad at what they do. Because they've never been forced to compress it.

"I help people with mindset and confidence" — head tilt.

"I help burnt-out tech managers build a 90-day resilience system so they stop losing their best people" — "How much?"

Same coach. Same expertise. Different sentence.

Your offer isn't unclear because you need more time. It's unclear because you haven't cut enough words yet.

One sentence. Try it today.
