## The PDF (deliverable)

### THE BUILD ORDER
*The 5 steps to a coaching business that sells — in order.*

Here's the truth: you don't have a content problem or a traffic problem. You have an **order-of-operations problem.** Most coaches start at step 3. The four steps below only work if you build them in sequence. Step 1 is the one almost nobody does — and it's the one the other four are built on.

---

**1. Validate the offer — before you build anything.**
Not a survey. Not "asking your audience what they want." The only validation that counts is someone paying you. You can extract and test a $5K+ offer in one afternoon — *before* you build a course, a site, or a funnel. Why first: everything downstream sits on top of the offer. Get this wrong and you'll grind for a year on a great funnel pointed at the wrong thing.
> *You can't grow into pain. If the offer doesn't fit who you are, you'll burn it down at $8K.*

**2. Build a self-liquidating front end.**
A low-ticket offer — roughly $40–$100 — whose job is to pay for your ads. This is the leverage the pros don't talk about. When your front end covers ad spend, traffic stops being a cost and becomes a lead machine that funds itself. Every sale pays for the next batch of buyers.
> *Free lead magnets predict nothing. A $47 buyer tells you more than 1,000 free downloads.*

**3. Run paid traffic — not the content treadmill.**
72% of offers that actually validate in 2026 do it with paid traffic, not by posting for twelve months and praying. Paid tells you in 7 days what organic takes 3 months to reveal. You're not buying reach — you're buying *answers*, fast.

**4. Let your email do the ascending.**
Buyers forget you within 72 hours of going quiet. The first ~14 days after a purchase is when someone is most likely to buy again — so that's when you show up. A simple welcome sequence is the highest-leverage asset in the whole machine, and it's the part nobody builds.

**5. Sell the backend without a sales call.**
Your high-ticket offer isn't pitched — it's *witnessed*. People watch you work, you open a few spots, they raise their hand already decided. No sales page. No call. By the time they ask "how do I work with you?", the selling is done.

---

**The order again:** Validate → Self-liquidate → Traffic → Email → Backend.

Start at step 1. Always.
