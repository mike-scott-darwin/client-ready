**[0:36–0:48] STEP 3 — PAID TRAFFIC, NOT THE CONTENT TREADMILL**
"Step three. Run paid traffic to that offer. Seventy-two percent of offers that actually validate in 2026 do it with paid traffic — not by posting and praying for twelve months. Paid tells you in seven days what organic takes three months to reveal."

**[0:48–0:58] STEP 4 — EMAIL DOES THE ASCENDING**
