## Prepared Q&A

**"How long does setup take?"**
The core four files take an afternoon. The system compounds from there. Week 1 you have basics. Week 4 you have something powerful. Week 7 it's running itself.

**"Does this only work with Claude Code?"**
No. The reference files are plain markdown. They work with Claude Projects, ChatGPT custom instructions, Cursor, any tool that accepts context. Claude Code just makes it seamless because it reads the files directly from your folder.

**"What about team access?"**
The files live in a git repo. GitHub, shared drive, whatever your team uses. Everyone reads the same context. Consistent outputs across the team.

**"How is this different from just uploading a brand guide to Claude Projects?"**
A brand guide is static. Reference files are living documents that get refined through research, decisions, and usage. The codification practice — research, decide, codify — is what makes it compound. Uploading a PDF is upload-and-pray. This is a system.

**"Can you do this for your clients' businesses?"**
Yes. That's the agency play. Extract their reference stack, build it out, and every output you produce is informed by it. The reference stack becomes the deliverable, not just the content.

**"What if I can't articulate my business clearly?"**
That's the whole point. The extraction process forces you to articulate what you know. Most business owners have never written down why they exist, who they actually serve, or how they sound. The act of doing that IS the strategic work. You understand your business more deeply by codifying it.
