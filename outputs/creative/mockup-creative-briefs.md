## General Design Notes

### Consistency Across All Mockups
- Same teal/cyan + black color scheme
- Same font family
- Michael's photo on main screen
- Professional, tech-forward aesthetic
- "AI-POWERED" positioning throughout

### Hierarchy
1. Product name (largest)
2. Component names (medium)
3. Subheads/callouts (smallest)

### What NOT to Include
- Price (handled in sales copy, not mockup)
- Guarantees (handled in sales copy)
- Testimonials (separate section)
