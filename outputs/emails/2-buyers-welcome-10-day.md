## Day 5: Quick Tip

**Subject:** The 2-minute test for your offer

---

Hey [NAME],

Quick tip today.

**The "Mom Test" for your offer:**

Explain your offer to someone who knows nothing about coaching. Your mom. Your neighbor. Anyone outside the industry.

If they can repeat back what you do and who it's for... you're clear.

If they look confused or say "so you're like a life coach?"... you need to sharpen.

Clarity isn't about being clever. It's about being obvious.

Try it today. Let me know what happens.
