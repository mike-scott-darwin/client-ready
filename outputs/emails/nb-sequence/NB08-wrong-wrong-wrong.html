Here's what most coaches do:

  1. Come up with an offer idea
  2. Spend 3 months building a course
  3. Design a fancy website
  4. Set up email sequences
  5. Maybe run some ads
  6. THEN find out nobody wants it

Wrong.

That's backwards.

You just wasted 3 months and $2,000+ on infrastructure for an offer you never validated.

Here's the engineering approach:

  1. Extract your offer (what you actually sell)
  2. Validate it with real money (not "what do you think?" surveys)
  3. THEN build the infrastructure

Test, validate, build. Not build, hope, pray.

I know coaches who've built entire funnels, shot video courses, hired designers... for offers that never made a single sale.

All because they skipped validation.

Don't be that coach.
