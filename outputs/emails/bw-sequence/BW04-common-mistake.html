Biggest mistake I see coaches make:

Starting from scratch.

Blank landing page. Blank email sequence. Blank everything.

It's not just slow — it's demoralizing. You stare at the cursor and wonder if you're even doing this right.

I did this for 6 months before I realized: the best creators don't start from scratch. They start from something that works and make it their own.

Whether it's templates, swipe files, or just a structure to follow — having a starting point changes everything.

That's why the Client Ready system gives you the prompts, not just the concepts. You're not inventing. You're extracting.

Same principle applies to everything downstream: landing pages, emails, ads.

Don't stare at blank pages. Find good examples and adapt them.
