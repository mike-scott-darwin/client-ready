Got this question yesterday:

"I went through the prompts but I'm not sure my offer is 'ready.' What if I put it out there and it flops?"

Here's the thing:

No offer is ready until it meets the market.

You can tweak your offer document forever. Adjust the wording. Second-guess the price. Wonder if you should add another bonus.

Or you can send it to 5 people and find out what's actually broken.

The first approach feels safe. The second approach gets you data.

Every successful coach I know launched before they felt ready. Then they fixed what didn't work based on real feedback.

Your offer doc isn't a final draft. It's a first hypothesis.

Test it. Learn. Adjust.

That's the game.
