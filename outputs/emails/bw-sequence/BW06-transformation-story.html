James came to me frustrated.

He had expertise — 15 years in corporate finance. He knew he could help entrepreneurs with their money. But he couldn't articulate the offer. Couldn't price it. Couldn't sell it.

His first attempt: "I help business owners with their finances."

Vague. Boring. Unsellable.

After extraction: "I help service business owners implement profit-first so they actually pay themselves every month."

Same expertise. Same guy. Completely different offer.

The shift? He stopped describing his process and started describing the outcome.

That's what the prompts force you to do.

James closed his first client within a month of getting clear.

What outcome does YOUR offer deliver? Not the process. The result.
