Quick roadmap of where this goes:

Stage 1: Extraction
You're here. Define the offer, know who it's for, articulate the transformation.

Stage 2: Validation
Test the offer with real people. Warm audience first. Get proof it works before building infrastructure.

Stage 3: Funnel
Landing page, email sequence, checkout. The system that runs without you.

Stage 4: Traffic
Paid ads, content, whatever drives qualified people to your funnel.

Stage 5: Scale
Raise prices. Add tiers. Build a team.

You don't need all five stages right now. You just need to nail Stage 1.

Most coaches skip straight to Stage 4 (traffic) with a broken Stage 1 (offer). They wonder why nobody buys.

Get the foundation right. Everything else gets easier.
