# Reactivation Sequence — The 202 ("Here's What I Did Next")

**Who:** 202 warm buyers who already bought Client Ready to build an offer. Not cold — they know Michael.
**Job:** sell them the thing they *didn't* see him do — codifying the whole business into files and running all marketing from them. Sort builders vs delegators. Reply-based (Miles: every email an offer, reply-based). No free magnet — they're buyers; sell the upgrade.
**Primary conversion:** DFY "we codify your brain for you" ($197) and/or Client Ready OS. Secondary: upgraded DIY $47 system for builders.
**Cadence:** Day 1, 2, 4, 6, 8 (5 emails). Send from `mail.codify.build`. Watch the DFY slot cap before Email 4/5 (guardrail #4).

> **Voice check:** direct, no hype, engineering, short sentences. No income promises to the reader (voice.md). The "48 files / 9 hours a week" is Michael's *process*, not a results claim for the buyer.

**Subject:** what I did after Client Ready
**Preview:** the part you didn't see

Here's what I did next — the part you didn't see.

Once I had the offer, I codified my entire business into 48 markdown files. Voice. Offer. Audience. Objections. Angles. All of it, written down where an AI could actually read it.

Then I ran everything from those files. Every ad. Every email. Every VSL. About nine hours a week. No agency. No content treadmill.

The marketing sounds like me — because it's built from me. Not templates. Not swipe copy. Me, on paper, feeding the machine.

That's the upgrade I'm handing you over the next few days.

Quick one before I do: what are you spending the most time on right now — the offer, the content, or getting clients? Hit reply and tell me.

— Michael

## Email 2 — Day 2 · The mechanism

**Subject:** why your marketing sounds like everyone else's
**Preview:** it's not the writer. it's the missing context.

Most "done for you" copy has one problem.

It's not you.

You can feel it when you read it back. So can your audience. That flat, coach-in-a-box tone that could belong to anyone.

Here's the thing — it's not the writer's fault, or the prompt's. It's the missing context. Generic in, generic out.

The fix isn't a better template. It's *your* context — your voice, your story, your offer, the exact words your buyers use — written down once, in files an AI can read every time.

Do that, and the output stops sounding like a template and starts sounding like you. Because it's extracted from you, not written for you.

That's the whole shift. Written-for-you → extracted-from-you.

Reply **FILES** and I'll show you exactly what that looks like.

— Michael

## Email 3 — Day 4 · Sovereignty + compounding

**Subject:** rent your marketing, or own it
**Preview:** one of these compounds

Two ways to run your marketing.

**Rent it.** A tool, an agency, a subscription that holds your stuff. Stop paying and it's gone. You built their asset.

**Own it.** Your context in plain files you keep. Any AI can run them. Nobody can take them.

Here's the part people miss: owned files *compound*. The offer file you write today writes your ads next month. Add one testimonial and every asset gets sharper. It's an appreciating asset — not a monthly expense.

You already started this when you built your offer with Client Ready. This is the rest of it: the whole business, on paper, working for you.

Reply and I'll send you the map of how the pieces fit.

— Michael

## Email 4 — Day 6 · The delegator sort

Simple question today.

Two kinds of people are reading this.

**Builders.** You want to run it yourself. For you, I rebuilt the Client Ready system so the extraction hands you the actual files — your offer, your ICP, your voice — not just a doc. You run it. You own it. Reply **MYSELF** and I'll point you to it.

**Delegators.** You'd rather it was handled. For you, send me 11 answers and I'll codify your brain for you: your ICP, your offer, a ready-to-send sales doc, and ad hooks — all in your voice — plus the files themselves, yours to keep and run forever. 48 hours. Reply **HANDLED**.

Neither is better. But building takes time you might not have this quarter.

Which one are you? Reply **MYSELF** or **HANDLED**.

— Michael

## Email 5 — Day 8 · The running agent + close

**Subject:** the version that runs without you
**Preview:** approve the content, don't make it

Here's the end state I want for you.

Your files are built. Every week, an agent drafts your ads, your emails, your DMs — in your voice, from your context — and hands them to you ready to send. You read them. You approve. They go out. Clients come in.

That's not a fantasy tool. It's just what happens once your business is codified. The work moves from *make the content* to *approve the content*.

That's where this ladder goes. We call it Client Ready OS. But all of it starts with the files.

If you want me to build them for you, reply **HANDLED**. I take a limited number each week so I can review every build myself — this week's slots go first-come.

If you'd rather build them yourself, reply **MYSELF** and I'll send you the upgraded system.

Either way: the offer you built was step one. This is the machine that runs it.

— Michael
