## Creative Direction Notes

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Deep navy (#1a1a2e) or off-white (#f5f5f5)
- Accent: Warm gold (#d4a373) for extraction/clarity moments
- Warning: Muted red (#c1666b) for problems/failed alternatives

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold sans-serif (Inter, Helvetica Neue)
- Contrast text: Handwritten or script for authenticity moments

**Style Guidelines:**
- Graphic styles: Clean, minimal, high contrast
- Lo-fi styles: Natural lighting, authentic imperfection, phone-quality aesthetic
- Avoid: Stock photo polish, corporate sterility, fake smiles

**Best for Testing:**
1. Lines 3, 12, 15 (Imposter syndrome — emotional hooks)
2. Lines 6, 23, 30 (Extract vs Find — core mechanism)
3. Lines 1, 21 (Price anchor — $17 vs $3K contrast)
4. Lines 5, 13, 28 (Outcome — DMs, inquiries, clients)
