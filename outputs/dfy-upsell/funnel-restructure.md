# Funnel Restructure: DFY Upsell + Community-First Model

**Context:** Adapting Terrance's model from Cat Howell hot seat (2026-03-07). Core change: community becomes central engine, DFY upsell replaces Sprint/Blueprint as OTO, high-ticket sells from community.

---

## Current Funnel vs. Proposed Funnel

### CURRENT

```
$47 Front-End
  ├── Bump 1: $37 DM Scripts
  ├── Bump 2: $67 Templates
  └── Bump 3: $97 Playbook
        │
        ▼
  OTO 1: $297 Sprint (4-week course + community access)
        │
        ▼
  OTO 2: $397 Blueprint (done-for-you strategy + community access)
        │
        ▼
  OTO 3: $47/mo Community ($1 trial — ONLY if declined OTO 1 AND OTO 2)
        │
        ▼
  Email ascension ──► $5K Accelerator (no-phone offer page)
```

**Problems this creates:**
- Community buried as last-resort downsell — most buyers never see it
- High-ticket ($5K) sold via cold email to strangers — high friction
- Sprint ($297) and Blueprint ($397) are high-commitment OTOs at checkout — cold buyer just spent $47 and now sees a $297 ask
- No recurring revenue engine — community at $47/mo with <5% take rate generates almost nothing

---

### PROPOSED

```
$47 Front-End
  ├── Bump 1: $37 DM Scripts
  ├── Bump 2: $67 Templates
  └── Bump 3: $97 Playbook
        │
        ▼
  OTO 1: $197 DFY Offer Build (full package + 30-day community trial)
        │ NO ──► Downsell: $97 DFY Lite (ICP + offer doc + 30-day community trial)
        │
        ▼
  OTO 2: $37/mo "What's Working Now" Newsletter (continuity, immediate charge)
        │
        ▼
  Thank You Page + Onboarding Form (triggers DFY build)
        │
        ▼
  Community ($97/mo after 30-day trial) ◄── THE ENGINE
        │
        ├── Weekly hot seat calls
        ├── DFY templates of the month
        ├── "What's Working Now" updates
        └── Access to Michael
              │
              ▼
  $5K+ Accelerator (announced in community — spots sell out)
```

## What Changes

### REMOVED from checkout flow

| Product | Old Position | What Happens To It |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|
| Sprint ($297) | OTO 1 | Content moves INTO community. Weekly calls, 4-week curriculum, accountability — all become community features. Sprint is no longer sold separately. |
| Blueprint ($397) | OTO 2 | Replaced by DFY Offer Build ($197) which is AI-automated. The custom-by-Michael version becomes what Accelerator clients get. |
| Community ($47/mo) | OTO 3 (conditional) | No longer a downsell. Now bundled as 30-day trial with OTO 1/Downsell. Price increases to $97/mo to reflect actual value (calls, DFY templates, access). |

### ADDED to checkout flow

| Product | Position | What It Does |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| DFY Offer Build ($197) | OTO 1 | AI builds their ICP, offer doc, landing page copy, ad hooks. 30-day community trial included. |
| DFY Lite ($97) | Downsell to OTO 1 | AI builds ICP + offer doc only. 30-day community trial included. |
| "What's Working Now" ($37/mo) | OTO 2 | Monthly newsletter — tested offers, templates, breakdowns. Low-friction recurring. (Mirrors Terrance's continuity offer.) |

### UNCHANGED

| Product | Position | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| $47 Front-End | Entry point | Same product, same price |
| $37 DM Scripts | Bump 1 | Same |
| $67 Templates | Bump 2 | Same |
| $97 Playbook | Bump 3 | Same |
| $57 One-Page Funnel | Standalone entry | Same — alternate ad entry point |
| $97 Funnel Snapshot | Standalone entry | Same — GHL affiliate trigger |
| $5K Accelerator | Backend | Same product, new sales channel (community instead of email) |
| GHL Training Portal | Delivery | All products still deliver here |
| GHL Affiliate | Passive revenue | Unchanged |

## What Sprint Becomes

Sprint doesn't disappear — it becomes community content.

| Sprint Element | Where It Goes |
|----------------|--------------|
| 4-week curriculum (Extract → Validate → Build → Launch) | Community learning path — self-paced, always available |
| Weekly group calls | Community hot seat calls (same format, now for all members) |
| Midweek check-ins | Community prompts (same cadence) |
| DM access to Michael | Community DM access (same) |
| Accountability tracking | Community feature |
| Homework reviews | Hot seat format on calls |

**The Sprint was $297 for 4 weeks of access. Now it's community content that keeps people paying $97/mo for as long as they're building.** A Sprint buyer paid $297 once. A community member at $97/mo who stays 6 months pays $582. Stays 12 months: $1,164.

## Implementation Sequence

**Phase 1 (Week 1-2): Test without breaking anything**
- Build DFY OTO page in GHL
- Build DFY Lite downsell page
- Set up onboarding form + Claude API integration
- Bundle 30-day community trial with both
- Keep Sprint and Blueprint OTO pages live as fallback

**Phase 2 (Week 3-4): Swap OTOs**
- Replace Sprint OTO with DFY OTO in checkout flow
- Replace Blueprint OTO with Newsletter continuity OTO
- Move Sprint curriculum into community
- Set community price to $97/mo

**Phase 3 (Month 2): Optimize**
- Track DFY take rate vs. old Sprint take rate
- Track community trial → paid conversion rate
- Track Accelerator inquiries from community
- Adjust pricing if needed ($197 DFY might go to $297 if take rate holds)

**Phase 4 (Month 3+): Scale**
- Add DFY templates of the month to community
- Open Accelerator spots in community first
- Raise community price to $197/mo once value is proven
- Test second DFY variant (offer + funnel build for $397)

## Decision Required

This restructure has one key tension with Client Ready's current philosophy:

**Current:** "No sales calls. No subscriptions. Sprint is one-time $297. Done."
**Proposed:** Community is a subscription ($97/mo). That's recurring.

The reframe: The community isn't a subscription you're locked into. It's month-to-month — cancel anytime. No annual commitment. You stay because the calls and templates are worth it. You leave when you've outgrown it (or graduated to Accelerator).

Terrance's framing: "I don't want to sell annual because I want the freedom to pivot." Same energy as "test, validate, build" — stay flexible.
