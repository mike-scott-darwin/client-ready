## Week 1: Extract and Validate

**Goal:** Know exactly what you sell and to whom — tested with real people.

**Unlocks:** Monday of Week 1

---

### Module 1.1 — Zone of Genius Deep Dive

Find the ONE problem you're uniquely positioned to solve. Not three problems. Not a category. One specific pain point where your experience gives you an unfair advantage.

**What you'll learn:**
- The Zone of Genius framework (expertise + experience + energy)
- How to narrow from "I help people with business" to a specific, sellable problem
- Why solving one problem well beats solving five problems poorly
- The "dinner party test" for knowing you've found it

**Supporting Document:** Zone of Genius Extraction Worksheet — guided questions that walk you through identifying your expertise intersection, with examples from real coaching businesses.

---

### Module 1.2 — Ideal Client Profile

Build a profile of a real person — not a marketing avatar with a fake name and stock photo. Someone you've actually helped or someone you know who fits.

**What you'll learn:**
- Why "women aged 25-45 who want transformation" is useless
- The "day in their life" method for understanding real pain
- How to identify buying triggers (what makes them act NOW)
- Where your ideal client already hangs out online

**Supporting Document:** Ideal Client Profile Template — fill-in framework covering their situation, pain points, failed solutions, buying triggers, and where to find them. Includes a completed example.

---

### Module 1.3 — Offer Construction

Build the actual offer — what's included, what you promise, how you deliver, and what you charge. This is the document you'll use for everything downstream.

**What you'll learn:**
- The offer stack framework (core offer + bonuses + guarantee)
- How to price without guessing (value-based, not cost-based)
- What to include vs. what to cut (less is more)
- Writing the transformation promise in one sentence

**Supporting Document:** Offer Document Template — complete fill-in template for your offer including transformation promise, what's included, delivery format, pricing rationale, and guarantee. This becomes your reference for every piece of copy you write.

---

### Module 1.4 — Warm Market Validation

Test your offer with real people before you build anything. Five conversations will tell you more than five weeks of overthinking.

**What you'll learn:**
- How to have validation conversations without being salesy
- The 5-question framework that gets honest feedback
- What "validated" actually means (and what it doesn't)
- How to adjust your offer based on what you hear

**Supporting Document:** Validation Conversation Scripts — exact scripts for reaching out, asking questions, and interpreting responses. Includes a tracking sheet for recording what you learn from each conversation.

---

### Week 1 Homework

**Submit to the WhatsApp group by Sunday:**

1. Your completed offer document (photo or PDF of the filled template)
2. Screenshots of 3 or more validation conversations
3. One voice note (under 2 minutes): what surprised you from the conversations and what you changed in your offer as a result

**Time estimate:** 2-3 hours watching training, 4-5 hours implementing and having conversations.

## Week 2: Build Your Funnel

**Goal:** Landing page live, checkout working, email automation running.

**Unlocks:** Monday of Week 2

---

### Module 2.1 — Landing Page Structure

The anatomy of a page that converts. Not design theory — the specific sections you need, in what order, and why each one matters.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 7-section landing page framework (headline, problem, solution, proof, offer, FAQ, CTA)
- Why long-form pages outperform short ones for cold traffic
- How to structure your page for people who scroll and people who read
- The headline formula that works for coaching and service offers

**Supporting Document:** Landing Page Blueprint — section-by-section outline with purpose, copy prompts, and a completed example page. Print it out and work through it section by section.

---

### Module 2.2 — Writing Copy That Sounds Like You

Fill-in frameworks, not generic swipe files. Your page should sound like you talking to someone who needs what you offer — not like a marketing template.

**What you'll learn:**
- The "conversation to copy" method (write like you talk, then tighten)
- Fill-in copy formulas for each landing page section
- How to write about results without making claims you can't back up
- The difference between benefits (what they get) and transformation (who they become)

**Supporting Document:** Copy Framework Workbook — fill-in-the-blank templates for every section of your landing page. Each template includes the formula, an example, and space for your version.

---

### Module 2.3 — Checkout and Order Bumps

Set up your checkout to maximize revenue per buyer. A good bump offer can add 30-40% to your average order value with zero extra traffic.

**What you'll learn:**
- Checkout page optimization (what to include, what to remove)
- The 3 types of bump offers that work (quick win, deeper dive, done-for-you element)
- How to write a bump description that converts in under 50 words
- Setting up checkout in GHL step by step

**Supporting Document:** Checkout Setup Guide — step-by-step GHL walkthrough with screenshots, plus a bump offer brainstorming worksheet with examples for coaching and service businesses.

---

### Module 2.4 — Email Welcome Sequence

The 10-day email sequence that turns buyers into fans and moves them up your product ladder. Each email has a specific job — no filler.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 10-email framework (what each email does and when it sends)
- Email 1: the critical first impression (delivery + what to do next)
- The nurture-to-ascension arc (build trust before asking for more)
- Subject line formulas that get opens

**Supporting Document:** Email Sequence Templates — all 10 emails pre-written as fill-in frameworks with subject lines, body copy structure, and CTAs. Includes notes on timing and automation setup in GHL.

---

### Week 2 Homework

**Submit to the WhatsApp group by Sunday:**

1. Your live landing page link
2. Screenshot of your checkout page with bump offer configured
3. Screenshot of your email automation active in GHL (showing the sequence is set up and ready)

**Time estimate:** 2-3 hours watching training, 5-7 hours building and writing.

## Week 3: Launch Traffic

**Goal:** First campaign live with real clicks coming in.

**Unlocks:** Monday of Week 3

---

### Module 3.1 — Campaign Architecture

How Meta ads actually work — no jargon, no guru tactics. The structure that lets you test efficiently and scale what works.

**What you'll learn:**
- Campaign, ad set, audience — what each level does
- The testing structure (how to test 3+ variations without wasting budget)
- Budget allocation for your first campaign (start small, scale with data)
- Audience targeting for coaching and service businesses

**Supporting Document:** Campaign Structure Template — visual diagram of the recommended campaign structure with audience targeting suggestions for coaching businesses. Includes a budget calculator for your first 2 weeks of ads.

---

### Module 3.2 — Writing Ad Copy

Three angles, three ads. Not one ad you hope works — three distinct approaches so you have data to learn from on day one.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 3-angle framework (problem agitation, transformation, identity callout)
- Hook formulas that stop the scroll (first line is everything)
- Primary text structure (hook, story, offer, CTA)
- How to write for cold traffic who has never heard of you

**Supporting Document:** Ad Copy Templates — fill-in templates for all 3 angles with hook variations, primary text frameworks, and CTA options. Includes 10 example hooks from real coaching ads that work.

---

### Module 3.3 — Creative Direction

What to use for images and video when you have no designer and no budget. Simple creative that performs better than overproduced content.

**What you'll learn:**
- Why simple creative often outperforms polished content
- The 4 creative formats that work for coaching offers (selfie video, text overlay, before/after, screenshot)
- Free tools for creating ad images and simple videos
- Image and video specs for Meta placements

**Supporting Document:** Creative Toolkit — specifications for every Meta placement, recommended free tools with links, and 10 visual templates you can customize. Includes a shot list for filming simple talking-head ad videos with your phone.

---

### Module 3.4 — Launch and First 48 Hours

Press publish and know what you're looking at. The first 48 hours tell you a lot — if you know what to look for.

**What you'll learn:**
- The pre-launch checklist (tracking pixel, payment test, automation test)
- How to read the first 24-48 hours without panicking
- The 3 metrics that matter at launch (CTR, CPC, landing page conversion)
- When to worry and when to wait

**Supporting Document:** Launch Checklist and Metrics Tracker — pre-launch verification checklist (15 items), plus a daily metrics tracking sheet for your first 14 days. Includes benchmark ranges so you know if your numbers are healthy.

---

### Week 3 Homework

**Submit to the WhatsApp group by Sunday:**

1. Screenshot of your live campaign in Ads Manager (showing ads are running)
2. Your 3 ad copy variations (paste into WhatsApp or share as a document)
3. Screenshot of your first 48-hour metrics

**Time estimate:** 2-3 hours watching training, 4-5 hours writing, creating, and launching.

## Week 4: Read, Optimize, and Plan Forward

**Goal:** Understand your data, make smart decisions, and build a 90-day plan.

**Unlocks:** Monday of Week 4

---

### Module 4.1 — Reading Your Numbers

The 5 metrics that actually matter at this stage. Ignore everything else. Most people drown in data — you'll know exactly what to look at and what it means.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 5 metrics: CTR, CPC, landing page conversion rate, cost per lead, cost per sale
- What each metric tells you (and what it doesn't)
- Benchmark ranges for coaching and service offers
- How to spot the bottleneck (where people are dropping off)

**Supporting Document:** Metrics Dashboard Template — a single-page template where you plug in your numbers and it shows you exactly where the bottleneck is. Includes benchmark ranges and a decision tree for what to fix first.

---

### Module 4.2 — Kill or Scale Decisions

A simple framework for deciding what stays and what goes. No gut feelings — data-driven decisions with clear rules.

**What you'll learn:**
- The kill/keep/scale framework (when to cut, when to wait, when to double down)
- Minimum data thresholds before making decisions (don't kill too early)
- How to reallocate budget from losers to winners
- The "one change at a time" rule for optimization

**Supporting Document:** Optimization Decision Framework — flowchart for making kill/keep/scale decisions based on your actual metrics. Includes worked examples so you can follow the logic with your own numbers.

---

### Module 4.3 — Quick Wins for Optimization

Small changes that move numbers. Not a full rebuild — targeted adjustments to your headline, ad hooks, and email subject lines.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 3 highest-leverage optimization points (headline, ad hook, email subject)
- How to write 3 new headline variations based on what you've learned
- A/B testing basics (test one thing at a time)
- When optimization isn't the answer (sometimes the offer needs to change)

**Supporting Document:** Optimization Playbook — a prioritized list of optimizations ranked by impact and effort. Includes new headline formulas, hook variations, and subject line frameworks for round 2.

---

### Module 4.4 — Your 90-Day Roadmap

What to do in months 2 and 3 without hand-holding. The Sprint gives you the foundation — the roadmap tells you exactly what to build next.

**What you'll learn:**
- The 90-day milestone framework (month 1 = launch, month 2 = optimize, month 3 = scale)
- How to set realistic targets based on your Sprint data
- When to add new offers vs. optimize the current one
- The upgrade path (Blueprint for done-for-you strategy, Accelerator for hands-on mentorship)

**Supporting Document:** 90-Day Roadmap Template — month-by-month planning template with milestone targets, weekly priorities, and decision triggers for when to add new products or scale spending.

---

### Week 4 Homework

**Submit to the WhatsApp group by Sunday:**

1. Your completed metrics dashboard (filled template with your actual numbers)
2. Your kill/keep/scale decisions with reasoning (which ads stayed, which got cut, why)
3. Your 90-day roadmap (filled template)

- **Screenshots over descriptions** — show your work, don't just describe it

- **Homework reviewed within 24 hours** of submission
- **Questions answered same day** (usually faster)
- **Honest, direct feedback** — if something isn't working, I'll tell you and tell you how to fix it
- **No fluff** — practical answers, not motivational speeches

## FAQ

**Q: How much time do I need per week?**
A: Plan for 5-10 hours. About 2-3 hours watching the training modules and 3-7 hours implementing and building. Evenings and weekends work fine.

**Q: Can I do this while working a full-time job?**
A: Yes. That's exactly who this is designed for. No live calls to schedule around. Watch the training when it suits you. Submit homework by Sunday.

**Q: What if I fall behind?**
A: Post in the WhatsApp group. We'll figure out a catch-up plan. The guarantee covers this — I keep supporting you until you launch.

**Q: What if I already have a landing page?**
A: Great — you'll move faster in Week 2. Use the training to audit and optimize what you have instead of starting from scratch.

**Q: Do I need tech experience?**
A: Basic computer skills. If you can use Google Docs and follow step-by-step screenshots, you can do this. The GHL setup guide walks you through everything.

**Q: What happens after 30 days?**
A: You have a launched funnel, 16 supporting documents, and a 90-day roadmap. Most Sprint members are self-sufficient. If you want more hands-on help, the Blueprint and Accelerator options are available.
