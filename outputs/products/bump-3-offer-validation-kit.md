## Prompt 1: The "Would I Buy This?" Test

This prompt puts your offer through the lens of a skeptical buyer who's been burned before.

```
I'm going to paste my coaching offer document below. Analyze it as if you were a skeptical potential customer who:
- Has been burned by courses before
- Is short on time and money
- Needs to justify this purchase to their spouse

For each section (problem, solution, mechanism, proof, price), give me:
1. What's working (be specific)
2. What's weak or unclear
3. One sentence I could add to make it stronger

Be brutally honest. I'd rather fix this now than waste money on ads.

---

[PASTE YOUR OFFER DOCUMENT HERE]
```

**What to do with the output:** Fix any "weak or unclear" items before moving on. Add the suggested sentences if they fit your voice.

---

## Prompt 2: The Clarity Check

Seven yes/no questions that expose confusion in your offer.

```
Read my offer document below and answer these questions with YES or NO, plus a one-sentence explanation:

1. Could a stranger understand what I sell in 10 seconds?
2. Is the transformation crystal clear?
3. Is there a specific mechanism (not just "coaching" or "support")?
4. Is the price justified by the value promised?
5. Are there at least 2-3 pieces of proof or credibility?
6. Is there urgency or a reason to buy now?
7. Would a tired, distracted person on their phone "get it"?

For any NO answers, tell me exactly what's missing and give me example language I could use.

---

[PASTE YOUR OFFER DOCUMENT HERE]
```

**What to do with the output:** Every NO is a conversion leak. Fix them in order of importance (questions 1-3 are most critical).

---

## Prompt 3: The Objection Finder

Surfaces every doubt a potential buyer might have.

```
You are a potential customer who is interested but hesitant. Read my offer document and list every objection, doubt, or question that would stop you from buying.

For each objection:
1. State the objection clearly
2. Rate how serious it is (1-10)
3. Suggest copy I could add to handle it

Focus especially on:
- "This won't work for my situation"
- "I don't have time"
- "I've tried similar things before"
- "This seems too good to be true"
- "I don't trust this person yet"

---

[PASTE YOUR OFFER DOCUMENT HERE]
```

**What to do with the output:** Address any objection rated 7+ immediately. Add the suggested copy to your sales page FAQ or body copy.

## Prompt 4: The Mechanism Audit

Tests whether your "how" is strong enough to differentiate you.

```
Analyze my offer's mechanism (the HOW behind the transformation). A strong mechanism:
- Is named and specific (not just "coaching" or "my system")
- Explains WHY it works
- Feels proprietary or unique
- Creates an "aha moment"

Review my offer document and tell me:
1. What is my current mechanism? (If unclear, say so)
2. Is it named? Is the name memorable?
3. Does it explain why this works when other things didn't?
4. Rate my mechanism 1-10 and tell me how to strengthen it

---

[PASTE YOUR OFFER DOCUMENT HERE]
```

**What to do with the output:** If your mechanism scores below 7, stop everything and fix it. A weak mechanism = a commodity offer that competes on price.

## Prompt 5: The "First 10 Seconds" Test

Simulates what happens when someone lands on your page and decides whether to keep reading.

```
Pretend you just landed on my sales page and you're scrolling fast on your phone. You'll give me 10 seconds max.

Based on my offer document:
1. What's the first thing that catches your attention?
2. Do you immediately know what's being sold?
3. Do you immediately know who it's for?
4. Is there a reason to keep reading?
5. What would make you stop scrolling and actually read?

Give me a rewritten headline and subheadline that would pass the 10-second test.

---

[PASTE YOUR OFFER DOCUMENT HERE]
```

**What to do with the output:** Use the rewritten headline/subheadline as a starting point. Test it against your current version.

# Part 4: Before/After Examples

See the specific changes that transformed weak offers into converting ones.

## Example 1: Fitness Coach

**BEFORE:**
> "I help busy professionals get fit and healthy through personalized coaching and accountability."

**AFTER:**
> "The 90-Day Dad Bod Destroyer: Lose 20 lbs without giving up beer or missing your kid's soccer games. For dads 35-50 who've tried everything."

**What changed:**
- Added specific outcome (20 lbs)
- Named the mechanism (90-Day Dad Bod Destroyer)
- Addressed specific objections (beer, kids' activities)
- Defined the audience precisely (dads 35-50)

---

## Example 2: Business Coach

**BEFORE:**
> "I help entrepreneurs scale their business to 6 figures with my proven strategies and mindset work."

**AFTER:**
> "The $10K Month Method: Land 3-5 high-ticket clients in 60 days using DMs (no ads, no content treadmill). For coaches making $2-5K/month who are tired of trading time for money."

**What changed:**
- Specific outcome (3-5 clients, $10K)
- Clear timeline (60 days)
- Named what you DON'T need (ads, content)
- Defined current state ($2-5K/month)

---

## Example 3: Life Coach

**BEFORE:**
> "Transform your life with holistic coaching that addresses mind, body, and spirit. I'll help you find clarity, purpose, and fulfillment."

**AFTER:**
> "The Clarity Accelerator: Know your next career move in 14 days — without quitting your job first. For burned-out professionals who feel stuck but can't afford to make the wrong choice."

**What changed:**
- Single specific outcome (career clarity)
- Tight timeline (14 days)
- Addressed the real fear (wrong choice)
- Defined emotional state (burned-out, stuck)

---

## Example 4: Marketing Consultant

**BEFORE:**
> "I help small businesses grow with digital marketing strategies including SEO, social media, and email marketing."

**AFTER:**
> "The Local Lead Machine: 50+ qualified leads per month for your [industry] business using Google and Facebook ads. We build it, we run it, you close deals. Setup: $2,500. Monthly: $1,500."

**What changed:**
- Specific deliverable (50+ leads/month)
- Clear scope (Google + Facebook ads)
- Division of labor clear (we run, you close)
- Price stated upfront (builds trust)

---

## Example 5: Relationship Coach

**BEFORE:**
> "Heal your relationship and create deeper connection with your partner through my compassionate coaching approach."

**AFTER:**
> "The Reconnection Protocol: Go from roommates to lovers in 30 days — even if you've tried couples therapy and it didn't work. For couples who aren't ready to give up but don't know what else to try."

**What changed:**
- Vivid before/after (roommates → lovers)
- Timeline (30 days)
- Addressed failed solutions (couples therapy)
- Named the emotional state (not ready to give up)
