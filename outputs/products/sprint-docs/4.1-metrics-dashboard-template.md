## The Bottleneck Finder

Work through these questions in order. Stop at the first "yes."

**1. Is your CTR below 1%?**
- YES: Your ad hook isn't working. People see the ad but don't click. Fix the ad — specifically the first line and the creative.
- NO: Move to question 2.

**2. Is your CTR good but CPC is above 5?**
- YES: Your targeting may be too narrow, or there's high competition for your audience. Try broader targeting.
- NO: Move to question 3.

**3. Are clicks coming but landing page conversion is below 1%?**
- YES: Your landing page has a problem. Check: Is the headline clear? Does it match what the ad promised? Is there social proof? Is the page loading fast on mobile?
- NO: Move to question 4.

**4. Are people buying but AOV is below 80?**
- YES: Your bumps aren't converting. Review bump descriptions — are they compelling? Is the value obvious? Consider testing higher bump prices.
- NO: Move to question 5.

**5. Is everything working but CPA is above your target?**
- YES: You might need more data (keep running), or your offer price needs to increase, or you need to optimize your best-performing ad further.
- NO: Your funnel is working. Move to scaling.
