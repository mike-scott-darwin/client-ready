# Copy Framework Workbook

**Sprint Module 2.2 — Estimated time: 2-3 hours**

---

## How to Use This

This workbook gives you fill-in templates for every section of your landing page. Work through it top to bottom. Don't try to be perfect — write fast, then edit.

**The Conversation to Copy Method:** Before you start writing, record yourself explaining your offer to a friend for 5 minutes. Transcribe it. Clean it up. That's 80% of your landing page right there. You already know how to talk about what you do — the templates below just give it structure.

## Voice Check

Read your copy out loud. Then answer:

- [ ] Does it sound like you talking to a friend?
- [ ] Or does it sound like a marketing template?
- [ ] Would you say these words in a real conversation?
- [ ] Is there any sentence that feels fake or forced?

If anything sounds like "marketing speak," rewrite it the way you'd actually say it.
