# Ideal Client Profile Template

**Sprint Module 1.2 — Estimated time: 30-45 minutes**

---

## How to Use This

Build a profile of a real person. Not a marketing avatar with a fake name and stock photo. Someone you've actually helped, or someone you know who fits.

**Why real people:** Avatars are made up. Real people have real problems you've actually seen. When you write copy later, you'll write to THIS person — and it'll sound authentic instead of generic.

## Completed Example

**Name:** Sarah

**Situation:** 34, senior accountant at a mid-size firm, married, one kid. Makes good money but feels trapped. Has 5 years of experience helping small businesses with their finances — sees the same mistakes over and over. Wants to coach small business owners on financial planning but has no idea how to package it.

**3am Worry:** "I've been saying I'll do this for two years. I've bought courses. I've started a website twice. Everyone else seems to have it figured out and I'm still in the same place."

**What She's Tried:** Watched 50+ YouTube videos on coaching businesses. Bought a course on building an online business (too advanced, skipped to Module 7, got lost). Started an Instagram account, posted for 3 weeks, stopped. Had 2 free calls with people who said "this is great" but never paid.

**Buying Triggers:** Her company announced restructuring (external). Realized she's been "thinking about it" for 2 years with nothing to show (internal). Tipping point: Saw a former colleague launch a consulting business and felt jealous — then angry at herself.

**Where She Hangs Out:** Instagram, LinkedIn. Facebook group for women in finance. Listens to "Online Marketing Made Easy" podcast. Follows Marie Forleo and a few business coaches on Instagram.
