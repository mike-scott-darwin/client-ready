# Offer Document Template

**Sprint Module 1.3 — Estimated time: 45-60 minutes**

---

## How to Use This

This is your single source of truth. Every piece of copy you write — landing page, emails, ads — starts here. Get this right and everything downstream gets easier.

**The goal:** By the end of this document, you'll be able to explain your offer in one sentence, know exactly what's included, and have a price you can defend.

## Offer Validation Checklist

Before you test this offer with real people, check these boxes:

- [ ] The problem is specific (not a category like "business growth" but a specific pain)
- [ ] You can describe the transformation in one sentence
- [ ] The "what's included" list is concrete (no vague "coaching" or "support")
- [ ] The price has a rationale (not just "seems right")
- [ ] You can explain who this is NOT for (as clearly as who it IS for)

If you can't check all five, keep refining before you move to validation.

## Completed Example

**Transformation:** "I take freelance designers who are drowning in project chaos from overwhelmed and undercharging to organized and profitable in 6 weeks."

**ONE Problem:** Freelancers who spend more time managing projects than doing design work, losing money on every engagement because they can't scope accurately.

**Who It's For:** Freelance designers doing 3-10 projects at a time, with at least 1 year of experience, who are tired of scope creep eating their profits.

**What's Included:**
| Component | What They Get | What It Does |
|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| Scoping Framework | Template + training for accurate project scoping | Stop undercharging and scope creep |
| Client Workflow System | Step-by-step process from inquiry to delivery | Never wonder "what's next" again |
| 4 Group Calls | Weekly 60-min calls with hot seat feedback | Get unstuck fast |
| Template Library | Proposals, contracts, onboarding docs | Don't start from scratch |
| Community Access | Private group for questions between calls | Never build alone |

**Price:** 497 one-time

**Guarantee:** "Implement the system and your next 3 projects will be scoped accurately — or I keep working with you until they are."

**Name:** The Freelance Delivery System

**One-Liner:** "I help freelance designers stop losing money on projects by installing a scoping and delivery system that takes 6 weeks."
