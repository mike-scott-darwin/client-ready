# Email Sequence Templates

**Sprint Module 2.4 — Estimated time: 2-3 hours**

---

## How to Use This

10 emails. 10 days. Each email has a specific job. Fill in the brackets with your own details, set up the automation in GHL, and you're done.

**Timing:** Email 1 sends immediately on purchase. Emails 2-10 send at 8am, one per day.

**Tone:** These should sound like you writing to one person — not a mass email. Conversational. Direct. No corporate speak.

## Email 1: Delivery + First Win

**Send:** Immediately on purchase
**Subject:** You're in — here's your first win
**Alt subjects:** "Your access is ready" / "Start here (takes 5 minutes)"

**Purpose:** Deliver the product and get them to take ONE action right now.

---

Hey [first name],

You're in. Here's your access: [LINK]

Before you dive into everything, do this one thing first:

[Describe the single fastest win they can get — the one action that takes 5-10 minutes and gives them a tangible result. Pull this from your product.]

That's it. Start there. Everything else builds on top of it.

If you run into any issues accessing the material, reply to this email and I'll sort it out.

Talk soon,
[Your name]

---

**Length:** 80-120 words. Short. They just bought — don't overwhelm them.

---

## Email 2: Origin Story

**Send:** Day 2, 8am
**Subject:** Why I do this (honest answer)
**Alt subjects:** "The real reason I built this" / "My honest answer"

**Purpose:** Build connection through your story. Not a highlight reel — the real version.

---

Hey [first name],

People ask me why I [do what you do / built this product].

The honest answer: [Your origin story in 3-4 sentences. What happened, what you learned, why it matters to you.]

That experience taught me something: [The key insight that drives your business].

That's why I built [product name]. Not because [what people assume]. Because [the real reason — connected to your experience].

[One sentence about what this means for THEM — bridge your story to their situation.]

More tomorrow.

[Your name]

---

**Length:** 150-200 words. Personal but not rambling.

## Email 3a: Advanced Tips (for buyers who opened the product)

**Send:** Day 3, 8am — ONLY if they've accessed the product
**Subject:** Now that you've started...
**Alt subjects:** "Quick tip for [product name]" / "Get more from what you just did"

**Purpose:** Reward engagement. Give them a tip that makes the product more valuable.

---

Hey [first name],

Now that you've started [specific part of the product they accessed], here's something that'll help:

[Specific advanced tip — something they wouldn't know unless someone told them. Be concrete.]

Most people [common mistake with this step]. Instead, try [your recommended approach].

[One more quick tip or nuance.]

Keep going. You're ahead of most people just by starting.

[Your name]

---

## Email 3b: Quick Start (for buyers who haven't opened the product)

**Send:** Day 3, 8am — ONLY if they haven't accessed the product
**Subject:** Haven't started yet?
**Alt subjects:** "5 minutes — that's all you need" / "Quick question"

**Purpose:** Gentle nudge. No guilt. Just make it easy.

---

Hey [first name],

Noticed you haven't jumped into [product name] yet. No judgment — life happens.

Here's the fastest way to start: [Describe the single easiest first step. Make it sound like 5 minutes, not a project.]

That's it. You don't need to go through everything. Just start with that one piece.

Here's your access link again: [LINK]

[Your name]

---

**GHL Setup for Day 3 Branch:**
1. Create an automation trigger: "Wait 3 days after purchase"
2. Add condition: "Has tag: product-accessed" (set this tag when they click the product link)
3. If YES → send Email 3a
4. If NO → send Email 3b

## GHL Setup Notes

### Creating the Automation

1. Go to **Automations → Create Workflow**
2. Trigger: **Payment Received** (for your product)
3. Add actions in sequence:
   - **Send Email** (Day 1 — immediate)
   - **Wait** 1 day
   - **Send Email** (Day 2)
   - **Wait** 1 day
   - **If/Else** branch (check "product-accessed" tag)
     - YES → **Send Email** (Day 3a)
     - NO → **Send Email** (Day 3b)
   - **Wait** 1 day
   - **Send Email** (Day 4)
   - Continue pattern through Day 10

### Tracking Product Access

1. In your product delivery email (Day 1), use a tracked link
2. Create automation: When link is clicked → Add tag "product-accessed"
3. This tag is what the Day 3 branch checks

### Subject Line Testing

Test different subject lines over time. Here are 10 extra formulas:

1. "Quick question about [topic]"
2. "This changed everything for me"
3. "Don't make this mistake"
4. "The simplest way to [result]"
5. "Real talk about [topic]"
6. "[Number] minutes to [result]"
7. "What nobody tells you about [topic]"
8. "I was wrong about [thing]"
9. "The [topic] shortcut"
10. "Read this before you [action]"
