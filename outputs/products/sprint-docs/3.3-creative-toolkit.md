## How to Use This

You don't need a designer. You don't need a big budget. Simple creative often outperforms polished content because it looks real — not like an ad.

---

## Meta Ad Placement Specs

| Placement | Image Size | Video Size | Aspect Ratio |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Feed (Facebook/Instagram) | 1080x1080 | 1080x1080 | 1:1 (square) |
| Stories / Reels | 1080x1920 | 1080x1920 | 9:16 (vertical) |
| Right Column (desktop) | 1200x628 | N/A | 1.91:1 (landscape) |

**Start with 1:1 (square).** It works across the most placements. Make a 9:16 version if you have time.

## The 4 Creative Formats That Work

### Format 1: Selfie Video

You talking to camera. 30-60 seconds. This is the most authentic format and often the best performer.

**How to film:**
- Use your phone camera (front-facing)
- Natural light — face a window
- Eye level (prop phone on a stack of books)
- Look at the LENS, not the screen
- Quiet room, no background noise
- One take is fine. Don't over-rehearse.

**Shot list:**

| Video | Angle | Opening Line |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| Video 1 | Problem Agitation | "Are you still [pain point]? Here's why..." |
| Video 2 | Transformation | "Imagine waking up to [result]..." |
| Video 3 | Identity | "If you're a [identity] who [situation], this is for you." |

**Length:** 30-60 seconds. Shorter beats longer for cold traffic.

**Captions:** Always add captions. Most people watch without sound. Use CapCut (free) to auto-generate them.

---

### Format 2: Text Overlay on Image

Bold text on a simple background or photo. The text IS the hook — the image just stops the scroll.

**Templates:**

| Template | Layout | Example |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Question | Bold question on solid color background | "Still explaining what you do at every event?" |
| Statement + "Here's why" | Statement on top, "Here's why" below | "Your content isn't converting. Here's why." |
| List | "3 things [audience] gets wrong about [topic]" | "3 reasons your coaching offer isn't selling" |

**Design rules:**
- Bold, clean sans-serif font (no script or decorative fonts)
- High contrast (dark text on light background, or white text on dark)
- Maximum 10 words on the image
- The rest goes in the ad copy (primary text)

---

### Format 3: Before/After

Split image showing the transformation. For coaching, this isn't physical — it's situational.

**Examples:**

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| "Confused about your offer" | "Clients booking without a call" |
| "Posting content, no sales" | "Validated offer, first customers" |
| "Trying everything, nothing works" | "One system, running on autopilot" |

**How to make it:** Use Canva. Two columns. Left side has the "before" text, right side has the "after" text. Simple backgrounds.

---

### Format 4: Screenshot / Social Proof

A screenshot of a DM, testimonial, or result. Feels organic — like someone sharing a win, not running an ad.

**Works best when you have:**
- DM conversations where someone praises your work
- Testimonial text from a client
- A result you can screenshot (payment notification, client message, etc.)

**If you don't have these yet:** Skip this format for now. Use the other three.

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Keep it simple — clean beats flashy
- Use natural photos over stock photos
- Add captions to all videos
- Test different formats (image vs video)
- Make the first frame (or first line of text) stop the scroll

**Don't:**
- Use stock photos of handshakes or laptops on beaches
- Overcomplicate the design
- Use more than 2 fonts
- Make text too small to read on mobile
- Forget to check how it looks on a phone screen (not just desktop)

---

## Creative Checklist

Before submitting your ads:

- [ ] At least 3 creative pieces ready (1 per ad)
- [ ] All images are 1080x1080 minimum
- [ ] Text on images is readable on a phone screen
- [ ] Video has captions
- [ ] The first frame / first line stops the scroll
- [ ] Nothing looks like a generic ad template
- [ ] You've checked how each creative looks in the Ads Manager preview
