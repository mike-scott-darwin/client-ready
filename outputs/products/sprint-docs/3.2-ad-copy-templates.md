## How to Use This

You're writing 3 ads — one per angle. Each angle approaches the same offer from a different direction. This gives you data on what resonates, not just one shot in the dark.

**The first line is everything.** People decide whether to keep reading in the first 2 seconds. Your hook (first line) does all the heavy lifting.

## 10 Proven Hook Examples

Adapt these for your offer:

1. "You've been posting content for 6 months. How many clients did it bring?"
2. "Most coaching businesses fail because they skip this one step."
3. "I spent 3 years building the wrong business. Here's what I'd do instead."
4. "Coaches who charge premium don't work harder. They do this differently."
5. "Still explaining what you do at every networking event? There's a fix."
6. "What if your offer sold itself — without a single DM?"
7. "The reason your funnel isn't converting has nothing to do with the funnel."
8. "If your business feels like a second job, it's not a scaling problem."
9. "Everyone says 'just post more content.' Nobody tells you why that doesn't work."
10. "You don't need another course. You need a validated offer."

## CTA Button

For cold traffic (people who've never heard of you): **"Learn More"**

This is lower commitment than "Buy Now" or "Sign Up." They're not ready to commit — they're ready to be curious.
