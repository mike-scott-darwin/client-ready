# 100+ Profitable Offer Ideas

Here's the truth: most coaches get stuck at "what do I even sell?" for months. They sit and wait for the perfect idea. Wrong.

You don't need a perfect idea. You need a specific one you can test.

This is a list of 100+ specific offer ideas, organized by market. They're deliberately narrow — a narrow offer for a narrow person sells faster than a broad offer for everyone. Read these to prime the pump, then swap in your own skill, your own audience, and your own angle. Pick one. Test it. Move.

None of these promise anyone will make money. They describe the *outcome you help someone reach*. That's the offer.

---

## How to Use This List

1. Skim the market closest to what you already do.
2. Circle 3 ideas that make you think "I could actually deliver that this week."
3. Rewrite each one in your own words, with your own client in mind.
4. Take the one that scares you the least to launch, and put it in front of real people.

Specificity is the whole game. "A fitness program" doesn't sell. "A 3-week plan to get a busy dad back to a daily 20-minute workout he'll actually keep" sells. Notice the difference in every idea below.
