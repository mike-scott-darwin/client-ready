## Part 4: Messaging Maps

Consistent messaging across all touchpoints.

### Core Message Template

| Element | Your Version |
|---------|--------------|
| **One-liner** | I help [who] achieve [what] through [how] |
| **Problem statement** | Most [who] struggle with [problem] because [reason] |
| **Solution statement** | [Offer] solves this by [mechanism] |
| **Transformation** | Go from [before] to [after] in [timeframe] |
| **Proof point** | [Specific result or credential] |

### Channel Adaptations

**For DMs:**
```
Short, conversational. Lead with curiosity, not pitch.
"Hey вҖ” saw you're working on [X]. I help [who] with [outcome]. Interested in chatting?"
```

**For Landing Pages:**
```
Full message. Headline = transformation promise. Body = proof + mechanism.
"Validate your $5K+ offer before you build anything вҖ” in one afternoon."
```

**For Emails:**
```
Story-driven. One idea per email. Clear CTA.
"Last week a client asked me [question]. Here's what I told her..."
```

**For Ads:**
```
Hook first. 3 seconds to stop the scroll.
"Most coaches waste months building offers nobody wants. Here's the fix."
```

## Part 6: Copy-Paste Headlines & Hooks

50+ proven variations. Customize the bracketed sections.

### Headline Formulas

**Transformation Headlines:**
- [Outcome] in [Timeframe] вҖ” Without [Pain They Want to Avoid]
- How to [Achieve Outcome] Even If [Objection]
- The [Adjective] Way to [Outcome] (That [Credibility])
- Stop [Painful Activity]. Start [Desired Activity].
- From [Before State] to [After State] in [Timeframe]

**Curiosity Headlines:**
- The [Outcome] Method Nobody's Talking About
- Why [Common Advice] Is Keeping You [Stuck]
- [Number] [Type of People] Are Doing This Wrong
- What [Authority Figure] Knows About [Topic] That You Don't
- The Real Reason [Outcome] Feels So Hard

**Proof Headlines:**
- How [Client] [Achieved Result] in [Timeframe]
- [Number] [People] Have Used This to [Outcome]
- The Exact [Framework/System] That [Generated Result]
- [Result] вҖ” Here's the Proof
- What [Impressive Number] [People] Know That You Don't

### Hook Variations (For Ads/Videos)

**Question Hooks:**
- "Are you still [doing painful thing]?"
- "What if [desired outcome] was easier than you think?"
- "Why do some [people] [succeed] while others [struggle]?"

**Statement Hooks:**
- "I wasted [time/money] on [thing] before I figured this out."
- "Most [people] will never [achieve outcome]. Here's why."
- "This changed everything for me."

**Pattern Interrupt Hooks:**
- "Stop. Before you [do common mistake], watch this."
- "Unpopular opinion: [controversial statement]"
- "I'm going to say something that might upset some people."
