# Ad Scaling Roadmap — Client Ready $47 Offer

$30/day → $60 → $90 → $200. One angle at a time. Each stage funded by revenue from the last.

---

## The Path

| Stage | Budget | Angle | Trigger | Monthly Cost |
|-------|--------|-------|---------|-------------|
| **1. Validate** | $30/day | [Before the Funnel](./before-the-funnel.md) | Day 1 — launch | ~$900 |
| **2. Expand** | $60/day | + [Content Merry-Go-Round](./content-merry-go-round.md) | 10+ sales, CPA confirmed | ~$1,800 |
| **3. Scale** | $90/day | + [Clarity Unlock](./clarity-unlock.md) | 30 sales validated | ~$2,700 |
| **4. Full** | $200/day | + [Misalignment](./misalignment.md) + CBO | AOV $100+ confirmed | ~$6,000 |

**Method:** ABO (Ad Set Budget Optimization) for Stages 1-3. Each ad set gets its own fixed daily budget. CBO only enters at Stage 4 with proven winners.

**Why sequential:** $30/day can't support 4 ad sets — each would get $7.50/day, not enough signal. One angle at $30/day = each ad gets ~$8-10/day. Clean data within a week. Revenue from each stage funds the next.

---

## The 4 Angles

| # | Angle | Hook | Awareness |
|---|-------|------|-----------|
| 1 | **[Before the Funnel](./before-the-funnel.md)** | "Everyone's selling you funnels. Nobody's asking: do you have an offer worth building one for?" | Problem Aware → Unaware |
| 2 | **[Content Merry-Go-Round](./content-merry-go-round.md)** | "You've been posting for 12 months. Getting likes. Nobody's buying." | Problem Aware |
| 3 | **[Clarity Unlock](./clarity-unlock.md)** | "Most coaches can't explain their offer in one sentence. 5 AI prompts fix that in an afternoon." | Solution Aware |
| 4 | **[Misalignment](./misalignment.md)** | "I burned down a business that was working. Best decision I ever made." | Unaware (belief shift) |

These 4 angles cover all awareness levels. When Before the Funnel and Misalignment perform, you're reaching the unaware market that scales.

**Per angle:** 1 copy block, 3 images (ugly static + product reveal + before/after), 1 video (silent review). 4 ads total. All UGC — real product screenshots, face cam, Notes app. No AI images.

# STAGE 1: VALIDATE

**Budget:** $30/day ABO | **Angle:** Before the Funnel | **Ads:** 4

This is the only angle running. $30/day across 4 ads = ~$8-10/day per ad. At $2 CPC, expect ~15 clicks/day. At 2% CVR, ~9 sales/month. At $100 AOV, ~$900 revenue. Break-even on front-end. Backend + recurring = profit.

### What to Produce (~90 min)

| Ad | Format | Copy | Headline |
|----|--------|------|----------|
| BtF-01 | Ugly Static | P4 Pattern Interrupt | H7 Belief Shift |
| BtF-02 | Product Reveal | P4 Pattern Interrupt | H7 Belief Shift |
| BtF-03 | Before/After | P4 Pattern Interrupt | H7 Belief Shift |
| BtF-V1 | Silent Review Video | P4 Pattern Interrupt | H7 Belief Shift |

Same copy block + headline across all 4 ads. Only the image format changes. Tests which IMAGE stops the scroll — one variable at a time.

- **P4 Pattern Interrupt:** "Everyone sells funnels and nobody asks the obvious question first" — shortest primary, under 60 words, earns spend fast at $30/day
- **H7 Belief Shift:** "Your Marketing Isn't Broken. The Thing You're Marketing Is Unclear."

Full creative specs: [launch-creatives-brief.md](./images/launch/launch-creatives-brief.md)
Full ad copy: [before-the-funnel.md](./before-the-funnel.md)

### Pre-Launch Checklist

- [ ] CAPI (server-side tracking) set up — Stape.io ($10/mo) or dev setup
- [ ] Landing page live in GHL (two variants: hybrid VSL + static mockup, 50/50 split)
- [ ] 3 image creatives produced (ugly static, product reveal, before/after)
- [ ] 1 video creative produced (silent review)
- [ ] 1 ABO campaign → 1 ad set (Before the Funnel), broad targeting
- [ ] All ads: "Learn More" CTA, Advantage+ Creative OFF, site links OFF, creative enhancements OFF
- [ ] Ad naming: `Problem Aware - [Format] - Before the Funnel`
- [ ] Link description: "5 AI prompts extract your $5K offer in one afternoon. Self-guided system. $47."
- [ ] $30/day budget set
- [ ] Ads Manager columns: Frequency, Results, Cost/Result, ROAS, AOV, Amount Spent, CTR, CPC, LP Views
- [ ] **72-hour hands-off period — no changes until Day 4**

### After 72 Hours

- [ ] Review $20 rule — kill zero-signal images (no clicks at $20 spend)
- [ ] Check frequency — anything 3.0+ is fatigued
- [ ] Replace killed creatives with new variants (different ugly static or before/after)
- [ ] Monitor CPA — target under $100

### When to Move to Stage 2

**10+ sales AND CPA confirmed under $100.** Revenue from Stage 1 funds Stage 2.

If CPA is above $100 after 10 sales, don't expand — troubleshoot first (see Troubleshooting section).

# STAGE 4: FULL SCALE

**Budget:** $200/day | **Add:** Misalignment + CBO | **New Ads:** 4

This is the first time CBO enters. You now have enough proven winners and enough budget for the algorithm to optimize.

| Allocation | Budget | What |
|-----------|--------|------|
| ABO Testing | $60/day (30%) | New Misalignment angle + creative refresh on existing angles |
| CBO Scaling | $140/day (70%) | Proven winners only |

### CBO Setup

1. Create one CBO campaign
2. One ad set inside it — Advantage Plus targeting, broad
3. Add 5-6 proven winners (10+ sales each) using **Post ID extraction** (keeps social proof)
4. Starting budget: $100-140/day
5. Scale by adding more winning creative — Cat Howell: *"Every time you raise the budget, you should be adding more ads into your ad set."*

### Stage 4 Checklist

- [ ] Produce 4 creatives for [Misalignment](./misalignment.md)
- [ ] Identify CBO candidates — any ad with 10+ sales
- [ ] Extract Post IDs for winners (keeps likes/comments/shares)
- [ ] Launch CBO campaign with 5-6 winners at $100/day+
- [ ] Keep ABO testing at $60/day for new creative
- [ ] Budget now $200/day total — fully funded by revenue
- [ ] Monitor CBO frequency — add fresh creative before fatigue hits

### Beyond Stage 4

**ASC (Advantage Shopping Campaigns):** Only after 100+ total sales and a matured pixel. This is the final scaling lever — let Meta's algorithm handle everything. Not before the data is there.

## Targeting

- Broad is default — creative IS targeting (post-Andromeda)
- Advantage Plus targeting — no interests, no lookalikes, no exclusions
- Target by country only: US, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand
- One ad set worldwide (broader, cheaper CPMs) vs one high-income only — compare
- No separate retargeting campaigns — the algorithm handles it within broad

---

## Kill Criteria

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| $20 spend, zero clicks | Kill that image creative |
| $20 spend, clicks but no add-to-cart | Kill that copy block |
| CPA > $150 after 72 hrs | Kill the ad set |
| CPA $80-120 | Let it run, monitor |
| CPA < $80 | Scale 20% every 48 hrs |
| Frequency 2.0 | Warning — monitor closely |
| Frequency 3.0+ | Fatigued — do NOT scale, roll out new creatives |

**On frequency:** *"If you try to scale a campaign with fatigued ads, you're gonna blow that up."* — Cat Howell. Check frequency BEFORE scaling any winner.

---

## Ad Setup Rules

Every ad, every stage:

- **CTA button:** "Learn More" (not "Shop Now")
- **Advantage+ Creative:** OFF
- **Creative enhancements:** OFF (except maybe music)
- **Site links:** OFF
- **Conversion event:** Purchase (not Add to Cart, not Lead)
- **Placements:** Advantage+ Placements (NOT manual "Feed Only")
- **Ad naming:** `[Awareness Level] - [Format] - [Angle]`

## Target Metrics

| Metric | Target | Note |
|--------|--------|------|
| Front-end AOV | $90-110 | $100+ before scaling |
| Bump take rate | 50%+ (any bump) | Below = fix bumps, not ads |
| CPA (break even) | < $100 | Normal range at 2% CVR, $2 CPC |
| CPA (profitable) | < $80 | — |
| Checkout CVR | 30% | — |
| Landing page CVR | 2-5% cold | Below 2% = messaging problem |
| CPC | Under $7 | Higher OK for niche B2B |
| CTR | Over 1% | Below = hook problem |
| ROAS | 2.0+ | Range: 2.0-2.35 |

### Validation Thresholds

| Sales | Status |
|-------|--------|
| 5-10 | "Onto something" — NOT validated |
| 30 | Minimum to trust data |
| 100 | Truly validated — numbers stabilize |

**Don't scale until AOV is $100+ consistently.** If bump take rates are low, fix bumps before increasing ad spend.

---

## Troubleshooting (Cat Howell's 8-Point)

If ads aren't converting, check in this order:

1. **Enough spend at ad level?** At 2% CVR, $2 CPC → need $100 per expected sale
2. **Enough landing page views?** At 2% CVR → need 50 views per expected sale
3. **CPC acceptable?** Target under $7
4. **CTR acceptable?** Over 1% — below = hook isn't stopping the scroll
5. **Ad "keeps the scent"?** Landing page matches what the ad promised
6. **Messaging & positioning?** Clicks but no sales = sales page losing the job
7. **Pricing?** $17-$67 sweet spot cold. $47 is confirmed.
8. **Do you actually care?** *"If it's not a song of your soul... you will give up."*
