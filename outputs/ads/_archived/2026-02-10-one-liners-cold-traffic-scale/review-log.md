## P1 Fixes Applied

### #9 — Founder results as replicable path
- **Before:** "Paid traffic with a validated offer made 114 sales in 30 days. Organic content for 12 months made zero. Pick your path."
- **After:** "I spent 12 months posting content with zero sales. Then I validated my offer and ran paid traffic. Different approach, different result."
- **Why:** Removed specific sales numbers and "pick your path" framing that implied reader could replicate founder's results. Now explicitly first-person founder story.

### #20 — 72-hour customer claim
- **Before:** "One afternoon. One validated offer. First paid customer within 72 hours. That's the speed when the offer is right."
- **After:** "One afternoon. One clear offer. Then you know what to sell before you build anything else. That changes everything."
- **Why:** Removed unsubstantiated 72-hour timeline claim. No typicality data supports this. Reframed as clarity outcome.

### #29 — Founder results with implied ROI
- **Before:** "Switched from posting daily to running $50/day in ads with a validated $47 offer. First 114 sales took 30 days. Not 12 months."
- **After:** "I stopped posting daily and ran ads to a validated offer instead. Having a clear offer changed everything. Yours starts with getting the offer right."
- **Why:** Removed specific sales count, ad spend, and timeframe that together created a calculable ROI claim.

### #4 — Social proof overstatement
- **Before:** "150+ coaches have already fixed it."
- **After:** "150+ coaches are inside building clearer offers right now."
- **Why:** "Fixed it" implied 150+ achieved a specific outcome. Proof only supports 150+ enrolled. Reframed as active community.

### #12 — $5K income-adjacent claim
- **Before:** "5 AI prompts turn what you know into a $5K offer."
- **After:** "5 AI prompts turn what you know into a clear, packaged coaching offer."
- **Why:** $5K figure implied income outcome with no client typicality data. Reframed as clarity/packaging.

### #14 — Product mismatch (bump vs core offer)
- **Before:** "If you're a coach who hates DMs and sales calls, 5 copy-paste scripts start real conversations without the cringe."
- **After:** "Coaches who hate selling but need clients — this builds an offer clear enough that people come to you. No cold DMs required."
- **Why:** DM scripts are the $17 order bump, not the $47 core offer. Rewritten to reference the core offer's benefit.

### #17 — Unsubstantiated $5K client claim
- **Before:** "Coaches with half your experience are landing $5K clients."
- **After:** "Coaches with half the experience are booking clients."
- **Why:** No data supports coaches landing $5K clients through this product. Removed specific dollar figure.
