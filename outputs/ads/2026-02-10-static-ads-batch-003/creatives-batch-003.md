## Testing Strategy

- **Round 1:** Run clean visuals (a versions) with all primaries. These stop scroll through imagery.
- **Round 2:** Test text overlays (b versions) against winners. Text overlays often outperform on DR and Testimonial ads.
- **Short-forms** share the same image as their parent primary.
- **Headlines** are separate text — they sit above the image in Meta Ads Manager, not on the image.

## Ideogram Settings

When generating in Ideogram:
- **Aspect ratio:** 1:1
- **Style:** Realistic (for photos) or Design (for text overlays)
- **Quality:** High
- **Negative prompt (add if available):** text, watermark, logo, blurry, low quality
