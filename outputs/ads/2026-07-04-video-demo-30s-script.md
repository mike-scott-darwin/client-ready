# AD1 — 30s Training Demo Script

**Strategy:** value-first training demo, NOT a pitch. The AI output stays hidden on purpose — the open loop ("what would it pull out of *me*?") is the click driver. Do not show the finished result.

**Persistent on screen:** top banner "What If You Could Identify The ONE Problem You're Uniquely Positioned to Solve" · bottom "$27 — link below 👇" · face-cam + burned-in captions (his exact words).

## Main script (~80 words, paces at 30s)

| Time | Spoken | On screen |
|---|---|---|
| 0–3s | "There's one problem only *you* can solve. Most coaches never find it." | Face cam, full frame |
| 3–7s | "Watch me pull mine out of AI — live. No theory." | Cut to screen-share |
| 7–13s | "I paste the first prompt into ChatGPT. Works in Claude, works in GPT — doesn't matter." | Paste prompt, zoom readable |
| 13–21s | "It starts interviewing me. My background. The stuff people already pay me for. Building my zone of genius, question by question." | AI asking questions, scrolling |
| 21–26s | "One afternoon, this becomes an offer so clear it sells without a call." | Face cam |
| 26–30s | "Curious what it'd pull out of *you*? Link below. Twenty-seven bucks." | CTA emphasis |

## Hook variants (A/B the first 3s — biggest lever)

The 0–3s opener does 80% of the work. Swap only these lines; keep the rest of the script identical.

- **V1 (control):** "There's one problem only *you* can solve. Most coaches never find it."
- **V2 (curiosity/proof):** "I asked AI to find the one thing only I can sell. Watch what it pulled out."
- **V3 (reframe/pain):** "You're not bad at marketing. Your offer's just invisible. Here's how I fixed mine."
- **V4 (specificity):** "One prompt. One afternoon. The offer you're supposed to be selling — watch me build mine."

## Rules (keep it working)
- **Open loop stays shut on the payoff.** Never show the finished zone-of-genius output.
- **Close on "…for you"** — the last beat must make them want it run on themselves.
- **Zoom the screen** enough to prove it's real (credibility), not to let them read every word.
- **No income claims.** Transformation + mechanism only.
- Captions = his exact words, burned in (sound-off viewers).
