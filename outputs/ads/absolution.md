# Absolution — Ad Copy

Core thesis: You're not the problem. You've been trying to grow into an offer that was never aligned to begin with.
Brand arc: Catches the coach stuck at **Confused** who has internalized the struggle as personal failure. Moves them toward **Clear** by relocating the blame from *them* to *the offer*.
Upstream wedge: Miles resolves absolution to "rebuild your offer so it closes in writing." Client Ready resolves it upstream — *extract the offer that's actually yours, then validate it this afternoon.* The relief is the hook; extraction + validation is the payoff.

---

## Awareness Level

**Problem aware, shame-loaded.** They feel the grind and blame themselves ("everyone else figured this out"). They have NOT reframed it as an offer-alignment problem. Lead with the weight coming off — not a pitch.
