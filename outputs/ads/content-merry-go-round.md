### Angle-Specific Notes

- This angle targets coaches who think the problem is their content strategy. The shift is: the problem is the offer underneath.
- **Do NOT attack content creation itself.** The message is "content works when the offer is clear" — not "content is useless." We're giving them permission to step off the treadmill, not insulting their effort.
- The alternative path is: fix the offer → then content works OR skip content and run paid traffic. Both are valid.
- Robin and Wendy are the direct testimonial matches. Other clients had the precursor problem (offer wasn't clear enough to even start content).
