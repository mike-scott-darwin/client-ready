## Post 1: Core Message (Direct)

**Hook:** Everyone teaches funnels. Nobody teaches the offer.

**Body:**
I've watched coaches spend thousands on:
- Funnel builders
- Ad courses
- Email sequences
- Landing page templates

Then wonder why nothing converts.

The problem was never the funnel.

The problem was they never knew what they were selling.

Here's the truth most "gurus" won't tell you:

A clear offer with an ugly funnel will outsell a confused offer with a beautiful funnel. Every time.

Before you buy another course, ask yourself:

Can I explain what I sell in one sentence?

If you hesitated, that's your real problem.

## Post 3: The One Sentence Test

**Hook:** If you can't explain what you do in one sentence, you don't have a marketing problem.

**Body:**
You have an offer problem.

I used to ramble when people asked what I do.

"Well, I help coaches with their... you know, like business stuff, and mindset, and also marketing, and..."

Nobody bought.

Because confusion doesn't convert.

Here's the test:

Can you fill in this blank?

"I help [specific person] achieve [specific result] through [your method]."

If you can't do that in 10 seconds, stop building funnels.

Fix the offer first.

## Post 4: The Prerequisite (Positioning)

**Hook:** Stop buying funnel templates for an offer you haven't validated.

**Body:**
The coaching industry has it backwards.

They teach:
1. Build funnel
2. Run ads
3. Hope it converts
4. Iterate when it doesn't

Here's what actually works:

1. Get clear on your offer
2. Test it with real people (not ads)
3. See if anyone reaches for their wallet
4. THEN build infrastructure

I spent 6 months building a course nobody bought.

The funnel was fine. The offer was confused.

Now I validate in an afternoon. Before building anything.

**CTA:** Comment VALIDATE if you want the process.

## Post 5: The Real Problem (Contrarian)

**Hook:** You don't need another funnel course.

**Body:**
You need to know what you're selling.

I know coaches with:
- 47 saved courses
- 12 half-finished certifications
- Beautiful Canva templates
- Zero paying clients

The information isn't the problem.

The clarity is the problem.

They can't answer:
- What specific problem do I solve?
- Who specifically has this problem?
- Why should they pay ME?

Until you can answer those, every course is a distraction.

Clarity first. Courses later.

**CTA:** What's the ONE question you can't answer about your offer? Comment below.
