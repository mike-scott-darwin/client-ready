# Ad Test Variations — Clarity First vs Validation

## Test Setup

| Element | Setting |
|---------|---------|
| Budget | $150 per ad (total $300) |
| Duration | 5-7 days |
| Campaign type | Advantage+ Sales |
| Audience | Broad (US) |
| Objective | Conversions (landing page) |

**What we're measuring:**
- CTR (which hook stops scroll)
- Landing page conversion (which positioning converts)
- CPA (cost per acquisition)

---

## Ad A: Validation Angle (Current Positioning)

**Primary text:**
Most coaches build first, validate second.

That's backwards.

I watched a coach spend 6 months building a course. Launch day: zero sales.

Not because the course was bad. Because she never tested if anyone would pay.

Here's what I do now:

1. Write the offer on one page
2. Show it to 10 people
3. See if anyone reaches for their wallet
4. THEN build

I made a system that does this in one afternoon. 5 prompts. One template. Done.

If you're tired of building things that don't sell, this is for you.

**Headline:** Validate Your Offer Before You Build

**Description:** 5 AI prompts that extract your $5K+ offer in one afternoon.

**CTA:** Get Started

---

## Ad B: Clarity Angle (New Positioning)

**Primary text:**
If you can't explain what you sell in one sentence, stop.

You don't have a marketing problem.
You don't have a funnel problem.
You don't have a traffic problem.

You have an offer problem.

I've watched coaches spend thousands on:
- Funnel builders
- Ad courses
- Email templates

Then wonder why nothing converts.

The problem? They never knew what they were selling.

I built a system that fixes this. 5 prompts. One afternoon. Total clarity.

Before you buy another course, fix this first.

**Headline:** Everyone Teaches Funnels. Nobody Teaches the Offer.

**Description:** Can't explain what you do in one sentence? Start here.

**CTA:** Fix Your Offer
