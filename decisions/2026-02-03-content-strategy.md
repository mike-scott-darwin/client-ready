# Content Strategy Decision

## Situation

Client Ready has solid reference files (soul, offer, audience, voice) and a daily email rhythm system designed. However:
- No defined content strategy
- 100% dependent on paid ads for traffic
- No organic flywheel building trust before purchase
- Platforms and cadence marked as "TBD" in reference files

Research completed on LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and newsletter-first models for coaches in 2026.

---

## Research Summary

### Key Findings

1. **Newsletter-first is the dominant model** — Email drives 90%+ of coach revenue; social is discovery
2. **X/Twitter favors anti-guru positioning** — Text outperforms video, conversation depth weighted 150x more than likes
3. **LinkedIn rewards expertise over virality** — Algorithm shift punishes clickbait, rewards genuine value
4. **DMs convert better than funnels** — 15-25% response rate vs 2% cold email
5. **Authentic voice is the 2026 differentiator** — AI spam flooding inboxes makes real humans stand out

### Top Performers to Study

| Platform | Who | Why |
|----------|-----|-----|
| X | Alex Hormozi, Dan Koe, Codie Sanchez | Anti-guru, newsletter-first, contrarian takes |
| LinkedIn | Justin Welsh, Lara Acosta, Chris Do | No-nonsense, tactical value, DM conversion |
| Newsletter | Justin Welsh ($2.3M), Ryan Holiday (320K daily) | Write once, repurpose everywhere |

---

## Options

### Option A: X + LinkedIn + Newsletter (Recommended)

**Primary:** X/Twitter (3-5 tweets/day, 1-2 threads/week)
**Secondary:** LinkedIn (3-5 posts/week, strategic commenting)
**Engine:** Newsletter-first (weekly, repurpose to social)

**Pros:**
- X algorithm favors Michael's anti-guru voice
- LinkedIn reaches B2B coaches (target audience)
- Newsletter-first matches existing daily email rhythm
- Repurposing keeps workload sustainable
- Both platforms reward text over video (plays to strengths)

**Cons:**
- Two platforms to maintain
- Requires consistent output

### Option B: X Only + Newsletter

**Primary:** X/Twitter (5+ tweets/day, 2-3 threads/week)
**Engine:** Newsletter-first

**Pros:**
- Simpler, one platform focus
- Faster iteration and feedback

**Cons:**
- Misses LinkedIn's B2B coach audience
- Less diversification

### Option C: LinkedIn Only + Newsletter

**Primary:** LinkedIn (5x/week, heavy commenting)
**Engine:** Newsletter-first

**Pros:**
- B2B coaches concentrated here
- DM conversion path clear

**Cons:**
- Slower growth than X
- Less suited to contrarian/edgy content

---

## Decision

**Option A: X + LinkedIn + Newsletter**

### Rationale

1. **Michael's voice fits X perfectly** — Anti-guru, direct, "Wrong." energy plays well
2. **Target audience is on LinkedIn** — Coaches considering $5K investment research on LinkedIn
3. **Newsletter-first already designed** — Daily email rhythm system exists in offer.md
4. **Repurposing makes it sustainable** — One piece of thinking becomes multiple posts
5. **Both platforms reward text** — No video production required to start

### The System

```
Weekly Newsletter (keystone)
    ↓
Deconstruct → 2 X threads + 3-5 LinkedIn posts
    ↓
Daily → 3-5 tweets (observations, one-liners, replies)
    ↓
High-intent signals → DM conversations
    ↓
$27 → Sprint → Accelerator
```
