# OTO 2: Reframe from "Done-For-You" to "Client Ready Blueprint"

## Situation

OTO 2 was positioned as a "Done-For-You Client Ready Funnel" — implying Michael builds a live, working funnel for the buyer. In reality, the deliverable is:

- A 100+ page custom strategy document (copy, messaging, positioning, emails, ads)
- A GHL snapshot (starter funnel template)
- A 60-minute integration call

The buyer takes the Blueprint and implements it themselves using the GHL snapshot. Michael does NOT build bespoke pages or set up their GHL account.

The sales page was over-promising ("I'll launch your entire system for you," "this is me doing the work FOR you") when the actual product is strategy + copy, not a live funnel.

## Decision

Rename to **"Client Ready Blueprint"** and reframe all copy to honestly position what's delivered:

- **Sprint ($297)** = templates + 4 weeks live coaching, YOU build
- **Blueprint ($397)** = custom strategy doc + copy + GHL snapshot + 1-on-1 call, YOU implement from it
- **Accelerator ($5K)** = bespoke pages + complete GHL setup, the actual full build (sold via email after onboarding, not as an OTO)

## Why

1. **Honesty** — Don't promise a built funnel when you're delivering a strategy document
2. **Clear value ladder** — Each tier has a distinct deliverable, not overlapping promises
3. **Accelerator bridge** — Blueprint buyers who want the full build have a clear upgrade path
4. **Sustainable** — Michael doesn't get sucked into building landing pages at $397

## What Changed

- `outputs/pages/oto2-dfy-full-page-copy.md` — Complete reframe: hero, opening copy, price intro, Who It's For, guarantee, scarcity, timeline, final close, CTA buttons, decline link, developer notes. Added GHL snapshot as deliverable. Added Accelerator bridge copy.
- `reference/core/offer.md` — Tier 3 renamed from "Done-For-You Client Ready Funnel" to "Client Ready Blueprint." Format, timeline, guarantee, promise all updated. All DFY references replaced (funnel metrics, email sequence, guarantee, OTO benchmarks).
- `outputs/products/oto2-done-for-you-funnel.md` — Title, description, timeline, guarantee, Who It's For, FAQ, Getting Started, AI generation prompt all reframed. Added GHL snapshot as deliverable.

## Price

$397 stays. Fair for custom strategy document + copy + GHL snapshot + 1-on-1 call at ~$80-100/hour effective rate.
