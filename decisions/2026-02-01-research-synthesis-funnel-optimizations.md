# Research Synthesis: Funnel Optimization Decisions

## Situation

Analyzed 8 research files from today's session covering:
- Miles Stutz ad strategy and copy techniques
- Order bump swipes and patterns
- Real case studies with hard numbers
- CPA/AOV frameworks
- Split test data from Cat Howell

Need to determine: What's validated, what should change, what to test next.

---

## Key Findings Synthesized

### 1. Order Bumps Are THE Lever

**Evidence:**
- Case study: AOV $14 → $23 (64% lift) from adding bumps
- Cat Howell split test: 3 bumps DOUBLED conversion rate (5.17% → 10.02%)
- Cat Howell: Earnings per page view $3.39 → $6.10 with more bumps
- Miles call: "That was the point when I was able to break even on ads with just bumps, no OTOs"

**Implication:** Order bumps don't just increase AOV — they increase conversion rate. More bumps = more revenue from same traffic.

### 2. Your Current Structure Is Validated

**Your structure:**
- Front-end: $27
- Bump 1: $37
- Bump 2: $67
- Target AOV: $100-120

**Benchmark comparisons:**
- $100 CPA is normal at scale (Cat Howell, Omon doing $80-100k/mo)
- With $100-120 AOV, you break even or profit at $100 CPA
- Your bump structure ($37 + $67 = $104 potential) is well-designed

**Verdict:** ✅ Structure is sound. Don't change pricing.

### 3. Copy Patterns That Convert

**From order bump swipes:**
- Specific numbers ("30 templates", "25+ headlines")
- Price anchoring ("$197 value" → $37)
- "Only on this page" exclusivity
- Fill-in-the-blank framing
- ONE-TIME OFFER framing

**From Miles:**
- "While others..." contrast structure
- "It's your turn" repetition
- Anti-content angle ("backup dancer for algorithm")

**Status:** ✅ Already applied to bump copy today.

### 4. Ad Strategy Framework

**The rules (from Miles + Cat Howell):**
- Launch 4 ad sets: 3 cold + 1 retargeting
- Retargeting at half budget — almost always wins
- Start at $50/day (not $100 — too volatile)
- 1 short + 1 long form copy per image
- 5-10 creatives per launch

**CPA decision framework:**
| CPA vs AOV | Action |
|------------|--------|
| > 1.5-2x AOV | Kill |
| ≈ AOV | Let it run |
| < AOV | Scale 20% every 48 hours |

**For your funnel ($100-120 AOV):**
- Kill threshold: > $150-200
- Run threshold: $80-120
- Scale threshold: < $80

### 5. What to Test When Conversion Is Low

**From Miles feedback:**
1. Test headline (biggest impact)
2. Add context: who it's for, payoff, PROOF
3. Consider unchecking pre-selected bumps
4. Add more proof to checkout page

**From Cat Howell:**
- Video vs no video: no video won (test assumptions!)
- Long form vs short form: testing in Feb

### 6. The $300 Rule

**From Miles call:**
> "If you spend $300 without a sale, change the snack. Don't optimize — change the offer."

**Testing priority:**
1. Offer (biggest impact)
2. Headline
3. Price
4. Design
