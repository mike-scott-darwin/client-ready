# Ad Strategy Update from Miles Stutz Hot Seat

## Situation

Banked two research files from Miles Stutz (Feb 10 hot seat call + member funnel data). Cross-referencing against our offer.md revealed 6 gaps between our planned ad strategy and what Miles is actually doing at scale (1,200/day on his snack, 250/day on retargeting profitably).

## Decisions

### 1. Add Mid-Funnel Branding Campaigns

**Decision:** Add engagement/video views campaigns retargeting buyer audiences.

**What it is:** Meta engagement campaigns optimized for through-play video views. 5-10 head-talking videos (90 seconds to 3 minutes), taken from Instagram. Retarget all buyer audiences. Budget 5/day per ad to start.

**Why:** Miles says this is the single best thing he has done for mid-funnel. His retargeting audience of 40,000 people gets 250/day in branding ads at frequency 6 — and it sells profitably. Creates familiarity that makes ascension to Sprint, Blueprint, and Accelerator feel obvious. Replaces the "post and pray" organic strategy.

**Implementation:** Launch after first 50-100 front-end sales (need audience to retarget). Start with 5 videos at 5/day each (25/day total). Scale as audience grows.

### 2. Launch Without OTOs — Front-End First

**Decision:** Launch with short-form sales page + 3 order bumps + thank you page. No OTOs until front-end AOV is proven at 100+.

**Why:** Miles was explicit on the call: "Short form page, three order bumps, straight to thank you page. Test it first before you commit to OTOs." He told this to multiple members. OTOs add complexity and distract from proving the core conversion. Our OTO pages (Sprint, Blueprint, Community) are designed and specced — they wait in reserve until the front-end is validated.

**Trigger to add OTOs:** 30+ sales AND AOV consistently 100+ from bumps alone.

**What this changes:** GHL funnel setup is simpler at launch. No OTO pages needed initially. Email sequences still reference Sprint/Blueprint/Accelerator — that ascension path remains via email, just not via immediate post-purchase OTO pages.

### 3. Ad Testing Budget to 50 Per Ad Set

**Decision:** Increase ABO testing budget from 15/ad set to 50/ad set.

**Why:** At 15/ad set, feedback loops are too slow. Miles runs testing at 50/ad set and launches 3 new ad sets per week. At 50/day per test set, you get meaningful data in 3-5 days. At 15/day, it takes 10-15 days for the same data — by which point the creative is stale.

**Budget impact:** Testing budget goes from roughly 45/day (3 ad sets at 15) to 150/day (3 ad sets at 50). Requires bigger war chest upfront but dramatically faster learning.

### 4. Creative Mix Formula — B-Roll Plus Static

**Decision:** Standardize ad testing creative mix: 2-3 B-roll videos with overlay text + 2-3 static images. Each creative gets one short-form copy and one long-form copy. Total: 8-10 ads per test ad set.

**Why:** This is Miles' exact formula at scale. B-roll with overlay text is his best-performing format currently. Static images provide comparison data. Short vs long copy tests messaging density.

**What this changes:** Ad creation process now has a specific recipe instead of "5-10 creatives per launch."

### 5. Checkout CVR Benchmark: 30 Percent

**Decision:** Add checkout-specific conversion rate (30%) as a distinct metric, separate from overall page-to-sale conversion.

**Why:** Giovanni's funnel data showed 17.9% checkout CVR. Miles immediately said "that should be 30%." This is a different metric than page-to-sale (which includes people who never reach checkout). Tracking both tells you WHERE the drop-off happens — is it the sales page (not reaching checkout) or the checkout page itself (reaching but not completing)?

**Metrics update:**
- Sales page to checkout rate (new): track separately
- Checkout CVR target: 30%
- Overall page-to-sale: 10%+ (existing)

### 6. Five-Second Test Pre-Launch Checklist

**Decision:** Add the 5-second test as a required pre-launch gate for any sales page or funnel page.

**Five questions (must be answerable instantly when looking at the page):**
- Who is this for?
- Why am I here? (the payoff)
- Why should I care? (unique differentiator / mechanism)
- Why should I believe? (trust signals, proof)
- What do I do next? (clear CTA)

**Implementation:** Add to offer.md under checkout optimization or as a standalone section. Run this test on every page before spending money on traffic.

## What Changes

**offer.md — Ad Strategy Framework section:**
- Add mid-funnel branding campaigns subsection
- Update launch structure to reflect "no OTOs until proven" approach
- Change ABO tester budget from 15 to 50 per ad set
- Add creative mix formula (B-roll + static + short/long copy)
- Add checkout CVR 30% benchmark to target metrics
- Add 5-second test to checkout optimization section

**No other reference files affected.** All changes are within the Ad Strategy Framework and Checkout Optimization sections of offer.md.
