# Bump Conversion Improvements

## Situation

Bumps are priced at 37/67/97. Product content is strong but sales copy significantly undersells what buyers actually get. Checkout copy and full sales pages contain outdated counts and miss major product sections entirely. This is leaving conversion rate on the table.

## Audit Findings

### Bump 1: Quick Win DM Scripts (37)

**Product actually contains:**
- 10 copy-paste scripts (Core 5 + Quick Cash 5)
- 6-step indirect conversation flow
- A-C-A framework (Acknowledge-Compliment-Ask)
- Qualifying questions progression (7 levels)
- Connection builder tactics
- Complete objection handlers (4 scenarios)
- Inbound inquiry script
- Tracking spreadsheet template

**Sales copy currently promises:**
- "5 copy-paste DM scripts" (undersells by half)
- No mention of conversation system, A-C-A framework, qualifying progression, or objection handlers

**Gap:** Sales copy advertises 50% of the actual product. The conversation system and objection handlers are arguably more valuable than the scripts themselves.

### Bump 2: Plug and Play Templates (67)

**Product actually contains:**
- Offer document template (with filled Client Ready example)
- 3 landing page swipe files (Short-Form, Long-Form, VSL Page)
- 30-day email sequence (pre-written, week by week)
- Messaging maps for consistency
- Client profile templates
- Awareness level messaging map (4 levels)
- 50+ copy-paste headlines and hooks
- 8 promo and quick cash templates (flash sales, price increases, founding member, bonuses, referrals, scholarships, deposit/payment plans, cash injection campaigns)

**Sales copy currently promises:**
- "25+ copy-paste headlines" (actually 50+)
- Lists 5 items on the sales page (actually 8 major sections)
- No mention of promo templates, quick cash templates, awareness level messaging
- No mention of the payment plan or scholarship templates

**Gap:** The most underrepresented bump. The promo templates alone (flash sale, referral drive, scholarship, founding member, cash injection) are worth the 67 by themselves. Sales copy only shows about 60% of what is inside.

### Bump 3: First 5K Client Playbook (97)

**Product actually contains:**
- Pricing psychology (why 5K is easier than 500)
- 3-question Pricing Confidence Framework
- 5 conversation frameworks (Discovery, Offer Walkthrough, DM Close, Building Something, Referral Ask)
- Warm 50 activation plan (5 categories, 4-week sequence)
- Complete objection playbook (5 scenarios with word-for-word responses)
- 3 annotated real closing conversations

**Sales copy currently promises:**
- Accurately represents all 5 major sections
- Minor: says "word-for-word objection responses" but doesn't specify 5 scenarios

**Gap:** Smallest gap. Sales copy is reasonably accurate. Minor improvements possible.

## Decision

### Fix 1: Update Bump 1 Copy (High Impact)

**Checkout copy changes:**
- Change "5 copy-paste DM scripts" to "10 copy-paste DM scripts + complete DM conversation system"
- Add mention of objection handlers
- Emphasize the complete sales conversation system, not just individual scripts

**Sales page changes:**
- Update "What's Inside" to reflect all 10 scripts
- Add sections for the conversation system, A-C-A framework, objection handlers
- Update the "What Just Happened" section with more specific claims

### Fix 2: Update Bump 2 Copy (Highest Impact)

**Checkout copy changes:**
- Change "25+ copy-paste headlines" to "50+ copy-paste headlines"
- Add promo and quick cash templates to the feature list
- Emphasize 8 complete sections, not 5

**Sales page changes:**
- Expand "What's Inside" from 5 items to 8
- Add sections for awareness level messaging, promo templates, quick cash campaigns
- Update "What Just Happened" to reference quick cash and promo capabilities
- Update the comparison table with more before/after contrasts

### Fix 3: Update Bump 3 Copy (Minor)

**Checkout copy changes:**
- Add "5 word-for-word objection responses" to make it more specific
- Add the Pricing Confidence Framework as a named deliverable

**Sales page changes:**
- Minor specificity improvements
- Add the 3-question Pricing Confidence Framework as a named tool

### Fix 4: Structural Copy Improvements (All Bumps)

**Checkout copy:**
- Add scarcity/urgency: "only available on this page" already present, ensure consistent
- Lead with quantity: specific numbers (10 scripts, 8 template packs, 50+ headlines)
- Add time-save claims where honest (e.g., "build your funnel in an afternoon instead of a month")

**Sales pages:**
- Add "TOTAL VALUE" stack at the bottom before the price (itemize components with individual values)
- Strengthen the "Who This Is For" sections with more specific qualifying criteria
- Add a "What You Get — Quick Reference" summary box near the top

### Fix 5: Product Description Updates (offer.md)

Update the offer.md bump sections to accurately reflect the full product scope (10 scripts not 5, 50+ headlines not 25+, etc.).

## What Changes

Reference files affected:
- reference/core/offer.md -- update bump descriptions to match actual product content

Output files affected:
- outputs/pages/bump-1-quick-win-dm-scripts.md -- updated checkout and sales page copy
- outputs/pages/bump-2-plug-and-play-templates.md -- updated checkout and sales page copy
- outputs/pages/bump-3-first-5k-client-playbook.md -- minor copy improvements

## What Does NOT Change

- Product files (outputs/products/) -- content stays the same, these are already strong
- Bump pricing (37/67/97) -- recently updated, no change needed
- Bump order (1/2/3) -- current order is correct (lowest friction first)
- Funnel stages or touchpoints -- copy changes only, no structural changes
