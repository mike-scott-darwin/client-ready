# Decision: Gemini Research Action Items

## What Changed

Reviewed all three Gemini deep research reports (offer validation, content pipeline metrics, creator positioning). Seven flags identified. Decisions below.

## 1. Run a $100 Validation Test in 7 Days

**Flag:** The "$100 Rule" — if an offer can't generate $100 from real buyers in 7 days, it needs pivoting. Long validation periods (30+ days) decrease success rates because perfectionism creeps in.

**Decision:** Launch a paid traffic test to the $47 front-end within the next 7 days. $50-100/day budget. The goal is $100 in revenue from strangers, not perfecting the funnel. If it doesn't hit $100 in 7 days, pivot messaging — not the offer.

**Why this matters:** We've been building. The research says building without buyer signal is the #1 failure mode for solo coaches in 2026.

## 2. Rebalance Anti-Guru Positioning

**Flag:** "Many 'anti-guru' positions have become formulaic and less differentiating." Pure systems focus can appear too clinical and limit emotional connection.

**Decision:** Keep the systems-first identity but inject more personal story into content. The engineering background, the 9-to-5 reality, the specific moments of figuring things out — these strengthen the system credibility, not weaken it. Don't strip personality from sales pages.

**What stays:** "Test, validate, build." Direct voice. No-BS.
**What changes:** More first-person stories in ads and emails. Less "here's the framework" — more "here's what happened when I used the framework."

## 3. Weight LinkedIn Over X

**Flag:** LinkedIn engagement averages 5.2% (carousels hit 6.6%). X has declined to 0.5-1% for medium accounts. Diminishing returns hit after 3-5 LinkedIn posts/week.

**Decision:** LinkedIn becomes primary organic platform. 3x/week, carousel-heavy. X stays active but deprioritized — use it for threads and voice, not as a growth channel. Don't exceed 3-5 posts/week on LinkedIn; additional frequency doesn't help.

**Update needed:** `reference/domain/content-strategy.md` — adjust platform weighting and posting cadence.

## 4. Don't Add a Free Lead Magnet for Validation

**Flag:** Free lead magnets show negative correlation with actual market success as validation tools. SLOs have 15-25% higher validation accuracy.

**Decision:** Stay SLO-first. The $47 front-end IS the validation mechanism. Don't build a free PDF or webinar to "warm up" before testing — it delays real signal. If we add a lead magnet later, it's for nurture, not validation.

## 5. Mobile-First Checkout

**Flag:** 83% of initial offer engagement comes from mobile devices.

**Decision:** Audit the checkout page and landing page on mobile before running paid traffic. If it's not clean on a phone, fix it first. This is a blocker for the $100 test.

**Checklist:**
- [ ] Landing page loads in <3 seconds on mobile
- [ ] CTA button visible without scrolling on mobile
- [ ] Checkout form works without horizontal scrolling
- [ ] Bump offers readable on mobile

## 6. Welcome Sequence Is Priority #1 Email

**Flag:** Welcome emails hit 82% open rates — far above any other touchpoint. Welcome sequences convert better than limited-time offers for coaching products.

**Decision:** The welcome sequence gets built and optimized before any other email workflow. It's the highest-leverage touchpoint in the entire funnel. Structure: value-first, then soft pitch — not urgency/scarcity.

**Update needed:** `reference/domain/funnel/email-rhythm.md` — elevate welcome sequence priority.

## 7. Strategic Opacity on Sales Pages

**Flag:** Over-documentation can overwhelm prospects. Some successful coaches maintain strategic opacity about certain processes. Complete transparency isn't always optimal for positioning.

**Decision:** On sales pages, show the transformation and the system name — not every step of the methodology. Save the full process breakdown for inside the product. The engineering-minded audience wants to know there IS a system, but they don't need to evaluate every step before buying.

**Where to apply:** Landing page copy, ad copy, OTO pages. Keep full methodology docs for portal content and community.

## Implementation Priority

1. **This week:** Mobile audit + $100 validation test (items 1, 5)
2. **This week:** Welcome sequence build (item 6)
3. **Next week:** LinkedIn content shift (item 3)
4. **Ongoing:** Voice rebalance in new content (items 2, 7)
5. **Hold:** No free lead magnet (item 4) — revisit after 50+ sales
