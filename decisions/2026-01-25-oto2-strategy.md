# OTO2 Strategy Decision

## Context

OTO2 is currently a $397 Done-For-You Funnel offer with 100 Customer Guarantee. Research suggests potential refinements to pricing, positioning, and bridging the gap to high-ticket backend.

**Current OTO2:**
| Element | Current |
|---------|---------|
| Price | $397 one-time |
| Offer | Done-For-You Client Ready Funnel |
| Scarcity | 10 spots/month |
| Guarantee | 100 Customers or keep working |
| Timeline | 30-day implementation |
| Target conversion | 5-10% |

**Current Funnel Gap:**
```
$297 (Sprint) → $397 (DFY) → $5,000+ (1:1)
```

The jump from $397 to $5K+ is significant. Research shows successful funnels use a "slack adjuster" tier ($997-$1,000) to bridge this gap.

---

## Research Summary

### Key Finding 1: Slack Adjuster Model
From SLO research: Real case study showed $27 → $97 bumps → **$1,000 slack adjuster** → $12,000 backend. The mid-tier recovers ad spend and qualifies buyers for high-ticket.

### Key Finding 2: Result-Focused Positioning
Hernan Vazquez (2025): "Position for results, not education. People don't want more courses. They want faster, better, easier results."

Current OTO2 says: "Done-For-You Funnel" (feature)
Could say: "100 Customers in 30 Days" (result)

### Key Finding 3: Competitor Pricing
| Competitor | Similar Offer | Price |
|------------|---------------|-------|
| Hernan Vazquez | $197 consultation | $197 |
| Cat Howell | $500 visioning workshop | $500 |
| Your OTO2 | DFY Funnel | $397 |

Your $397 is in range but could be higher with result-focused repositioning.

### Key Finding 4: AOV Impact
Hernan increased upsell pricing from $47 → $97/$197 and saw **160% AOV increase**. Higher pricing with strong positioning converts.

---

## Options

### Option A: Keep As-Is ($397)

No changes. Current offer is well-structured.

**Pros:**
- Already has 100 Customer Guarantee
- 30-day timeline is clear
- $397 is accessible

**Cons:**
- Big gap to $5K+ backend
- Feature-focused positioning
- May leave money on table

---

### Option B: Raise to $497 + Result Reframe

- Raise price from $397 → $497
- Reposition from "Done-For-You Funnel" to "100 Customers in 30 Days"
- Keep 10/month scarcity

**Pros:**
- Higher AOV
- Result-focused positioning
- Still below $1K decision threshold

**Cons:**
- May reduce conversion rate
- Requires sales page update

---

### Option C: Add $997 OTO3 Tier

- Keep OTO2 at $397
- Add OTO3 at $997 ("Client Ready Accelerator")
- OTO3 = OTO2 + more hands-on time

**New funnel:**
```
$27 → Bumps → $97/$297 OTO1 → $397 OTO2 → $997 OTO3 → $5K+
```

**What OTO3 could include:**
- Everything in OTO2
- 2x 1:1 strategy calls (vs 1x)
- Priority implementation (14 days vs 30)
- First $500 ad spend management

**Pros:**
- Bridges $397 → $5K gap
- Follows proven slack adjuster model
- Captures high-intent buyers

**Cons:**
- Adds funnel complexity
- More fulfillment work

---

### Option D: Replace with 1:1 Call Offer

- Replace $397 DFY with $297-$497 Strategy Call
- 60-90 minute 1:1 to plan funnel
- Use call to qualify for $5K+ backend

**Pros:**
- Less fulfillment
- Direct path to high-ticket
- Follows Cat Howell's $500 model

**Cons:**
- Less "done-for-you" value
- Requires Michael's time
- May feel like step backward

---

### Option E: Reframe as "Implementation Sprint"

- Keep price at $397 (or raise)
- Reframe from DFY to "Implementation Sprint"
- "We build it WITH you in 14 days"

**Pros:**
- "Sprint" language works in OTO1
- Less fulfillment than pure DFY
- Cohort-based delivery option

**Cons:**
- More live involvement needed
- Must differentiate from OTO1 Sprint

---

## Recommendation

**Option B + elements of Option C:**

1. **Raise OTO2 to $497** — Research supports higher pricing
2. **Reframe to "100 Customers in 30 Days"** — Result-focused
3. **Consider $997 tier later** — Test $497 first, add slack adjuster if conversion holds

This delivers:
- Higher AOV now
- Room to add $997 tier without restructuring
- Result-focused positioning for 2026 buyer psychology

---

## Decision

**[ ] Option A** — Keep as-is ($397)
**[ ] Option B** — Raise to $497 + result reframe
**[ ] Option C** — Add $997 OTO3 tier
**[ ] Option D** — Replace with strategy call
**[ ] Option E** — Reframe as Implementation Sprint
**[ ] Other** — Custom approach
