# Client Ready = Codify-for-Coaches

> **STATUS UPDATE (2026-07-01): Customer-facing codify play NOT pursued.** Decided codify doesn't add enough value as a selling point. It remains the **internal production method** — how Client Ready deliverables get built with the skill tools — not a customer-facing angle or ascension. The cold front-end stays pain-first; the DFY sells its 6 deliverables on merit; the "Client Ready OS / running agent" ascension is shelved. The rung-map and reactivation ideas below are retained for reference only.

**The one shift:** written-for-you → **extracted-from-you** → **runs-itself.**

Same buyer (coaches whose expertise *is* the business). Same promise (get clients). Upgraded mechanism: your expertise codified into sovereign reference files you own, so your marketing sounds like you and eventually runs itself. Don't reinvent the funnel — layer the thesis onto every existing rung.

**The Codify thesis, in three lines:**
1. Context > prompts. Generic in, generic out — the fix is *your* context written down, not a better template.
2. Extract → codify → compound. Files built once write your ads/emails/VSLs going forward; every addition sharpens every future output.
3. Sovereignty. Markdown you keep, any AI runs. Owned, not rented — an appreciating asset, not a monthly expense.

**Why the name survives:** *Client Ready* now means **ready for clients AND ready to run in your voice.**

## (c) The back-end ascension: **Client Ready OS**

**Recommendation: name it `Client Ready OS`.** (Alternates considered: "The Client Machine," "Client Ready Live," "Your Client Ready Agent." OS wins — durable umbrella, engineering-voice, owns the "sovereign files + running system" combo without the hype of "autopilot.")

**What it is (the destination the funnel climbs toward):** a month-to-month subscription where —
1. Your reference files (your "brain") are **hosted and kept current**, and
2. A **weekly agent drafts your ads, emails, DMs, and VSL in your voice, from those files** — you approve, it ships, and
3. The **room** (weekly calls, community, DM access) stays.

The work shifts from *"make the content"* to *"approve the content."* That is the running-itself promise made concrete.

**How the current continuity reorganizes under it:**
- **Community ($97/mo) → Client Ready OS.** Keep $97/mo to validate; consider a higher "OS + agent" tier once the weekly-agent fulfillment is proven and automated.
- **Newsletter ($37/mo) → "OS Lite"** — monthly brain module + what's-working, the low-tier feeder.
- **Accelerator ($5K) → "Client Ready OS, installed with you"** — the done-with-you build of the whole system.

**Ascension logic (unchanged, resharpened):** buyer extracts (or has us extract) their brain → assets compound → they want it to *run* → they join the OS → power users take the Accelerator to have it fully installed. Community-first backend still applies (Accelerator sold from the OS room).

## Guardrails — how each is handled

1. **Every rung a real deliverable.** Each upgraded rung above ships something a coach can hold/run (files, generated assets, a working agent). No hollow tripwires.
2. **Front door stays paid.** $47 remains the funnel entry. Free "Build Order" magnet is organic-discovery only.
3. **Sort for delegators.** Built into the reactivation (email 4: "run it yourself / want it handled") and the DIY $47 vs DFY $197 fork. Coaches skew builder → but the delegator fork captures the ones who'll pay to skip the work.
4. **DFY fulfillment load.** 202 warm buyers → a DFY spike could swamp Michael's ~10-min-per-build review. Mitigations: (a) cap DFY slots/week and use honest scarcity in the close, (b) push more of the generation+QA into the Claude pipeline so review is lighter, (c) if uptake stays high, raise DFY price or route overflow to the DIY path. **Decide the weekly cap before sending email 4.**
5. **Self-liquidation = cold-ads concern.** Validate the upgraded offer on the **warm 202 first (zero ad cost)**. Only turn on lookalike ads once the reactivation proves the upgraded mechanism converts. Sequence: reactivate 202 → measure → then ads (folds into 2026-06-28-cash-flow-relaunch-plan.md, warm-first).
