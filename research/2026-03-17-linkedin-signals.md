# LinkedIn Signals — 2026-03-17

## Audience Pain Language (exact quotes from posts)
- "I’ve been posting every day but getting no results—no clients, no engagement."
- "Another coaching program that feels like a waste of money—when will I find something that actually works?"
- "Struggling to transition from corporate to coaching—how do I even start?"
- "My low-ticket offers aren’t converting—what am I doing wrong?"

## Competitor Positioning (how other coaches position)
- Many coaches focus on "breaking free from the 9-to-5" or "turning your side hustle into a full-time business."
- Some emphasize "scam-free" or "results-driven" coaching to counter anti-guru sentiment.
- Others highlight "affordable entry points" with low-ticket digital products to attract hesitant buyers.

## Opportunity Signals (high-engagement posts about problems Client Ready solves)
- Posts about "content grind fatigue" (posting daily with no ROI) resonate strongly.
- Discussions on "how to structure a coaching offer that sells" get high engagement.
- Coaches openly sharing their struggles with pricing and client acquisition spark conversations.

## Content Angles (hooks and framings worth adapting)
- "Why posting every day won’t fix your coaching business (and what will)"
- "The real reason your low-ticket offers aren’t selling—it’s not the price"
- "How I went from corporate burnout to booked-out coach in 6 months"

## Potential Leads (coaches struggling with offers)
- Coaches expressing frustration with "no clients despite consistent effort"
- Consultants questioning if their pricing is "too high or too low"
- Former corporate professionals unsure how to package their expertise
