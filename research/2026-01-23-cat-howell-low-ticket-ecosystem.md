# How to Build a Profitable Low Ticket Ecosystem from $17-$97 Offers

**Speaker:** Cat Howell
**Format:** Live Q&A / Training Call
**Key Product Mentioned:** Low Ticket Profit Lab ($88/month membership)

---

## Key Takeaways

### 1. Sell First, Build Later
- Don't wait until your product is perfect
- Cat launched her membership with an order page built 30 minutes before the call
- "The biggest thing I'm seeing is overthinking"
- Had $10K weeks from products that weren't even built yet (pre-orders)

### 2. One Big Idea Per Ecosystem
- Every product in your ecosystem should connect to ONE big idea
- Example: "When entrepreneurs tap into their zone of genius, sales become effortless"
- Don't try to hit everything at once — "you're not hitting anything"

### 3. Low Ticket Funnel Structure
- Front-end offer: $17-$97
- Order bump: Adds to AOV
- Upsell: $67-$97
- Target AOV: $100-$200 per funnel
- One funnel with 3 offers can be profitable standalone

### 4. Customer Awareness Levels (Eugene Schwartz)
| Level | Description | How to Sell |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Very Aware | Existing customers | Direct: "Here's the link. Buy now." |
| Solution Aware | Know they need help | Lead with solution |
| Problem Aware | Know the pain, not the fix | Storytelling, pain-point marketing |
| Unaware | Don't know they have a problem | Biggest market, hardest to reach |

**Key insight:** Problem-aware and unaware are the BIGGEST markets — that's where the money is.

### 5. Headlines That Work
- Lead with tangible outcomes, not emotional language
- People don't think "I need to feel safe investing" — they think "My bank account is empty every month"
- Don't reveal your method in the headline — leave mystery
- Always expand features: "Monthly call with Cat" → "Monthly call so you can accelerate results and get more profit"

### 6. Low Ticket Offer Structure
1. **Welcome video**
2. **Mindset shift** (perspective change — this is what hooks them)
3. **The How-To** (tactical content)
4. **Next Step** (CTA)

Each video: 6-10 minutes max. Shorter = more valuable for busy entrepreneurs.

### 7. Facebook Ads Post-Andromeda Update
- Old method: One winning ad for years → stopped working in April 2024
- New method: Creative along customer awareness spectrum
- Need ads for problem-aware AND solution-aware audiences
- Use Instagram stories/reels as ad creative source
- "$3 a day strategy" for follower growth (90% order bump rate)

### 8. Niche Down to Cut Through Noise
- "Facebook ads" is too broad — everyone teaches it
- Better: "Facebook ads for country western clothing stores"
- Or: Lead with a unique method/framework with mystery
  - "The Ugly Duck framework"
  - "The one sentence that will change your business"
  - "$3/day strategy"

### 9. Selling is Service
- "If you really care about helping people, you gotta sell"
- "It is in everyone's best interest that you sell"
- "People love it when you sell to them" (if you're passionate about what you're offering)

## Audience Advice Given

### For Multi-Topic Experts (Minlang - Financial Literacy + Human Design)
- Don't cram everything into one offer
- Break into micro-offers that feed the ecosystem
- Lead with tangible outcome: "How to have surplus cash at end of month"
- Explain the method (subconscious work) INSIDE the training, not in headline

### For Zone of Genius Coaches (Khan - French coaches)
- Separate funnels for different avatars (new coaches vs. established but misaligned)
- Lead with money/relationships — not "status"
- What worked: "$27 masterclass + $17 bump + $67 upsell"
- Live launching works because you're TALKING about it constantly
- Evergreen still needs some nurturing energy

### For E-commerce Ad Experts (Anna)
- Niche down: "How to position testimonials on your e-commerce site to triple conversions"
- Specific > broad
- Don't overthink tech — you can sell via PayPal invoice and deliver via private YouTube links
- Sell before you build
