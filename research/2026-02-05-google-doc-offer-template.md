# Google Doc Offer Template (Phased Approach)

**Source:** [Google Offer Template] Phased Approach.md

---

## Template Structure

A complete Google Doc sales letter format for high-ticket offers. Designed for DM-based selling without traditional sales pages.

---

## The Sections

### 1. Vision
Open with founder name + hook + promise.

**Template:**
> "[YOUR NAME] here, good to see you!
> Here's what's going down…
> I'm helping ## of folks [VISION]."

### 2. Constraints
What they WON'T have to do. List 4-5 things they hate.

**Template:**
> "We're going to do this together without…
> - [Thing They Hate]
> - [Thing They Hate]
> - [Thing They Hate]"

### 3. Plan
The 3 systems/phases that make up the program.

**Structure for each phase:**
1. What most people do (old plan)
2. Why that leads to terrible result
3. What you do differently
4. The outcome

### 4. Process
Week-by-week or phase-by-phase breakdown.

**Key principle:** "Make this as specific as possible. Tangible beats intangible. Time frames work wonders."

### 5. Proof Statement
List niches/results you've achieved. Examples:
- Manifesting
- Real Estate Investing
- Personal Development
- Relationship Coaching
- Marketing & Lead Gen
- Health/Binge Eating
- Anxiety Relief

**Or use specific client stories with quotes.**

### 6. Hand Holding
What support they get. Meeting cadence, community access, feedback areas.

### 7. Investment
Use a named payment model:
- "Pay as you profit model"
- "Pay as you create model"
- "Pay as you scale model"

**Template:**
> "Your investment to [OUTCOME] follows the [PAYMENT MODEL]
> Which is why you can get started today for [$$$$]/Week."

### 8. Terms
"Love It Or Leave It" — community, not hostage situation.

Options:
- Leave anytime, can't rejoin for a year
- Commit for X weeks, then flexible

### 9. Guarantee
Can be: "No guarantees. Big promises or refunds." (risk on you overdelivering)

### 10. Amplifiers
Bonuses and perks. Focus on HOW each benefits them.

### 11. FAQ
Personal email for questions.

### 12. CTA
**Template:**
> "Don't send me any money now!
> Step #1: PM Me 'I'm In'
> Step #2: We'll get you enrolled
> Step #3: You'll go through a quick 5 minute onboarding process where you can book your 1-on-1 call"

---

## Key Insights

### Constraint-Led Positioning
Lead with what they WON'T have to do. This pre-handles objections and makes the offer feel different.

### Named Payment Models
Naming the payment structure ("Pay as you profit") makes arbitrary pricing feel like a system.

### PM-Based CTA
No payment link in the doc. Forces conversation, builds relationship, allows qualification.

### Proof Format
List of niches served is powerful — shows breadth without needing individual testimonials.

---

## Application to Client Ready

Could use this format for:
- Accelerator ($5K) sales doc
- Sprint ($297) upgrade pitch
- DFY ($397) proposal

The "PM me 'I'm In'" CTA matches warm DM-based selling approach.
