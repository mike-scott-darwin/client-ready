# Email Sequences & Order Bump Copy

Consolidated research on email strategy and high-converting bump copy.

---

## Non-Buyer 30-Day Email Strategy

### The Problem

Non-buyers who don't convert in 14 days currently go dark. No further nurture. No long-term ascension path.

**2026 Reality:**
- Average sales cycle: 14-30 days
- Non-buyers need different cadence than buyers
- Email list is an asset — can't just abandon

---

## Strategic Framework

### Miles Stutz Insight

> "Lead customers to the LIMITATIONS of their purchase to create a need for the call."

**Applied to Non-Buyers:** Lead non-buyers to the COST of NOT purchasing.

### Three Core Emotions

| Emotion | Application |
|---------|-------------|
| **Recognition (Pride)** | "You're not like other coaches who buy courses and don't implement" |
| **FOMO (Regret)** | "Every week without a clear offer = more wasted time" |
| **Reciprocity (Gratitude)** | Value-first emails that create obligation |

---

## Recommended 30-Day Sequence Structure

**Cadence:** 10 emails over 30 days (not daily — non-buyers need breathing room)

| Phase | Days | Emails | Purpose |
|-------|------|--------|---------|
| **Abandonment Recovery** | 1-7 | 4 | Convert hot leads who almost bought |
| **Value + Relationship** | 8-14 | 2 | Build trust, no pitch |
| **Re-Engagement** | 15-23 | 3 | Fresh angles, social proof |
| **Soft Close** | 24-30 | 1 | Final pitch + transition to nurture |

### Phase 1: Abandonment Recovery (Days 1-7)

**Goal:** Convert the "almost-bought" segment

| Day | Email | Template Type |
|-----|-------|---------------|
| 0 (1hr) | Soft Abandon | Cart recovery |
| 2 | Cost of Waiting | Pain amplification |
| 4 | Objection Killer | "I already know my offer" |
| 6 | Contrarian Hook | "Stop niching down" |

### Phase 2: Value + Relationship (Days 8-14)

**Goal:** Build trust through pure value. No pitch.

| Day | Email | Content |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 10 | Direct Close | "Link's here if you want it" |
| 14 | Pivot to Value | Process vs Outcome |

### Phase 3: Re-Engagement (Days 15-23)

**Goal:** Re-pitch with new angles for those who needed time

| Day | Email | Angle |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 16 | Wrong Wrong Wrong | "Build it and they will come — Wrong." |
| 19 | Case Study | Specific transformation story |
| 22 | The Real Cost | Calculate cost of waiting another month |

### Phase 4: Soft Close (Days 24-30)

**Goal:** Final opportunity before transitioning to weekly nurture

| Day | Email | Angle |
|-----|-------|-------|
| 26 | You Can't Go It Alone | Free resources = wasted time |
| 30 | Last Call | Guarantee + final pitch |

**After Day 30:** Transition to weekly broadcast list

## Email Content Strategy

### Core Themes to Hit

Based on audience pain points:

1. **Validation anxiety** — "What if nobody buys?"
2. **Wasted effort** — Built infrastructure for offer that didn't sell
3. **Wrong advice** — Told to quit job, build course, run ads
4. **Imposter syndrome** — Need more credentials before charging premium

### Contrarian Angles

| Angle | Email Hook |
|-------|------------|
| Anti-niche | "Stop niching down" |
| Anti-course | "You don't need another course" |
| Anti-perfect | "Your offer doesn't need to be perfect" |
| Anti-quit-job | "Keep your 9-to-5" |
| Anti-funnel | "Don't build a funnel yet" |

### CTA Strategy

| Phase | CTA Type | Example |
|-------|----------|---------|
| Days 1-10 | Direct | "Grab the $27 guide: [LINK]" |
| Days 11-14 | None | Pure value, no link |
| Days 15-26 | Soft | "Link's in the PS if you want it" |
| Days 27-30 | Direct | "Last chance: [LINK]" |

## Order Bump Copy Swipes

### Key Patterns from High-Converters

#### 1. Specific Numbers
Every bump uses specific quantities:
- 30 templates, 9 secrets, 60 topics, 4 hacks, 6 scripts

#### 2. Price Anchoring
Compare to higher-value alternative:
- "Clients pay $6,000/month" → $27
- "Could charge $49" → $17
- "$497 consulting calls" → $17

#### 3. Dollar Results
Tie to revenue/savings:
- "$8,000,000 generated"
- "$1M per script"
- "$100K+ per month"
- "37% reduction in ad spend"

#### 4. Time Savings
Emphasize speed:
- "in minutes"
- "instead of hours"
- "30 DAYS of emails in minutes"

#### 5. Exclusivity
Create urgency through scarcity:
- "Not available anywhere else"
- "Only on this page"
- "Can't get this anywhere else"

#### 6. Fill-in-the-Blank Framing
Reduces perceived effort:
- "Simply click a template, fill in the blank... hit send"
- "Swipe to turn DMs into..."

#### 7. Client Tier Proof
Borrowed authority:
- "6, 7 and 8-Figure clients"
- "7 & 8 figure clients"

## Order Bump Copy Examples

### Example 1: 30 Fill-in-the-Blank Email Templates ($27)

**Checkbox headline:** "Yes, Please! Include the Upgrade!"

**Copy:**
> ONE-TIME OFFER – ONLY $27: GET 30 FILL IN THE BLANK EMAIL TEMPLATES! Never ask yourself after the 1-Email Workday Challenge "What the heck do I write about now?" Steal my 30 BEST, FILL-IN-THE-BLANK email templates that will give you an EXTRA 30 DAYS of emails in minutes. Simply click a template, fill in the blank for your subscribers and offer, then hit send. These are the same templates I've used to generate over $8,000,000 for my 6, 7 and 8-Figure private clients, and they can be yours today too! CHECK the Box Above "Yes, Please! Include the Upgrade!" to add these 30 FILL-IN-THE-BLANK Email Templates to your order for only $27! (Not available anywhere else as my clients pay me $6,000 a month and you get it for $27 – only on this page.)

**Techniques:**
- Pain point opener
- Specific number (30)
- Time savings
- Dollar proof ($8M)
- Price anchor ($6K/mo vs $27)
- Exclusivity

### Example 2: 60 Viral Topics + 60 Hooks Bundle ($17)

**Checkbox headline:** "ADD 60 Viral Topics + 60 Hooks Bundle"

**Copy:**
> Grab This No-Brainer Add On For $17: I'm going to give you the 60 Most Viral Topics to speak about that get views on ALL major platforms PLUS+ get the 60 Most Viral Hooks that grab attention in the first 2 SECONDS of any video. The first 2 seconds dictate if the video wins or loses. This bundle I could easily charge $49 for it but steal it from me today for just $17

**Techniques:**
- "No-Brainer Add On" framing
- Specific numbers (60 + 60)
- Specific timeframe (2 SECONDS)
- Stakes statement (wins or loses)
- Price anchor ($49 vs $17)
- Casual language ("steal it from me")

### Example 3: 4 Secret Targeting Hacks ($17)

**Checkbox headline:** "Yes, I Want Access To The '4 Secret Targeting Hacks' Video Series"

**Copy:**
> ONE TIME OFFER (97% OFF): Get Access To These "4 Secret Targeting Hacks" Video Series Where I Reveal My Most Hidden Targeting Secrets That Have Allowed My Most Successful Clients To Jump To 7 and 8 Figures In Record Time While Bypassing The Hidden Social Media Algorithm That Allows You To Spend EVEN Less While Generating Even More Customers! **Techniques:**
- Percentage discount (97% OFF)
- "Secret" and "Hidden" language
- Client results (7 and 8 Figures)
- Outcome paradox (Spend Less + More Customers)
- Price anchor ($497 vs $17)
- Specific result (37% reduction)
