# End-of-Day Crystallize

## Question Asked
You spent the day doing honesty work — killing language that over-promised and replacing it with what you actually deliver. The Blueprint reframe, the Self-Paced removal, the qualifier "not a fully built live funnel" — these are all moves toward alignment. But the session also revealed something structural: your value ladder now has a clean $297 → $397 → $5K progression where each tier is defined by who does the work (you build / you implement from a custom doc / Michael builds). That clarity is real. What's less clear is whether the person who buys the Blueprint at $397 — someone willing to pay more than the Sprint buyer but not ready for $5K — actually has the skill to implement from a strategy document. The Sprint buyer gets 4 weeks of live coaching. The Blueprint buyer gets a 60-minute call. The harder deliverable comes with less support.

**Is the Blueprint buyer someone who can execute from a document, or is the $397 price point attracting someone who thinks they're buying closer to done-for-them than "here's your strategy, go build"?**

## User Response
Clarified that Sprint buyers get WhatsApp group access for accountability + sequenced training with homework — not just templates. At $297, that's structured ongoing support. Blueprint buyer pays $100 more but gets no ongoing accountability mechanism beyond a single 60-minute call. The distinction: Blueprint buyer is paying for Michael's brain (strategy done right), not hand-holding. They're a different person — someone who knows what they want and can execute from a document.

User's instinct: the price point and positioning are reasonable. The data will tell once it's live — if Blueprint buyers convert but don't implement, that's the signal to watch.

## Insight Captured
The value ladder tiers are now cleanly differentiated by support model, not just deliverable:
- Sprint ($297): structured training + WhatsApp accountability group — ongoing support, you build
- Blueprint ($397): custom strategy doc + 1 integration call — Michael's brain, you implement
- Accelerator ($5K): done-with-you — both build together

Watch metric: Blueprint implementation rate. If buyers convert but don't implement, the product may need an accountability layer or the positioning needs to filter harder for self-starters.
