> "Then Miles' team suggested adding more order bumps. That single adjustment shifted everything."

## What Actually Drove Growth

### 1. OTO1 Implementation
- Clear OTO1 positioning
- Simple psychological upsell flow
- "Backend finally started working for me instead of leaving money on the table"

### 2. Follow-Up Email Sequences
- Well-timed (not pushy, just intentional)
- Strategic sequences, not just broadcasts

### 3. More Order Bumps
- The single biggest lever for AOV
- 64% AOV increase from this alone

## Tracking Note

> "Some sales were not fully reflected inside Meta due to iOS privacy changes and recent Meta updates. But you can clearly see the real numbers inside GoHighLevel (GHL)."

**Takeaway:** Don't trust Meta's reported conversions. Check actual payment processor (Stripe, GHL, etc.) for real numbers.
