# Context Engineering vs Prompt Engineering — YouTube Mining

## Source

YouTube video on why "prompt engineering" is a distraction and "context engineering" is what actually produces quality AI output.

## Core Thesis

AI is an amplifier, not a generator. The quality of output is determined entirely by the structure and quality of the information (context) you feed it before you ask anything. Prompts without a system are "ingredients without a kitchen."

## Key Frameworks

**The Context Layer** — Build structured middleware between you and AI:

- Identity: Who you are
- Goals: What you are trying to achieve this quarter/week
- Projects: Active work and its status
- Style: Your voice, tone, and preferences
- Decisions: Recent choices and the reasoning behind them

**Capture, Structure, Feed Forward** — Three-step execution:

1. Capture: Systematically record inputs (decisions, interactions, ideas) in one place
2. Structure: Organize data with types, dates, and connections so it is machine-readable
3. Feed Forward: Load structured context into AI before asking it to work. This primes the AI to think like you.

## Key Insights

- Lists of "100 Best Prompts" are useless because they lack your specific business nuance
- AI has no memory of your life, past decisions, or constraints — without context it hallucinates a generic persona
- Feed AI chaos, get "polished chaos" — grammatically correct but useless
- Obsessing over specific tools is shortsighted because tools change — building a context capture system is the transferable skill
- "Tools change, systems last"
