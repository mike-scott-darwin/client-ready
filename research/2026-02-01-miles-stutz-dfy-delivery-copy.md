# Miles Stutz DFY Delivery Copy Analysis

**Source:** Michael Scott - Daily Client Funnel_Feedback.pdf (Miles Stutz post-purchase delivery document)

---

## Purpose

This is the document buyers receive after purchasing Miles' Done-For-You Daily Client Funnel. It's designed to:
1. Validate their purchase decision
2. Build excitement before they open the deliverable
3. Reinforce the transformation they're about to experience

---

## Key Copy Techniques

### 1. Immediate Validation
> "I want to congratulate you on making one of the MOST IMPORTANT decisions of your entrepreneurial journey"

Opens by making buyer feel smart for purchasing.

### 2. "While Your Competitors..." Contrast Structure

Three parallel comparisons:
- While competitors have NO IDEA → you're about to install the exact system
- While most entrepreneurs are a few bad months from corporate → you cover costs on day 1
- While everyone is a "backup dancer for algorithms" → you have automatic clients

**Pattern:** While [bad thing competitors do] → you [good thing you now have]

### 3. "It's Your Turn" Repetition

Builds momentum through repeated phrase:
- ✅ It's your turn to wake up to stripe hits...
- ✅ It's your turn to never worry where your next client is coming from...
- ✅ It's your turn to cover all your costs on the first day of every month...
- ✅ It's your turn to have a vacation-proof business...
- ✅ It's your turn to finally join the 1% club...

### 4. Specific Outcomes (Miles' Numbers)
- "100-300 buyers every week"
- "4-6 high-profit clients daily"
- "Cover all living costs and business expenses on the first day of every month"
- "Opening 10 spots and having them fill in 6 hours"

### 5. Anti-Content Angle
- "professional backup dancer for social media algorithms"
- "spending 2 hours creating content that gets 12 likes"
- "deleting Instagram off their phone"

This resonates with coaches tired of the content hamster wheel.

### 6. Mini Case Studies by Type

**Agencies:** inconsistent revenue → covering expenses on day 1, everything else pure profit

**Consultants:** chasing clients + following up on invoices → always sold out with waitlist, 10 spots fill in 6 hours

**Coaches:** 2 hours content for 12 likes → deleting Instagram, 100-300 automatic buyers/week

### 7. Exclusivity Framing
- "An Ability That Less Than 1% Of Coaches Will Ever Have"
- "finally join the 1% club"

### 8. Simple Next Step
> "All you have to do is: Crack open your Custom Daily Client Funnel, follow your personalized Installation Plan, and implement everything I show you inside this PDF... That's it."

## Application to Client Ready Bumps

**For Bump 1 (Templates $37):**
- Use "While others..." contrast: While others stare at blank pages → you plug in and go
- Add specificity: "3 hours instead of 3 days"
- Mini case study structure: "Coaches who used these templates..."

**For Bump 2 (Traffic Kit $67):**
- Anti-content angle fits perfectly: "Stop being a backup dancer for algorithms"
- "First 10 buyers" roadmap = specific outcome
- "It's your turn" structure could work well here

**General principles:**
- More checkmarks/bullets with outcomes
- Contrast structure (while competitors... you...)
- Specificity over vagueness
- Exclusivity framing
