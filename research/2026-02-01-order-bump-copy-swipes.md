## Key Patterns

### 1. Specific Numbers
Every bump uses specific quantities:
- 30 templates, 9 secrets, 60 topics, 4 hacks, 6 scripts

### 2. Price Anchoring
Compare to higher-value alternative:
- "Clients pay $6,000/month" → $27
- "Could charge $49" → $17
- "$497 consulting calls" → $17

### 3. Dollar Results
Tie to revenue/savings:
- "$8,000,000 generated"
- "$1M per script"
- "$100K+ per month"
- "37% reduction in ad spend"

### 4. Time Savings
Emphasize speed:
- "in minutes"
- "instead of hours"
- "30 DAYS of emails in minutes"

### 5. Exclusivity
Create urgency through scarcity:
- "Not available anywhere else"
- "Only on this page"
- "Can't get this anywhere else"

### 6. Fill-in-the-Blank Framing
Reduces perceived effort:
- "Simply click a template, fill in the blank... hit send"
- "Swipe to turn DMs into..."

### 7. Client Tier Proof
Borrowed authority:
- "6, 7 and 8-Figure clients"
- "7 & 8 figure clients"

### 8. Headline Framing Options
- "Yes, Please! Include the Upgrade!"
- "Yes, include the [BENEFIT] with my order!"
- "ADD [SPECIFIC THING]"
- "Yes, I Want Access To..."

### 9. Opening Framing Options
- "ONE-TIME OFFER – ONLY $X"
- "ONE TIME OFFER (Just $X)"
- "ONE TIME OFFER (97% OFF)"
- "Grab This No-Brainer Add On For $X"
