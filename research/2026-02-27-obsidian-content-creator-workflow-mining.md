# Obsidian for Content Creators — Blog Post Mining

## Source

Blog post from a 13-year content creator running a 6-figure writing business using Obsidian as the backbone.

## Core Thesis

The real value of a knowledge system is not organization — it is making your best thinking accessible when you need it. Content creation becomes assembly, not creation from scratch. Knowledge and content compound when you link raw thinking to finished output.

## Key Workflow

### Capture (NOT in Obsidian)

Obsidian mobile is bad. Quick capture uses separate tools:

- Voice notes: Letterly (transcribes automatically, 2-tap copy)
- Text notes: Bear (zero-friction Apple shortcut, syncs across devices)
- Key principle: zero friction when an idea strikes. Capture everything.

This validates the OpenClaw phone capture architecture: quick thoughts go through Discord/Telegram messaging, not Obsidian mobile. Obsidian is for the laptop only.

### Knowledge Base (Obsidian)

- Atomic notes: one idea per note, linked together via wiki links
- Mimics brain synapses — connections, not categories
- Graph view shows clusters of related ideas
- Link raw knowledge to finished content — when revisiting a topic 6 months later, see what was already covered
- Knowledge compounds: each note makes every related note more valuable

### Content Assembly

Writing is pulling together micro ideas into a complete piece:

- Main writing in center pane
- Supporting ideas in side panes
- "Instead of starting from scratch, simply assembling insights already captured"
- No more hunting through folders or trying to remember where something was saved

### Content Calendar

Projects plugin: visual calendar view for scheduling across platforms. Separate calendars for short-form, newsletters, articles. Each calendar entry links to the actual content note.

### Launch Mapping

Canvas feature: visual whiteboard for email launch sequences. Spot gaps in strategy. See the big picture without keeping it in your head.

## Mapping to Client Ready

### What validates our architecture

- Capture happens OUTSIDE Obsidian (phone messaging via Discord/Telegram to OpenClaw, not Obsidian mobile)
- Obsidian is the laptop viewing and linking layer only
- Assembly over creation: reference files (soul, offer, audience, voice) ARE the pre-captured insights. /ads, /organic, /vsl assemble from them.
- Content compounding: linking research to decisions to content means nothing gets done twice

### What we could adopt

- Atomic notes in journal/: one idea per entry, wiki-linked to reference files
- Canvas for funnel launch mapping (visual whiteboard for email sequences, ad creative rotation, OTO flow)
- Projects plugin for content calendar (currently tracked in content-strategy.md tables, could become visual)
- Backlink finished content to source research: content/published/ entries link back to research/ files that inspired them

### What we skip

- Bear/Letterly capture apps (replaced by Discord/Telegram messaging to OpenClaw)
- Manual folder organization (our reference/ structure already handles this)
- Graph view as primary discovery tool (the agent does pattern discovery via Job 8 graduation scans)

## Key Quote

"The real magic isn't in the app itself. It's in building a system that captures your best thinking and makes it accessible when you need it most."

This IS the Main Branch thesis. The app is interchangeable. The system — reference files as context, structured capture, assembly over creation — is what produces results.
