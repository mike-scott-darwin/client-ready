# Cat Howell AOV Benchmarks (Feb 2026)

Source: Cat Howell Facebook group reply (Feb 4, 2026)

---

## The System

### Phase 1: Order Bumps First

> "Start with your order bumps as these are the easiest to create - have three on your page and ideally 50% of your customers should be buying the order bumps (keep testing until it gets to this rate)."

**Target:** 50% bump take rate (combined)

**Why bumps first:** Easiest to create, fastest to test, biggest AOV lever before touching upsells.

---

### Phase 2: Upsells (One at a Time)

Add upsells sequentially. Don't launch multiple at once.

**Conversion benchmarks by price:**

| Price Point | Expected Conversion |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Below $60 | 20-30% |
| Above $60 | 10-20% |
| Membership trials | 5-10% |

---

## Key Rules

1. **Test with volume:** 30 sales per split test minimum before deciding
2. **AOV gate:** Don't scale ads until AOV is solid over $100
3. **Sequential testing:** One upsell at a time, not multiple
