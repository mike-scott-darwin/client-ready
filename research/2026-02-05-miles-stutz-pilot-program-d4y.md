# Miles Stutz Pilot Program: Digital Snacks Install (D4Y)

**Source:** Pilot Program_ The Digital Snacks Install.md

---

## The Hook

> "Want to know why I'd rather scale my coaching business to $100k/mo+, getting 100+ daily customers instead of getting one big client here & there?"

**Answer:** Cash flow $100k/month without:
- Hiring employees
- Doing 1:1 client work
- Posting face on social media / building personal brand

---

## The Pain Points (2018-2024)

Miles positions his transformation story around these pains:

| Pain | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Always chasing clients | DMing leads, following up on sales calls, begging for strategy sessions — even on vacation |
| Constant content production | Posting every day, giving away best stuff free, hoping someone buys |
| Constant lead generation | Cold outreach, commenting, growth hacking, praying algorithm works |
| Always competing | Being undercut by competitors with "you don't pay" guarantees |

---

## The 2025 Transformation

> "My Digital Snacks let me solo-generate 100+ customers and $5k-$10k daily in as little as 4 hours of work"

**Three components:**

1. **Digital Snacks ($7-$97):** Automates marketing, attracts customers, pre-sells for high-ticket. "Turns browsers into Stripe notifications without daily content or DMs."

2. **The Profit Center:** Replaces sales team — buyers upgrade themselves to high-ticket without handholding. Provides daily cash flow.

3. **Instant ROI Ads:** Generate 100+ customers daily in 2-3 hours/week. High-touch offers reserved for $10k+ premium clients.

---

## The Pilot Program Offer

**Price:** $1,997 (or $1,250 down + $1,250 in 30 days)
**Format:** Done-for-you (D4Y) installation
**Timeline:** 14 days to launch, 60 days of support
**Capacity:** 5 spots (real scarcity)

**Promise:** 100+ customers from one Digital Snack

### Phase 1: Digital Snacks
- Uncover most profitable snack from existing expertise/content
- Build Digital Snack offer + 2 order bumps + 2 OTOs
- Handle all market research and messaging

### Phase 2: The Profit Center
- Complete buyer-to-client pipeline
- Backend automations
- Write emails & ascension offers
- Works whether online or not

### Phase 3: Instant ROI Ads
- Implement proprietary ad system ($0 to $2,000/day profitable)
- Write ads and set up campaigns
- Self-funding, automated customer acquisition
