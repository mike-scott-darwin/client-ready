## Key Frameworks

### Eugene Schwartz Awareness Levels

| Level | How to Sell |
|-------|-------------|
| Very Aware | Direct: "Here's the link. Buy now." |
| Solution Aware | Lead with solution |
| Problem Aware | Storytelling, pain-point marketing |
| Unaware | Biggest market, hardest to reach |

**Key insight:** Problem-aware and unaware are the BIGGEST markets.

### Low Ticket Offer Structure

1. Welcome video
2. Mindset shift (perspective change — this hooks them)
3. The How-To (tactical content)
4. Next Step (CTA)

Each video: 6-10 minutes max.

### Andromeda Update (Meta Ads 2025-2026)

| Before | After (Andromeda) |
|--------|-------------------|
| You told Facebook who to target | Creative IS the targeting |
| Interest targeting worked | Broad targeting + creative as filter |
| 2-3 winning ads could scale | Need 15-20+ diverse creatives |

**Requirements:**
- 10+ video (UGC/Direct Response) + 10+ statics
- Diversify ANGLES, not colors
- Value proposition in first 5 seconds
- Use Conversions API (CAPI)

## Instagram Content Patterns

**Data:** 235 posts analyzed (Feb 2020 - Jan 2026)

### Top Hook Patterns

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| Counter-intuitive | "My ads look like garbage but get 50% cheaper customers" |
| "Here's the exact..." | "Here's our exact campaign launch structure" |
| Story → Lesson | "I spent $1K testing what I thought was stupid..." |
| Problem acknowledgment | "Why aren't my ads working anymore?" |
| Comment CTA | "Say Seven if you want my free guide" |

### Engagement Data

| Post Type | Result |
|-----------|--------|
| Comment CTAs | 100-485 comments (best for lead gen) |
| Lifestyle/personal | 2K-28K likes (brand building) |
| Marketing content | Fewer likes, more comments |

### Themes She Repeats

1. **Simplicity wins** — "Just 2 campaigns"
2. **Ugly beats pretty** — Raw creative outperforms polished
3. **Creative > Targeting** — Meta's AI handles targeting
4. **Refresh weekly** — 50-100 new ads/week via AI
5. **Test before scale** — Validate first
