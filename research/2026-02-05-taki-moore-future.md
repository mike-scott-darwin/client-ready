# The Future: Taki Moore

**Source:** The Future_ Taki Moore.md

---

## 1: Founder Mojo

> "People don't buy coaching; they buy coaches."

**Key principles:**
- Your energy, lifestyle, and authenticity are the real attractors
- Most magnetic thing: **give yourself full permission to be yourself**
- Build a business where scaling makes your life better, not more complex
- **Mojo = Living your best life + running a great business** (one without the other = burnout or boredom)

**When mojo is lost:** Often means you've lost track of what you actually want.

**Your #1 role as founder:** Chief Wanting Officer (CWO) — know what you want.

---

## 2: Maker vs. Manager

**Two founder roles:**
- **Maker (creator):** Create new ideas, communicate them, set direction
- **Manager (operator):** People, cash, execution

Most coaches are makers by nature.

> "Scale yourself into the fun bits, not out of them."

**Key insight:** If you don't love your business at $30K/month, you won't magically love it at $300K/month. Add zeros only to a business you enjoy now.

---

## 3: Content & Brand Width

> "Stop filling space with content. Content should expand minds, not just fill feeds."

**Principles:**
- Founder content > content machine
- If you wouldn't read it yourself, don't send it
- Shift from *volume* to *brand width* (time on brand)

**Data point:** Every buyer had consumed 47 minutes of content before purchasing.

> "The more they binge, the more they buy."

> "Do cool shit, share cool shit."

---

## 4: Sales Without Sales Calls

> "Everybody loves to buy; nobody likes to be sold."

**Taki's test:** Removed sales calls for 3 months → **4x sales with no sales team**

**The formula:**
- Clear offer
- Weekly deadline-driven campaigns
- Bingeable content
= Frictionless sales

**Testing principle:** Run big changes as a 6-week experiment. Problem → Hypothesis → Test → Review.

---

## 5: Delivery & Client Experience

**Model evolution:**
- Old: DIY (course only) vs. Done-for-You (agency)
- New: **Done Accelerated With You (DXY)** — AI tools + personalized coaching for faster wins

**Retention formula:** Results + Relationship

> "As the world gets more digital, personal proximity becomes the premium."

Speed + proximity = the true products at higher levels.

---

## 6: Money & Motivation

> "Money is 'fun tickets.' It's not the goal, it's the fuel for creating and living."

**Clarity principle:**
- Nothing is more dangerous than someone who knows exactly what they want
- Nothing is more painful than someone who doesn't
- If you don't know what you want, start with what you *don't* want

---

## Golden Threads

> "Live a dope life. Do dope shit. Share it."

- The more real you are, the more attractive you become
- Scale passion, not just profit
- Protect your mojo at all costs — it's the multiplier for every strategy
