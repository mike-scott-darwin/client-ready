# Miles Stutz Template Product Structure

**Source:** DS_ 3 PROVEN 6-FIGURE DIGITAL SNACK TEMPLATES.pdf

---

## Purpose

This document shows how Miles structures and packages his template products. Useful for informing what to include in template-based bumps and how to position them.

---

## What Miles Includes in His Template Pack

1. **3 Complete Digital Snack Funnels** (each "6+ figures")
2. **All Landing Pages, Offer Stacks, and Sales Copy**
3. **Proven Ad Creative That's Currently Running**
4. **Complete Upsell Sequences and OTO Pages**
5. **Messaging Maps and Client Profiles**
6. **Pre-Launch Email Sequences**

---

## Positioning by Use Case

Miles positions templates by WHO YOU HELP, not just what's inside:

| Template | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| DM Scripts | Lead generation or sales |
| Digital Snacks | Content creation or product development |
| Daily Client Funnel | Systems and automation |

**Insight:** Categorize by outcome/audience, not features.

---

## His Customization Framework

### Step 1: Choose Your Template
Pick based on expertise and target audience.

### Step 2: Customize the Copy
- Replace industry-specific terms with your niche
- Adjust pain points to match your audience
- Modify pricing based on market positioning
- Update testimonials and proof elements

### Step 3: Adapt the Pricing
- Test different price points
- Consider positioning (premium vs accessible)
- Maintain the same funnel structure and flow

### Step 4: Deploy and Test
- Start with proven ad creative as inspiration
- Use the exact funnel structure
- Test one element at a time

## Application to Client Ready Bump 1

Current Bump 1 (Plug & Play Templates - $37) includes:
- Offer document template (with filled example)
- Landing page swipe files (3 layouts)
- 30-day email sequence
- Messaging maps & client profiles
- Copy-paste headlines & hooks (25+)

**Gaps vs Miles' offering:**
- ❌ No ad creative included (that's in Bump 2)
- ❌ No OTO/upsell page templates
- ✅ Has messaging maps
- ✅ Has email sequences

**Potential additions for future version:**
- OTO page templates (Sprint, DFY)
- More niche-specific variations
- Video script templates

---

## Key Insight

Miles proves you can charge premium ($hundreds) for "templates" when positioned as:
1. **Proven** ("6-figure funnels")
2. **Complete** (not just one piece, the whole system)
3. **Currently running** (not outdated)
4. **Use-case specific** (match your expertise)

For Bump 1 at $37, the value prop is solid. Could potentially unbundle into higher-ticket template product later.
