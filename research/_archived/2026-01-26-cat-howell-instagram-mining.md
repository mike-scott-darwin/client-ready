## Top Performing Hook Patterns

### Pattern 1: Counter-Intuitive Claim
> "[Thing that sounds wrong] is actually [getting results]"

Examples:
- "My ads look like complete garbage, but they're getting customers 50% cheaper"
- "Hot take: Your fancy ads are probably hurting your results"

**Why it works:** Creates curiosity gap, challenges assumptions

### Pattern 2: "Here's the exact..."
> "Here's the exact [system/structure] I use for [result]"

Examples:
- "Here's our exact campaign launch structure for Meta"
- "Here's my 2-framework approach"

**Why it works:** Specificity signals value, transparency builds trust

### Pattern 3: Story → Lesson
> "[Time ago] I [made mistake]. [Bad result]. Here's what I learned..."

Examples:
- "Spent $1,000 testing what I thought was a 'stupid' offer idea..."
- "A couple years ago I woke up with a 'brilliant' idea... Zero sales."

**Why it works:** Vulnerability + practical takeaway

### Pattern 4: Problem Acknowledgment
> "[Pain point they feel]? It's not what you think..."

Examples:
- "Why aren't my ads working anymore?"
- "Your Facebook campaigns aren't failing. Your tracking is broken."

**Why it works:** Meets them where they are, reframes the problem

### Pattern 5: Comment CTA
> "Comment [WORD] and I'll send you [specific thing]"

Examples:
- "(Say Seven if you want my free ads scaling guide...)"
- "Comment LOW below and I'll send you the training"

**Why it works:** Drives engagement, starts DM conversations

## Engagement Analysis

### Highest Comment Posts (Lead Gen)
Posts with "Comment X for Y" CTAs drove 100-485 comments. Examples:
- "Comment BUNDLE below for instant access" → 105 comments
- "(Say Seven if you want my free ads guide)" → 261 comments
- "Comment ADS for the step by step campaign set up" → 270 comments

### Highest Like Posts (Brand)
Personal/lifestyle content got highest likes:
- "Jesus loves you" → 28,887 likes
- Funnel Hacking Live photos → 2,486 likes

**Insight:** Marketing posts get fewer likes but more comments (better for lead gen).

## Content Themes She Repeats

1. **Simplicity wins** — "Just 2 campaigns", "No complicated setup"
2. **Ugly beats pretty** — For low-ticket, raw creative outperforms polished
3. **Creative > Targeting** — Meta's AI handles targeting, your job is creative
4. **Refresh weekly** — 50-100 new ads per week via AI
5. **Test before scale** — Don't optimize what you haven't validated

## Patterns NOT to Copy

1. **Event promotion posts** — Low engagement, not applicable
2. **Pure lifestyle content** — Gets likes but not leads (unless building personal brand)
3. **Hashtag overuse** — Cat barely uses hashtags, most posts have 0-5

---

## Recommendations

1. Use "Comment CTA" pattern 1-2x per week for lead gen
2. Lead with counter-intuitive claims to stop scroll
3. "Here's the exact..." format for value posts
4. Story → Lesson for building trust
5. Keep marketing posts focused on comments, not likes
