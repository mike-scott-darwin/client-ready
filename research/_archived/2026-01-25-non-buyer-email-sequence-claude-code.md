# Non-Buyer 30-Day Email Sequence Strategy

**Research Question:** How to integrate a 30-day email sequence that ascends non-buyers to the new $27 Client Ready Offer System?

---

## Current State Analysis

### What Exists

**`outputs/email-sequence-non-buyers.md`:**
- 7 emails over 14 days
- Goal: Convert to front-end purchase
- Style: Miles Stutz + Michael Scott voice
- Issue: References old "$17-$27" pricing (now $27)
- Gap: Stops at Day 14, no long-term nurture

**`reference/domain/funnel/email-templates-30-days.md`:**
- Bill Mueller's 30 email templates
- Templates organized by purpose (soft teach, FAQ, contrarian, etc.)
- Ready to adapt for Michael's voice

### The Problem

Non-buyers who don't convert in 14 days currently go dark. No further nurture. No long-term ascension path.

**2026 Reality:**
- Average sales cycle: 14-30 days ([Patrick Sargis](https://x.com/PatrickSargis/status/1883987240415813773))
- Non-buyers need different cadence than buyers
- Email list is an asset — can't just abandon

---

## Strategic Framework

### Miles Stutz Insight (Jan 2026 Call)

> "Lead customers to the LIMITATIONS of their purchase to create a need for the call."

**Applied to Non-Buyers:** Lead non-buyers to the COST of NOT purchasing.

### Three Core Emotions (2025 Email Strategy)

| Emotion | Application |
|---------|-------------|
| **Recognition (Pride)** | "You're not like other coaches who buy courses and don't implement" |
| **FOMO (Regret)** | "Every week without a clear offer = more wasted time" |
| **Reciprocity (Gratitude)** | Value-first emails that create obligation |

## Recommended Structure

### The 30-Day Non-Buyer Sequence

**Cadence:** 10 emails over 30 days (not daily — non-buyers need breathing room)

| Phase | Days | Emails | Purpose |
|-------|------|--------|---------|
| **Abandonment Recovery** | 1-7 | 4 | Convert hot leads who almost bought |
| **Value + Relationship** | 8-14 | 2 | Build trust, no pitch |
| **Re-Engagement** | 15-23 | 3 | Fresh angles, social proof |
| **Soft Close** | 24-30 | 1 | Final pitch + transition to nurture |

## Email Content Strategy

### Core Themes to Hit

Based on audience pain points (`reference/core/audience.md`):

1. **Validation anxiety** — "What if nobody buys?"
2. **Wasted effort** — Built infrastructure for offer that didn't sell
3. **Wrong advice** — Told to quit job, build course, run ads
4. **Imposter syndrome** — Need more credentials before charging premium

### Step 3: Set Up Automation

**Trigger:** Visited sales page, didn't buy, gave email (lead magnet or quiz)

**Segmentation:**
- If buys at any point → move to buyer sequence
- If opens but doesn't buy → tag as engaged, continue sequence
- If no opens for 14 days → tag as cold, reduce frequency
