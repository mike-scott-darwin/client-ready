## Pain Points Targeted

### Primary Pains (Emotional)

1. **Appointment Prison**
   - "Escape the appointment prison I built for myself"
   - Stuck on sales calls, trading time for money
   - Scaled to $100K/mo but MORE stressed, not less

2. **Lead Generation Anxiety**
   - "Never think or worry about Lead Generation ever again"
   - Daily hustle: posting content, sending DMs, booking calls
   - Exhausting manual prospecting

3. **The Scaling Lie**
   - "I was sold a lie" — hire people, work ON business, hit $100K
   - "Things don't get easier. They get more intense."
   - More revenue = more complexity, not more freedom

4. **Sales Call Dependency**
   - Need to close on phone to make money
   - Calendar packed with calls
   - Can't scale without hiring closers

### Secondary Pains

- Posting content daily with inconsistent results
- Running ads that lose money
- Hiring setters/closers who underperform
- Fulfillment overwhelm (too many clients, not enough time)
