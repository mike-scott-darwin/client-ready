# Conor Boyland: Making $1M from a $27 Low-Ticket Offer

**Source:** Email from Conor Boyland (Idea to Income)
**Relevance:** Near-identical model to Client Ready — $27 front-end, bumps/upsells, backend accelerator

---

## His Model

**Front-end:** $27 — "Idea to Income" (3 AI bots that extract ideas, build offer, write copy)
**Order bump:** $33 (on checkout page)
**Upsell 1:** $67
**Upsell 2:** $77
**Backend:** $3,000-6,000 — "Idea to Income Accelerator" (coaching, weekly feedback in private Google Doc, group calls, DFY funnels/templates, AI tools)

**AOV:** ~$70 (front-end + bumps + upsells average)

---

## His Math

| Source | Revenue |
|--------|---------|
| Front-end (10 sales/day × $70 AOV × 365) | ~$250K |
| Backend (250 Accelerator clients × $3K) | $750K |
| **Total** | **$1M** |

**Key insight:** Front-end revenue is a quarter of the total. The BIG money is backend (LTV).

---

## Key Takeaways for Client Ready

### 1. AOV Target Confirmation
Conor's $70 AOV from a $27 sticker price validates the Cat Howell benchmark. Our max one-time cart is $842 ($27 + $17 + $37 + $67 + $297 + $397). Even modest bump/OTO take rates should hit $70+ AOV easily.

### 2. 10 Sales/Day is the Benchmark
At $70 AOV, 10 sales/day = $250K front-end. This aligns with Cat's "don't scale until AOV is $100+" — at $100 AOV, 10 sales/day = $365K front-end.

### 3. Backend is 3x Front-End Revenue
250 Accelerator clients from ~3,650 front-end buyers = ~7% backend conversion. At $3K average, that's $750K. Our Accelerator is $5K — if we convert even 5% of front-end buyers, that's significant.

### 4. His Offer Positioning Mirrors Ours
- "Everyone has ideas but struggles to put them into words that sell" → Our "Zone of Genius extraction"
- AI bots that do the work → Our AI prompts in the $27 system
- "$27 to make it really hard to say no" → Same self-liquidating logic
- Accelerator = personal help to launch and scale → Same as our Accelerator

### 5. His Bump/Upsell Pricing
- $33 bump (we have $17, $37, $67)
- $67 and $77 upsells (we have $297 Sprint, $397 Blueprint)
- Our upsells are significantly higher-priced. If conversion holds, our AOV should be much higher than his $70.

### 6. Building in Public as Content Strategy
He's building this in public — using the journey as content. "Breaking the beliefs that might be keeping you stuck." This is a content pillar we haven't activated yet (Behind the Scenes exists in content-strategy.md but no content yet).

### 7. Imperfection Positioning
"Everything I've ever built is wildly imperfect. My funnels aren't the best, my numbers are not elite." Anti-guru angle — same as our voice.md positioning. Permission-giving that perfection isn't required.

### 8. Distraction Warning
"Your biggest enemy is distraction. It takes several months to build properly." This could be a content angle — and it's a real risk for our audience.
