## 7 Figure Freedom

> "Your business will either be built by default or by design" — Dan Bolton

**Key insight:** Most people create a business they want to run away from.

**The self-sabotage principle:**
> "Subconsciously, you will not grow into pain"

If the current version of your business sucks and you have no desire to change it, your subconscious mind knows it won't change if it gets bigger → leads to self-sabotaging growth.

**Solution:** Get extremely clear on your vision. Follow the 4 Levels of Scaling.

## 5 Business Archetypes

**Key principle:** It's about owning YOUR archetype. What aligns with you?

### The Consultant
- Loves to do the work FOR or WITH people (DFY, DWY)
- Thrives being involved in the process
- Examples: Freelancers, DFY Agencies

### The Coach
- Loves personal breakthroughs from helping people overcome challenges
- Usually intimate, 1:1, solving problems
- Engaging with people with their time

### The Mentor
- Prefers high-level overview, strategic advice
- Thrives in "one to many" or group coaching
- Big picture, high-level strategy, how to think (not tactics)

### The Creative
- Thrives with white space in calendar
- Formulating ideas, creating things, synthesizing information
- Creating, writing, and learning makes them feel alive

### The Builder
- Optimizing for scalability
- Managing tasks and operations
- Optimizing for long-term revenue, big teams

**Example:**
> "Alex Hormozi doesn't build the businesses... He spends 15,000 hours writing his next book and creating YouTube content. He's more of a mentor and creator while Leila builds the business and manages the team."

## Offers

### Focus Principle
> "Have 1 Core Offer that's crushing. Focus. When you have exhausted your intimate offer, expand with a scalable offer."

### Three Offer Types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Intimate** | 1:1, coaching, service |
| **Scalable** | Leveraged with your time |
| **Pop-Up** | 6-week mastermind, not ongoing |

### Two Parts of an Offer
1. **The perception** of what it is
2. **What it actually is**

Ideally both should be EPIC.

### Proximity Principle
> "When you have done the work to build a personal brand, people want to work with YOU, not be handed off to someone else or a course. You are the best product to sell."

### Pop-Up Products
- Workshops
- Intensives
- Masterclass
- Challenges
- 5-Day Slack / Voxer
- Short-term masterminds (5-10 weeks, focused strategy)
- Sell your time

### 3 Key Things for an Offer

**1. Dream Outcome**
- Deeply understand the ONE thing
- Who they are → who they want to be
- Ask them what they want and give it to them

**2. Benefits**
- Articulate the dream outcome AND the benefits
- Who is the dream client that wants the dream outcome?

**3. Logistics**
- Time commitment
- Pricing

### 16-Week Model
> "16 weeks works extremely well. Get amazing results and re-sell into a longer commitment."

Every problem you solve creates a new problem. Have a multi-year vision for each client solving a stack of problems over time.

### Prepay Hack
> "It's 10k paid across 10 weeks, or if you want to save 20% you can prepay today."

People will work harder to save money than to make it. Keep the prepay option open for clients.

## Content Creation

### 4 Types of Content

**1. Educate**
- Demonstrate your expertise
- People see you as smart, an expert
- Not much deep connection with you or brand
- **Key:** Be a painkiller, not a vitamin — give lightbulb moments

**2. Share**
- Bring people into your life
- Family, friends, things you do, interests
- Day to day, thoughts and opinions

**3. Show**
- Client testimonials
- Interviews
- Past work & projects
- Showcase unique mechanism / process

**4. Give**
- Opportunities
- Assets and trainings
- Invitations to work with you (webinar, community, call)

> "Consistency beats intensity with frequency of posting"

## Funnels

**Dog Whistle Marketing:** Speaking to 1 type of person, solving 1 problem really well.

### The 4 Parts

**1. Core Identity Community**
- Niche with dream aspirations and values

**2. Growth Engine**
- IG funnel ads
- Organic growth on FB

**3. Content Cadence**
- 1 main attention channel
- 1 nurture channel (YouTube)
- Email

**4. Conversion Systems**
- Setting (DMs)
- Mini VSL
- Cash campaigns

> "Your funnel does not increase the quality of people in it, it just increases the trust with them."
