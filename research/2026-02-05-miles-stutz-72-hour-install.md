# Miles Stutz: 72-Hour Digital Snacks Installation Guide

**Source:** DS_ 72 Hour Install.md

---

## Core Promise

> "From Zero to 200-300 Buyers Per Week"

---

## The Transformation Story

### The Before State: Content Hamster Wheel

**The fear:** "I used to be scared shitless of running Facebook ads."

**What he tried:** Organic only — posting every day, sliding into DMs, converting to sales calls.

**The stress:** "Always one bad month away from losing everything."

Key pain points:
- Every sale mattered
- Constantly chasing 5K-7K deals to pay bills
- Giving away best content for free on YouTube/Instagram
- "Hoping someone would eventually buy"

**The identity crisis:**
> "I was basically a backup dancer for social media platforms, and they were calling all the shots."

### The Breaking Point

> "Why am I giving all my best content away for free when I'm struggling to make consistent money?"

Hours spent on content that:
- Might get seen
- Might get engagement
- Might eventually lead to a sale

Meanwhile: stressed about money, unpredictable income, working harder than corporate for less.

### The Discovery: Digital Snacks

Instead of creating next free YouTube video → turned same content into a "Digital Snack" ($7-$97).

**What changed:**
- Could run ads profitably (getting paid immediately)
- $0 to $1,000/day profitable in 60-90 days
- 200-300 buyers every week (not leads — buyers)
- Buyers became high-ticket clients

### The New Reality

> "I cover my business expenses and living costs on the first day of every month."

**How:** One email to buyer list → high-ticket offer sells out in seconds.

**The transformation:**
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Social media backup dancer | Actual business owner |
| Chasing every sale | People chase him |
| Giving away best content free | Getting paid for it |
| One bad month from disaster | Expenses covered day 1 |

---

## The "AI Gold Rush" Positioning

> "We're in the middle of what I call the 'AI Gold Rush' — a 6 to 12-month window where it's actually easier to sell information than it is to get people to consume free content."

**Why now:**
- So much free content (especially AI-generated)
- People overwhelmed with free information
- When someone pays — even $27 — they actually consume it, value it, get results

> "This is the first time in history where selling your expertise is easier than giving it away for free."

**Urgency:** "This window won't last forever. The coaches who figure this out now are going to dominate the next few years."

---

## What Gets Installed

1. Turn existing expertise into profitable Digital Snacks
2. Set up simple funnels that convert browsers to buyers
3. Run ads that make money from day one
4. Build list of buyers who become high-ticket clients
5. Cover expenses on first day of every month
