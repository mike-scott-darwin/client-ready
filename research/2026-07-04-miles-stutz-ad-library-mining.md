## 5. Copy Techniques (the reusable skeleton)

1. **Scroll-stop title = the image.** The headline IS the creative (text on plain/branded background).
2. **Confession open.** "I closed a $15K client yesterday. No call." / "I'm good on sales calls. That turned out to be the problem."
3. **False-cause → true-cause pivot.** "I thought the problem was [me / price / proof]. It was actually [the offer / the structure]."
4. **The one-line thesis.** "Read → decide → buy." / "The offer explains itself."
5. **Specificity as proof.** Named students, exact dollars, "161 niches," "$20/day," "612 sales in Stripe."
6. **The checklist of ✅ deliverables** near the close.
7. **Guarantee baked into the CTA + "keep the swipe file anyway."** Risk reversal that also signals confidence.
8. **P.S. that re-frames, never re-pitches.** "Being great on the phone isn't a flex if the phone owns your calendar."
