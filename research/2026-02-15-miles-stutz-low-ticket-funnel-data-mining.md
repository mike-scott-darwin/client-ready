## Landing Page Structure (What Giovanni Built)

Full sales page breakdown — this is the actual page structure that produced the numbers above.

**Section 1 — Hero**
- Headline: "Get The Exact Mental Processes I Use to Help Pro's Conquer Fear of Failure and Pressure to Perform"
- Subheadline: 45-minute Fearless Mind Workshop positioning
- CTA button above the fold

**Section 2 — Problem Identification**
- "You're Sublime in the Nets But Tight and Tense in a Match"
- Calls out the gap between practice performance and match performance
- "Because of Your Fears, Nerves, Anxiety and Pressure to Perform — NOT Your Technique"
- Reframes the problem from technical to mental

**Section 3 — Frustration Mirror**
- "You're Frustrated Because You Know You've Got the Brains and Potential to Score Freely in Matches, But You..."
- Lists specific pain points the audience recognizes

**Section 4 — Before/After Comparison**
- Two-column layout: "And You're Identified As" vs "And After Completing The Mini Workshop You'll..."
- 8 before/after contrasts showing the transformation
- Visual format makes the gap obvious

**Section 5 — Social Proof (Testimonials)**
- "Here's What Players Say" with player testimonials
- Media logos (The Telegraph, Cricketrith, others) for credibility

**Section 6 — About / Authority**
- "Hi my name is Gio Colussi"
- Personal story establishing credibility
- Photo with coaching context

**Section 7 — Product Introduction**
- "The Fearless Mind Mini Workshop"
- Introduces what they're actually getting

**Section 8 — What's Included Breakdown**
- Detailed module/content listing
- Social proof screenshots embedded

**Section 9 — Who Is This For**
- Specific audience targeting
- Checkboxes or tags for self-identification

**Section 10 — Challenge Questions**
- "Are You Facing Any Of These Challenges?"
- Forces reader to self-identify with the problem

**Section 11 — Offer Stack with Bonuses**
- Main product: The Fearless Mind Mini Workshop
- Plus 5 FREE Bonuses (for 47)
- Bonus 1: Unlock Your Batting Potential
- Bonus 2: Batting Assessment
- Bonus 3: Striking the Right Balance
- Bonus 4: Nerves are your SuperPower
- Bonus 5: Fearless Mind Community
- Total value: 552 — limited time 47

**Section 12 — Guarantee**
- Money-back guarantee badge
- "No Questions Asked" positioning

**Section 13 — FAQ**
- Common objection handling in accordion format

**Section 14 — Final Testimonials**
- "Why Players Sign Up" — more social proof

**Section 15 — Final CTA**
- Urgency-driven close

## Key Takeaways for Client Ready

**What's working:**
- Organic traffic from Instagram bio converts at 3.8% visitor-to-sale — decent for cold-ish traffic
- 2-step checkout with opt-in first (32.42% opt-in rate captures leads even if they don't buy)
- Strong before/after section makes transformation tangible
- 5 bonuses at 47 price point creates high perceived value (552 value stack)
- Problem reframe (it's not your technique, it's your mind) is a strong angle

**What needs work (per Miles):**
- Checkout CVR at 17.9% is below the 30% benchmark — checkout page needs optimization
- Bump only converting at 2.38% — could test different bump offer or positioning
- AOV of 57.86 is low — more aggressive bump/upsell strategy needed

**Patterns relevant to our funnel:**
- Value stack with bonuses is the same pattern we use (total value vs price)
- 2-step checkout captures email before payment — same as our GHL setup
- Before/after format on landing page is effective for transformation offers
- Community access as a bonus (not the main offer) — interesting positioning choice
- Price test recommendation (cut in half) aligns with the "test price sensitivity" principle

**Landing page structure comparison to ours:**
- Giovanni uses 15 sections — more than typical but follows a logical flow
- Heavy on social proof (testimonials appear 3 times throughout the page)
- Problem identification comes before solution — classic problem-agitate-solve
- Authority section (about me) placed mid-page, not at top
- FAQ handles objections before final CTA
