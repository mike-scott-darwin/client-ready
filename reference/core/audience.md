# Audience

## Primary Audience

**Who:** Coaches and service providers who want to sell high-ticket offers ($5K+) but are stuck at the early stages

**Specifics:**
- May still have a 9-to-5 (that's okay — Michael won't tell them to quit)
- Have expertise/experience but haven't packaged it into a clear offer
- Overwhelmed by "build a course" or "scale with ads" advice
- Want a practical, engineering-minded approach — not guru fluff

## Demographics

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Role | Coaches, consultants, service providers |
| Stage | Early to mid — have skills but not systems |
| Income | Side hustle to early full-time ($0-$100K from coaching) |
| Tech comfort | Low to medium — need simple systems |
| Mindset | Action-oriented, skeptical of hype |

## The 3am Worry

"I know I can help people, but I don't know how to package what I do into something people will actually pay for. Everyone else seems to have it figured out. What if I'm not cut out for this?"

## Pain Points

1. **Unclear offer** — Can't articulate what they sell in a way that makes people buy
2. **No funnel** — Trading time for scattered calls, no system to attract clients
3. **Overthinking** — Stuck perfecting instead of launching
4. **Wrong advice** — Told to quit job, build course, run ads — none of it fits their stage
5. **Imposter syndrome** — Feel like they need more credentials before charging premium

## What They've Tried

- Watched hours of YouTube content on "how to start a coaching business"
- Maybe bought a course or two that was too advanced or too generic
- Posted on social media inconsistently
- Had a few clients through word-of-mouth but can't replicate it
- Created offers that didn't sell or underpriced their services

## Dream Outcome

- **Clarity:** Know exactly what they offer and who it's for
- **System:** Have a funnel that brings in leads without constant hustle
- **Confidence:** Can charge $5K+ because the value is obvious
- **Freedom:** Build the coaching business alongside their life (not instead of it)

## Why They Trust Michael

- He won't tell them to quit their 9-to-5 (practical, not idealistic)
- Engineering approach — test, validate, then build
- No fluff, no 47-page guides — clear and actionable
- Uses AI to speed up the process (modern, efficient)
- Understands the "stuck in your head" problem

## Who This is NOT For

- People who want to "get rich quick" without doing the work
- Coaches who already have working funnels and consistent $10K+ months
- Those unwilling to put their offer in front of real people
- Perfectionists who won't ship until everything is "ready"
