# Hybrid VSL Landing Page Reference

The page format used across all Client Ready standalone trainings and front-end landing pages. Replaces "naked VSL" (video only) and text-only sales pages.

---

## Why Hybrid VSL

Naked VSLs are dying. Cold traffic bounces because they don't know what they're watching or why they should care. Text-only pages miss people who'd rather watch than read.

The Hybrid VSL combines both: readers can read, watchers can watch, skimmers can scroll the screenshots. Everyone gets the same pitch in their preferred format.

This is the dominant format in the $27-$97 low-ticket coaching/digital product space. Used by Cat Howell, Miles Stutz, Hernan Vazquez, Allie Bjerk, Alex Cattoni, and most practitioners running $100K+/month in paid traffic.

---

## The 7 Sections

Every hybrid VSL page has 7 sections. Every section has a job. Skip one and the page breaks.

### 1. Headline + Sub-headline
**Job:** Stop the scroll. Tell them what this is and who it's for.
- Must match the ad headline ("one pizza, get pizza")
- Main headline: under 15 words, states the transformation promise
- Sub-headline: who this is for + timeframe + one "without" statement

### 2. Video (5-15 min)
**Job:** Build trust. Show the product. Prove you're real.
- Hook (0-30 sec): restate headline promise
- Problem (30 sec - 2 min): articulate their pain better than they can
- Solution (2-5 min): what the product is, how it works, what they get
- Proof (5-8 min): results, walk through the product live
- CTA (last 30 sec): "scroll down and grab it"
- Show the actual product on screen. Screen share the PDF, run the prompts, show the template.

### 3. Problem Section
**Job:** Make them feel understood. Agitate the pain.
- 3-5 short paragraphs, written like you're in their head
- Use their language, not yours ("I can't figure out my offer" not "optimal value proposition development")
- Be specific ("spending 45 minutes on sales calls with people who ghost you" not "stuck")

### 4. Solution + What's Inside
**Job:** Show them what they're getting. Make it concrete.
- Product name + format + outcome + timeframe
- Components as deliverables, not features ("5 AI prompts that extract your zone of genius" not "AI-powered extraction technology")
- 3-step "here's how it works" (max 3 steps)

### 5. Visual Evidence
**Job:** Prove the product is real. Kill the "is this a scam?" objection.
- Screenshots, GIFs, or screen recordings of the actual product
- Real screenshots only — not mockup generators, not stock photos
- GIFs > static images (movement catches attention on scroll)
- Mobile screenshots at 1:1 or 9:16 ratio

### 6. Social Proof
**Job:** Let other people do the selling.
- Screenshots of actual messages (DMs, emails, Skool posts)
- Specific results ("landed 3 clients in first week")
- 2-3 testimonials is enough — don't pad with weak ones
- If no testimonials yet: show your own results, screen record yourself using the product, be honest ("this is new — here's my guarantee")

### 7. CTA + Pricing + Guarantee
**Job:** Tell them exactly what to do next. Remove friction.
- Price with anchor (~~$197~~ $47)
- Bullet recap of what's included
- Guarantee restated next to the price
- One button. One action. No multiple options.
- Bumps appear on checkout page, not on the landing page.

## The 5-Second Test

Run on every page before spending a dollar on traffic. Open the page, look at it for 5 seconds, then answer:

| # | Question | What You're Checking |
|---|----------|---------------------|
| 1 | Who is this for? | Target audience visible instantly |
| 2 | Why am I here? | The payoff / result they'll get |
| 3 | Why should I care? | Unique differentiator |
| 4 | Why should I believe? | Trust signals, proof, credibility |
| 5 | What do I do next? | Clear CTA — one action, obvious |

If you can't answer all five in 5 seconds, the page isn't ready.

| Bump pages | Checkout add-ons | Not landing pages — different format entirely |

OTO pages don't need the hybrid format because the buyer is already mid-checkout. Hybrid VSL is for cold traffic where you have 5 seconds to prove relevance.

## Key Principles

- **Ad-to-page congruence:** Headline must match the ad. If the ad says "validate your offer in one afternoon" and the page says "build a sustainable coaching business," you lose them.
- **Mobile-first:** 80%+ of paid traffic is on mobile. Font 16px+, buttons 48px+ height, first CTA visible without scrolling, page loads under 3 seconds on 4G.
- **Visual evidence is the trust layer:** Screenshots and GIFs of the actual product separate your page from scam pages. This is what most coaching pages are missing.
- **One CTA, one action:** Don't give them 3 options. One button. One thing to do.
- **Deliverables, not features:** "5 AI prompts that extract your zone of genius" not "AI-powered extraction technology."
