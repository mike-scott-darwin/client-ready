## Key Messaging

**Why this matters:**
> "The quality of your answers directly determines the quality of your custom Blueprint. The more detailed information you provide, the more precisely I can craft your positioning, copy, and funnel strategy to feel authentically yours and convert your ideal clients."

**Guidelines for clients:**
- Take your time — Quality over speed
- Be detailed — "Think conversation with a friend"
- Share authentic voice — natural language
- Include specific examples and stories
- NO wrong answers — honesty beats perfection
- Share links to successful marketing material

**For new businesses:**
- Skip questions that don't apply
- Make educated guesses based on research/intuition
- Share hypotheses about who you want to serve
- Describe vision, even if not fully formed

## Form Structure

**Time investment:** 45-90 minutes (can be completed in multiple sessions)
**Sections:** 8 focused sections covering every aspect of business and goals

**Sharing requirement:** Before submitting, client must enable: Share → General access → Anyone with the link → Viewer

---

## Section 8: Identity Foundation (NEW)

**Why this section exists:** Most people build a business they eventually burn down — not because the tactics fail, but because it doesn't fit who they are. These questions help us build something you'll actually sustain.

**Questions:**

1. **How would you describe yourself in your business right now?** (3 sentences max)
   - Not your offer. YOU. Where are you mentally, emotionally, practically?

2. **Who do you need to become to run this offer effortlessly?**
   - What version of you shows up every day without forcing it?

3. **What beliefs, habits, or fears must that version of you be free of?**
   - What's currently in the way? What do you need to let go of?

4. **Describe your ideal work day when this business is running.**
   - What time do you start? What do you actually do? When do you stop? Be specific.

5. **What do you want to be FREE of?**
   - Hours? Specific tasks? Stress? Client types? Name it.

**Note to client:** There are no wrong answers. These questions help us design a funnel that fits YOUR life — not a template that works for someone else.
