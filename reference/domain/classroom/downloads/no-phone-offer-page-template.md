# No-Phone Offer Page Template

**Complete sales page structure for $2K-$5K offers**

---

## Page Structure Overview

```
1. Headline + Subhead
2. Who This Is For
3. The Problem
4. The Solution (Your Offer)
5. What's Included
6. The Process (Week by Week)
7. The Guarantee
8. Pricing
9. FAQ
10. Final CTA
11. Who This Is NOT For
```

---

## Section 1: Headline + Subhead

**Headline formula:** [Specific outcome] in [timeframe]

**Subhead formula:** For [specific person] who wants [desire] without [pain they want to avoid]

### Example:

**Headline:**
# A Validated, Profitable Funnel in 8 Weeks

**Subhead:**
For coaches who already know their offer works — and want it built right the first time, without months of trial and error.

### Your version:

**Headline:**
# _______________________________________________

**Subhead:**
_______________________________________________

## Section 2: Who This Is For

**Purpose:** Pre-qualify buyers. Make the right people feel seen.

### Template:

**This is for you if:**

- You've already validated your offer (even at a small scale)
- You want speed + expert guidance, not another course to figure out alone
- You're ready to invest in getting it right the first time
- You have [specific prerequisite — e.g., "bought the $47 system" or "have paying clients"]

### Example:

**This is for you if:**
- You've bought the Client Ready Offer System or Sprint
- You know your offer works — you've made sales
- You want a complete funnel, not just templates
- You're ready to invest $5,000 to save months of trial and error

---

## Section 3: The Problem

**Purpose:** Articulate the pain so they feel understood.

### Template:

You've got the offer. Maybe you've even made sales.

But you're stuck at [specific bottleneck].

Every day you [painful daily reality].

You've tried [thing they've tried]. It [why it didn't work].

What you really need is [the gap your offer fills].

### Example:

You've got the offer. Maybe you've even made sales.

But you're stuck at "DIY everything."

Every day you're toggling between landing page builders, email tools, and ad platforms — none of which talk to each other.

You've tried courses. You've tried templates. You've tried figuring it out yourself. It works... slowly. And you're not sure if you're doing it right.

What you really need is someone who's done this before to build it WITH you — so you can focus on serving clients, not fighting tech.

---

## Section 4: The Solution (Your Offer)

**Purpose:** Name the offer and position it.

### Template:

**Introducing [Offer Name]**

[One sentence describing what it is]

[One sentence describing the transformation]

[One sentence describing who it's for]

### Example:

**Introducing the Client Ready Accelerator**

A done-with-you intensive where we build your complete funnel together in 8 weeks.

You go from "validated offer" to "system that generates clients" — with expert guidance at every step.

For coaches who want it done right, not just done.

**Purpose:** Answer "What exactly do I get?"

### Template:

**Here's everything you get:**

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| [Deliverable 1] | [What it is + what it does for them] |
| [Deliverable 2] | [What it is + what it does for them] |
| [Deliverable 3] | [What it is + what it does for them] |
| [Access type] | [Calls, Slack, Loom — be specific] |
| [Bonus if any] | [What it is + what it does for them] |

| Zone of genius extraction | Refined positioning that attracts perfect clients |

## Section 6: The Process (Week by Week)

**Purpose:** Answer "What happens after I pay?"

### Template:

**How it works:**

| Week | Focus | What Happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 0 | Onboarding | You fill out the intake form, we schedule kickoff |
| 1-2 | [Phase 1] | [What you build/do together] |
| 3-4 | [Phase 2] | [What you build/do together] |
| 5-6 | [Phase 3] | [What you build/do together] |
| 7-8 | [Phase 4] | [What you build/do together] |

### Example:

**How it works:**

| Week | Focus | What Happens |
|------|-------|--------------|
| 0 | Onboarding | Intake form, kickoff call scheduled within 48 hours |
| 1-2 | Extraction + Validation | Refine your offer, nail your positioning |
| 3-4 | Funnel Build | Landing page, checkout, email sequences |
| 5-6 | Traffic Setup | Ad creative, targeting, first campaign live |
| 7-8 | Optimize + Launch | Refine based on data, full system running |

---

## Section 7: The Guarantee

**Purpose:** Remove risk.

### Template:

**The Guarantee:**

[Specific promise]. If [condition for failure], [what you'll do].

### Example:

**The Guarantee:**

Profitable funnel or we keep working until it is.

If your funnel isn't generating customers by Week 8, I don't stop. We keep going — no extra charge — until it works.

---

## Section 8: Pricing

**Purpose:** State price clearly. No "starting at."

### Template:

**Investment:** $[PRICE] one-time

[Optional: What that breaks down to]

[Optional: Payment plan if offered]

### Example:

**Investment:** $5,000 one-time

That's 8 weeks of done-with-you funnel building, 3 strategy calls, unlimited async support, and every template you need.

Payment plan available: 2x $2,600

---

## Section 9: FAQ

**Purpose:** Handle remaining objections.

### Include these:

1. **"What if I haven't validated my offer yet?"**
   → [Answer: This isn't for you yet. Go through the Sprint first.]

2. **"How much of my time does this require?"**
   → [Answer: Expect 3-5 hours/week for your part.]

3. **"What if it doesn't work?"**
   → [Answer: Guarantee explanation.]

4. **"Why no sales call?"**
   → [Answer: If you need a call to decide, you're not ready yet.]

5. **"What happens after 8 weeks?"**
   → [Answer: You have a working system. Optional ongoing support available.]

---

## Section 10: Final CTA

**Purpose:** Close.

### Template:

**Ready?**

[Button: Get Started / Apply Now / Join the Accelerator]

[What happens when they click — "You'll fill out a quick form and I'll be in touch within 24 hours."]

---

## Section 11: Who This Is NOT For

**Purpose:** Repel wrong fits. Build trust with right fits.

### Template:

**This is NOT for you if:**

- [Disqualifier 1]
- [Disqualifier 2]
- [Disqualifier 3]

### Example:

**This is NOT for you if:**

- You haven't made any sales yet (start with the $47 system or Sprint)
- You want someone to "do it all" with zero input from you (this is done-WITH-you)
- You're looking for the cheapest option (this is the premium path)
- You need a sales call to feel confident (if you need convincing, you're not ready)
