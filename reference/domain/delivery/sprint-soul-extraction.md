# Sprint Soul Extraction Module

**Purpose:** Week 0/1 identity work for Sprint ($297) clients. Ensures they build an aligned offer before touching any funnel tactics.

**Position in Sprint:** Before any funnel work. "Before we build, let's make sure it fits."

---

## Why This Exists

> "Most people build something they'll burn down in 18 months. Let's not do that."

The Sprint isn't just about building a funnel. It's about building the RIGHT funnel — one that fits your life, not one you'll abandon when it gets hard.

**The problem we're solving:**
- People build offers that make money but hurt to show up for
- They quit not because tactics fail, but because the foundation is misaligned
- "Subconsciously, you will not grow into pain" — if it doesn't fit, you'll sabotage it

**The insight:**
> "Income leaps follow identity leaps, not the other way around."

Before we build your system, we need to know: Who are you becoming? What does that person's business look like?

---

## Delivery Format

### 1. Welcome Loom (15 min)

**Title:** "Before We Build: Why Alignment Beats Tactics"

**Key points to cover:**
- Most funnels fail not because of bad tactics, but bad fit
- You can't grow into pain — if it hurts to show up, you'll burn it down
- The next 4 weeks will go faster if we're building the RIGHT thing
- These questions aren't "woo" — they're practical design constraints
- Your answers shape everything we build together

**Tone:** Direct, practical, not spiritual. Frame as "smart business design."

---

### 2. Soul Extraction Prompts (Client Completes Before Week 1 Call)

**Instructions to client:**
> "Take 20-30 minutes with these. Don't overthink. First instinct is usually right. These answers will guide everything we build."

---

**Prompt 1: Ideal Work Day**

> Describe your ideal work day when this business is running smoothly.
>
> - What time do you wake up?
> - What do you actually DO during work hours?
> - How many hours are you working?
> - What are you FREE of?
>
> Be specific. "Work from home" isn't an answer. "Start at 9am, 2 hours of client calls, 1 hour of content, done by 2pm" is.

---

**Prompt 2: The Escape**

> What are you trying to escape?
>
> Not the positive vision — the negative. What do you want to NEVER do again?
>
> Examples: Sales calls. Posting daily. Chasing clients. Working weekends. Answering to a boss. Trading time for money.

---

**Prompt 3: The Burndown Test**

> What would make you abandon this offer in 6 months?
>
> Be honest. What would cause you to quit, pivot, or burn it down?
>
> Examples: "If I had to do 10+ calls a week." "If I had to post content every day." "If clients were constantly needy."
>
> These aren't weaknesses. They're design constraints. We build AROUND them.

---

**Prompt 4: The Identity Shift**

> Who do you need to become to run this business effortlessly?
>
> Not skills. IDENTITY. How does that person think? What do they believe? How do they show up?
>
> Compare to who you are now. What's different?

---

**Prompt 5: The Drop**

> What's one belief, habit, or fear you need to let go of to make this work?
>
> Just one. The big one. The thing that keeps showing up.

---

### 3. Week 1 Call: Hot Seat Review

**Facilitator notes:**

- Review each person's Soul Extraction answers before the call
- Identify misalignments: "You said you want to work 20 hours but your offer requires 40 hours of delivery"
- Surface conflicts: "You want no sales calls but your offer is $5K — how do buyers decide?"
- Validate fits: "This offer matches your ideal day perfectly"

**Key questions to ask:**
- "Does this offer fit inside your ideal day?"
- "What would the person you're becoming do here?"
- "Is this something you'll still want to do in 2 years?"

## Success Metrics

How we know this is working:
- Completion rate: Do clients actually fill out the prompts?
- Testimonial quality: Do testimonials mention "fits my life" or "sustainable"?
- Retention: Do Sprint clients stick around / ascend to higher tiers?
- Burndown rate: How many clients abandon their offer within 6 months?
