## Key Messaging

**Why this matters:**
> "The quality of your answers directly determines the quality of your DFY offer build. Be honest and specific — these answers shape everything I create for you."

**Guidelines for clients:**
- Takes 10-15 minutes
- Be specific — first instinct is usually right
- NO wrong answers — honesty beats perfection
- Include examples where possible

**For new businesses:**
- Make educated guesses based on research/intuition
- Share hypotheses about who you want to serve
- Describe vision, even if not fully formed

---

## The 8 Questions

**Time investment:** 10-15 minutes

1. **What do you do?** (Brief description of your expertise — not an elevator pitch, just what you actually do)

2. **Who do you help?** (Describe your ideal client — the more specific, the better)

3. **What transformation do you provide?** (Where are they before working with you? Where are they after?)

4. **What have you already tried?** (Courses, funnels, offers, coaching — what's your history?)

5. **What's your biggest stuck point right now?** (The one thing blocking progress)

6. **What would make this investment worth it?** (What outcome would make you say "that was worth every penny"?)

7. **Describe your ideal work day when this business is running.** (What time do you start? What do you actually do? When do you stop? Be specific.)

8. **What do you want to be FREE of?** (Hours? Specific tasks? Stress? Client types? Name it.)
