# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Michael Scott ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information in connection with our internal ad research tool.

## Information We Collect

Our ad research tool accesses publicly available information from the Meta Ad Library API, including:
- Public ad creative content (text, images, videos)
- Advertiser page names and IDs
- Ad delivery dates and status
- Aggregated demographic and geographic data (as provided by Meta)

## How We Use Information

We use this information solely for:
- Competitive research and market analysis
- Improving our own advertising strategies
- Internal business purposes

## Information Sharing

We do not:
- Sell any data collected through the Meta Ad Library API
- Share data with third parties for their marketing purposes
- Use the data to target or contact individuals

## Data Retention

Ad research data is retained only for as long as necessary for our internal business analysis purposes.
