# Misalignment

## Core Hook

"I've built offers that made money and made me miserable. Now I only build what I can show up for."

## Why It Works

- **Unique story:** Only Michael can tell this (7+ years in the search)
- **Hidden fear:** "What if I succeed and still hate it?"
- **Permission:** Validates their instinct that something feels off
- **Trust:** Vulnerability without weakness

## The Story (Michael's Version)

You've built things that worked on paper. Clients paid. Revenue came in. But showing up felt like dragging yourself through concrete. The success made it worse — now you couldn't quit without looking crazy.

The insight: **You can't grow into pain.** If it hurts at $5K/month, it'll hurt worse at $50K/month. The business doesn't fix the misalignment. It amplifies it.

## Variations

### Short (Ads, Hooks)
- "I burned down a business that was working. Best decision I ever made."
- "Success that hurts to show up for isn't success."
- "You can't grow into pain."

### Medium (Captions, Email)
- "The worst business I ever built made money. The best one I ever built made me want to show up."
- "If you're white-knuckling every day, the offer is wrong. Not you."
- "Most coaches are one burnout away from quitting. Not because they're failing — because they're succeeding at the wrong thing."

### Long (VSL, About Page)
- "Three years ago I had a coaching business that was 'working.' Clients. Revenue. The whole thing. I hated every minute of it. Showing up felt like punishment. I thought: 'It'll get better when I scale.' It didn't. It got worse. So I burned it down and started over. Now I help coaches validate offers they'll actually want to keep — before they build the infrastructure that traps them."

## Voice Match

- Vulnerable but grounded
- "Here's the truth" energy
