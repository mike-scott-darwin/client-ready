## Primary Angles

### 1. The "Won't Tell You to Quit" Angle
**Hook:** "The Coach Who Won't Tell You to Quit Your 9-to-5"

**Why it works:**
- Contrarian to typical guru advice
- Meets people where they are
- Builds trust through realism

**Use in:** Bio, about pages, introductions

---

### 2. The "15 Minutes" Angle
**Hook:** "Create your complete offer document in 15 minutes"

**Why it works:**
- Specific timeframe (not "fast" — exactly 15 minutes)
- Implies simplicity
- Counters the "it takes months" belief

**Use in:** Lead magnet, $7 offer, ads

---

### 3. The "$7 to $5K" Ladder Angle
**Hook:** "Validate with $7 low ticket offers and scale to $5K+ high ticket clients"

**Why it works:**
- Shows the path (not a leap)
- Low risk entry point
- Aspirational but achievable endpoint

**Use in:** Community description, funnel explanation

---

### 4. The "Engineering Approach" Angle
**Hook:** "Test, validate, build — not wishful thinking"

**Why it works:**
- Appeals to logical thinkers
- Differentiates from "manifest your success" crowd
- Implies proven methodology

**Use in:** Content, sales conversations, objection handling

---

### 5. The "Reality Over Perception" Angle
**Hook:** "You can't grow into pain"

**Why it works:**
- Memorable phrase
- Counters "fake it till you make it"
- Implies substance over style

**Use in:** Educational content, offer positioning

---

### 6. The "AI Does 90%" Angle
**Hook:** "We're going to use AI to do 90% of the heavy lifting"

**Why it works:**
- Modern, efficient
- Reduces perceived effort
- Curiosity driver

**Use in:** $7 offer, lead magnet, content
