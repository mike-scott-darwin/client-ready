# Content Merry-Go-Round

## Core Hook

"You've been posting for 12 months. Getting likes. Nobody's buying. The problem isn't your content — it's your offer."

## Why It Works

- **Specific pain:** Directly from audience.md ("content merry-go-round")
- **Shifts blame:** From "I'm bad at content" to "the system is broken"
- **Clear alternative:** Paid traffic instead of organic hamster wheel
- **Permission to stop:** They've suspected this, you confirm it

## The Problem (Their Reality)

They've been told: "Post consistently and the clients will come." So they:
- Post 3-5x per week
- Get likes, comments, follows
- Nobody DMs to buy
- Feel like they're doing something wrong
- Double down on more content

**The real issue:** Their offer is unclear. More content won't fix a confusing offer.
