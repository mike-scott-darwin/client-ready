# Anti-Customization Angle

**Status:** Secondary — test after Before the Funnel, Misalignment, Content Merry-Go-Round, and Clarity Unlock are running.

---

## The Enemy

The belief that every client needs a custom offer. Coaches spend weeks tailoring proposals, rewriting pitches, adjusting deliverables for each prospect. It feels like "good service." It's actually a scaling trap.

## The Reframe

"Custom" = unfocused. If your offer needs customizing for every prospect, it's not clear enough. Clear, repeatable offers scale. Custom offers keep you stuck.

## Why This Fits Client Ready

The $47 system gives you ONE offer document template. The Plug & Play Templates (Bump 2) are literally named "plug and play." The entire philosophy is: build one clear offer, not ten fuzzy ones. This angle IS the product thesis.

---

## Hooks

1. "Stop customizing your offer for every prospect. Build one that works for all of them."
2. "I used to rewrite my offer for every single lead. Then I realized: if it needs rewriting, it's not clear enough."
3. "The coaches charging $5K+ don't have 10 different offers. They have one. It's just that clear."
4. "Every time you customize your offer, you're telling yourself it's not good enough as-is."
5. "Custom proposals are a coping mechanism for unclear offers."

## Awareness Level

**Problem Aware.** They know they're spending too long on proposals/customization but don't frame it as an offer clarity problem.

## Pairs With

- **One Afternoon** — One template + one afternoon = done (vs weeks of custom proposals)
- **Engineering Approach** — Build once, validate, repeat (vs bespoke every time)
- **Counterintuitive Reveal** — "I stopped customizing my offers. My close rate went up."
