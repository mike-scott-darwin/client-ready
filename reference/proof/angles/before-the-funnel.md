# Before the Funnel

## Core Hook

"Everyone's selling you funnels. Nobody's asking: do you have an offer worth building one for?"

## Why It Works

- **Contrarian:** Competitors (Cat, Miles, Hernan) all assume you have an offer
- **Reframes the problem:** Offer > funnel (most people have it backwards)
- **Positions you as prerequisite:** The step BEFORE scaling coaches

## Variations

### Short (Ads, Hooks)
- "Before the funnel. Before the ads. Before the content calendar. Do you have an offer anyone will pay for?"
- "Stop building funnels for offers that don't work."
- "The funnel isn't the problem. The offer is."

### Medium (Captions, Email Subject Lines)
- "I see coaches spending $5K on funnels for offers they haven't validated. Wrong order."
- "Here's what the funnel gurus won't tell you: the tech is the least important part."
- "You don't need a better funnel. You need to know if anyone will pay."

### Long (VSL, Story)
- "I spent 6 months building a course nobody bought. The funnel was perfect. The offer was wrong. Now I validate before I build anything."

## Voice Match

- Direct, contrarian
- "Wrong." energy
- Engineering mindset: test before you build

## Pair With

- "Test, validate, build" (mechanism)
- "15 Minutes" angle (speed)
- "$27 to validate" (low-risk entry)

## Use In

| Context | How |
|---------|-----|
| Cold traffic ads | Lead with the question |
| Landing page headline | "Before the funnel..." |
| Content hooks | Pattern interrupt |
| Objection handling | "Do you even have an offer yet?" |

## Testimonial Evidence

Every client came in with the same root problem — the offer was unclear. Not the funnel, not the marketing, not the content.

| Client | Their "Before the Funnel" Problem |
|--------|-----------------------------------|
| **Nicole** | Creating programs "for women" — broad, no clear offer or ideal client |
| **Alexia** | Giving sessions at low-ticket, hadn't shaped or packaged a real offer |
| **Renee** | 10 years couldn't define niche — couldn't build anything without that foundation |
| **Carly** | Unclear how to utilize skills for maximum impact — no structured offer |
| **Robin** | Throwing content randomly — no clear offer underneath the marketing |
| **Wendy** | Months on the brink of launching — couldn't articulate who she serves or what they need |

**Strongest quotable:** Renee — "I have struggled with this for 10 years and Michael helped me with that in an hour and a half session."
